## Snapshot

A right-back who's as honest as they come but nowhere near good enough for Premier League football. McLoughlin will tackle, will keep his shape, and won't do anything spectacular in either direction, which makes him a perfectly adequate fourth-choice fullback. However, expecting anything more than basic competence is folly, and he'll be exposed against any team with genuine attacking ambition.
