Jan Kuchta is a 29-year-old Czech center-forward currently playing for Sparta Praha. Standing at 184 cm, he is known for his offensive contributions. While initially listed as left-footed, public reporting indicates he is right-footed.

Recent Form

Jan Kuchta has demonstrated strong offensive output in the 2025/2026 1. Liga season, recording 8 goals and 4 assists in 1,088 minutes played, earning an average FotMob rating of 6.92. He consistently scores highly in goals, assists, and overall rating compared to other strikers in the league. Notably, he scored two goals in just 18 minutes during a 5-2 home win against Slovacko on March 15, 2026.

His recent match involvement includes playing 32 minutes against Karvina on April 5, 2026, where he received a yellow card, and 81 minutes in a Europa Conference League loss to AZ Alkmaar on March 19, 2026. Kuchta has also been an unused substitute in some league matches, indicating varied playing time within the squad.

Tactical Profile

As a center-forward, Kuchta's primary strengths lie in his attacking contributions, with FotMob highlighting his high scoring in goals and assists for a striker in the 1. Liga. Despite his offensive prowess, some analyses suggest weaknesses in his discipline, passing, and defensive contribution, indicating a player primarily focused on attacking play. He predominantly operates as a central striker, which aligns with his "Marksman" archetype.

Kuchta's preferred foot is right, a detail consistently reported across multiple platforms. His role as a "Seconda Punta" suggests he operates slightly behind or alongside a main striker, contributing to both scoring and creation, which is supported by his assist numbers.

Career Highlights

Jan Kuchta has accumulated significant silverware throughout his career, winning five titles across two major Czech clubs. With Slavia Prague, he secured the Czech Cup and the 1. Liga title in the 2020/2021 season. Following his move to Sparta Prague, he continued his success, winning the 1. Liga in both the 2022/2023 and 2023/2024 seasons, and adding another Czech Cup in 2023/2024.

Individually, Kuchta was the joint top scorer in the 2020–21 Czech First League with 15 goals and was recognized as the Czech First League Forward of the Season for 2023–24. He also received the Czech First League Player of the Month award in January 2021. On the international stage, Kuchta debuted for the Czech Republic national team on October 8, 2021, and was included in Ivan Hašek's 26-man squad for UEFA Euro 2024.

Personality

Jan Kuchta's personality has drawn public attention due to disciplinary incidents. In November 2023, he was notably removed from the Czech Republic national team camp, along with two other players, after allegedly visiting a nightclub two days before a crucial UEFA Euro 2024 qualifying match. This incident aligns with assessments that rate his discipline as "Very Weak". He also received a five-match ban in 2022 for an on-field incident where he stepped on an opponent's head.
