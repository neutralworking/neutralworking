## Snapshot

A Danish centre-forward with legitimate athleticism and a willingness to press relentlessly from the front. Kaufmann's movement in the box is intelligent and he's not entirely hopeless on the ball, but his finishing remains wildly inconsistent and he can disappear for long stretches. He works hard but lacks the consistency required for a regular starting role.
