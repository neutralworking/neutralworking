## Snapshot

A left-back operating at the lower reaches of the French pyramid, Roussillon remains a capable ball-progressor with decent range on the pass and the ability to contribute in the half-space when pushing forward. At 33, he's a functional defender who understands positioning and rarely gets caught out of shape, but he lacks the recovery pace and explosive athleticism to cover modern wingers with any real conviction. For Sochaux at this level, he offers experience and steadiness—a squad anchor rather than a dynamic threat.
