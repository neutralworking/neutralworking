## Snapshot

Centre-back with excellent reading of the game, capable of stepping into midfield and breaking lines with progressive passing, comfortable on the ball and positionally smart. He's not the quickest recovery option and can be undone by genuine explosive pace, particularly against number nines who are dangerous in transition. Cultured defender suited to possession-based football.
