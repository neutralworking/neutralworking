## Scouting Notes


CountryCroatia

ClubHajduk Split

PositionForward

Born05 August 2000







****

A small, fast and furious striker with an elite-level dribble and great spatial awareness, Sego is the premier product of the recently revamped Hajduk Split academy. He has a knack for creating chances both for himself and for team-mates. This season he has been introduced to senior football via the club’s reserve side that play in the second division, with his appearances carefully weighed. Sego has been talked about for years and if he continues to develop the way he has, he could grow into a classy, versatile and modern forward. **[Aleksandar Holiga](https://twitter.com/AlexHoliga)**  
  
**October 2018 update** Slowly being introduced into Hajduk’s first team. Made his debut in February and scored his maiden goal in his fifth appearance in May.  
  
**October 2019 update** Turned out he wasn’t quite ready for senior football so he was returned to Hajduk’s ‘B’ team, who play in second division, where he is a regular. Has also made the Croatia Under-21 squad.  
  
**October 2020 update** Loaned out within the Croatian Prva HNL to Slaven Belupo in January, but did not play much. Now on another loan with NK Bravo in the Slovenian top flight, where he is a regular.  
  
**October 2021 update** Has had three different loan spells over the past 12 months, still showing hints of potential but nothing more than that. Now with NK Varazdin, who were relegated to the Croatian second division last season.  
  
**September 2022 update** After a really good season helping Varazdin in their promotion straight back to the top flight, he signed for them on a permanent deal. Is continuing to progress and has found his way back into the Croatia Under-21 squad but can he take even more steps forward?
