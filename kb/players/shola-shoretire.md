## Scouting Notes

Young English winger on loan at Zwolle getting consistent minutes in the Eredivisie, which is valuable seasoning for a player still learning his trade. He's got genuine dribbling ability and can operate on either flank with reasonable conviction, pressing triggers and a willingness to take defenders on in the half-spaces. The problem is he's neither a consistent finisher nor a reliable creator yet—too many moments where he makes the right run but delivers a poor pass, or beats his man and runs out of ideas. At 22, playing at Zwolle's level, he's a developing talent with England youth caps behind him, but there's still a substantial gap between this and being a top-flight regular.
