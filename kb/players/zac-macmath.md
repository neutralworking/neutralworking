## Snapshot

A steady, unglamorous MLS custodian who has been Colorado's reliable first-choice for years. MacMath reads the game with the patience of a veteran, commands his box with authority, and rarely beats himself—his distribution is sensible rather than creative, and he'll play the percentages on every pass. The obvious limitation is athleticism: he's not a shot-stopper who will bail you out with a reflex save, and against athletic forwards driving the channels, he can look a touch slow off his line.
