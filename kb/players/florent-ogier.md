## Snapshot

A 36-year-old centre-back running on tactical intelligence and familiarity with Clermont's system rather than physical prowess. He knows where to stand and when to step, which works fine against Ligue 1 forwards of modest quality, but he's a liability against any side with genuine pace in the final third. At this stage of his career, he's a depth piece who helps on the training ground more than on the pitch.
