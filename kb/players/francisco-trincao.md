## Snapshot

A right winger with genuine technical refinement—intelligent positioning in the half-spaces, capable of both inverted runs and genuine crosses, which is rarer than it sounds at this level. He's comfortable receiving on the turn and progressing the ball with purpose rather than panic, and his workrate off the ball keeps him honest. The limitation is rawness in big moments; he can go quiet in genuinely high-pressure fixtures, and his left foot remains a suggestion rather than a proper alternative.
