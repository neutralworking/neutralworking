## Snapshot

A tornante winger-midfielder with sharp interception timing and composure under pressure, Bajić reads the game intelligently and uses guile to win possession in advanced areas, but his pass accuracy is unreliable and mid-range distribution leaks control in transition. Discipline problems suggest he chases interceptions recklessly, creating gaps on the flank, while his limited tactical range means he struggles to adapt positionally when systems shift. For a club needing a disruptive pressing presence in the wide half-spaces, his awareness and tackling offer genuine value; he functions best as a temporary lever against set, predictable opponents rather than as a season-long solution.
