## Snapshot

Left winger who gets up the pitch with genuine intent and carries the ball with a bit of urgency. His pressing is willing rather than surgical, and he struggles against press-resistant fullbacks who have time to settle. He's a runner rather than a technician, and while that's useful in the Swiss Super League, his lack of a true left foot limits his ability to manipulate space or find dangerous cut-backs.
