## Snapshot

A centre-back with excellent physical tools and the ability to play out from the back with genuine composure — progressive passer who isn't afraid to carry into midfield and break lines. His issue is positional discipline; he can get caught ball-watching in transition, and against truly elite strikers with movement, he's still learning the fine margins.
