## Snapshot

A commanding shot-stopper with excellent positioning and genuine distribution range who has been the backbone of Feyenoord's defensive reliability for years. He reads the game with composure and rarely makes the catastrophic error, though he's not a sweeper-keeper in the modern sense—he's content to stay home and trust his defenders. His weakness is in dealing with the chaotic low cross or the scrambled second ball, where his angles can become uncertain, but as a traditional Eredivisie goalkeeper he's very good.
