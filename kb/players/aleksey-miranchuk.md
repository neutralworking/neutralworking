## Scouting Notes

A left-sided midfielder with genuine technical class and the ability to operate as a No. 8 or No. 10 depending on the shape. Miranchuk's passing range and composure under pressure are above-average, and he contributes meaningfully to possession-based football. However, he lacks explosive athleticism, which limits his impact in transition, and he's not a progressive dribbler — his game is about retention and finding the next pass, which sometimes feels passive.
