## Scouting Notes

A Greek centre-forward who operates best as a focal point in a rigid 4-4-2, winning first contact and holding the ball through sheer physical presence. His touch is functional rather than refined, and he's not a creative threat—he exists to be fed and to turn shots on goal. He's a fine option for Como's style, but he won't progress with the club if they ascend; he's a tool for a specific tactical moment.
