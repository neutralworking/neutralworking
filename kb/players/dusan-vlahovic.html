Dušan Vlahović: Player Research Brief

Recent Form

Dušan Vlahović's current season (2025/2026) at Juventus has seen him register 3 goals and 1 assist in 14 Serie A appearances. Across all competitions, he has accumulated 6 goals and 2 assists in 18 matches. His playing time in Serie A amounts to 693 minutes, with an average FotMob rating of 6.61.

His shooting accuracy in the league stands at 36.67%, having placed 11 of his 30 shots on target, resulting in a goal for every 10 shots taken. Vlahović's goal-scoring rate is approximately 0.39 goals per 90 minutes this season. Recent match logs indicate periods of limited minutes and some appearances as an unused substitute, including a bench role in a 2-0 win against Genoa in April 2026.

Tactical Profile

Dušan Vlahović is recognized as a complete striker, combining physicality with technical skill and a keen eye for goal. He excels at receiving the ball in central areas or wide channels, demonstrating strong hold-up play and the ability to turn and dribble past defenders. His intelligent movement allows him to create space for shooting opportunities, often employing "double movements" to lose defenders.

Operating effectively as a focal point, Vlahović's presence enables teammates to make runs or drop deep into space. He consistently seeks optimal goalscoring positions, often anticipating play before his opponents. While predominantly left-footed, he is capable of varied finishes, prioritizing accuracy over sheer power. Although a formidable aerial presence, particularly for flick-ons from long balls, his precision in scoring with his head has been noted as an area for improvement. His athleticism allows him to exploit defensive gaps and make runs in behind.

Career Highlights

Vlahović has accumulated several significant accolades throughout his career. He was named Serie A Best Young Player for the 2020–21 season. With Juventus, he secured the Coppa Italia in the 2023–24 season, notably scoring the decisive goal in the final. In the same season (2023-2024), he was recognized as the Footballer of the Year and Striker of the Year.

His early career saw him become the youngest debutant and goalscorer for Partizan, where he won the Serbian championship and Serbian Cup in the 2016–17 season. During his time at Fiorentina, Vlahović achieved a remarkable feat in 2021 by scoring 33 Serie A goals in a calendar year, a tally matched only by Cristiano Ronaldo since 1951. He is also one of only two players born after 2000, alongside Erling Haaland, to have scored at least 40 goals in Europe's top five leagues.

Personality

Dušan Vlahović is described as a "true leader on the pitch," driven by a dedication to continuous improvement and an ambition to be among the world's best strikers. He is characterized as feisty and aggressive, someone who engages in battles with defenders and is resilient in the face of challenges. However, there have been observations regarding his psychological state, with concerns that he can become discouraged after initial mistakes, which may impact his overall performance. His MBTI personality type has been identified as ESTP.

Condition / Medical History

Dušan Vlahović is currently fit as of April 2026, having recently recovered from a stress reaction. However, he underwent surgery in December 2025 to repair a tendon avulsion of the left adductor, an injury that sidelined him for approximately 14 weeks, with his return projected for March 2026.

His medical history includes several notable injuries. In late 2025, prior to his surgery, he experienced a tendon injury and a muscle injury. Earlier in 2025, he dealt with a thigh injury and physical discomfort. In 2024, he had another thigh injury and a muscle injury. A back injury occurred in September 2023, and he also experienced groin injuries in late 2022. Throughout his career, Vlahović has missed approximately 11.3% of matches due to various injuries, with pubalgia being his longest absence.
