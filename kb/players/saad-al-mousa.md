## Snapshot

Saad Al Mousa is a physically imposing destroyer-dribbler, capable of winning aerial battles and using his balance to shield the ball effectively, but his technical limitations will often see him struggle to play out from the back, and his lack of tactical awareness can lead to him being caught out of position. His pace allows him to make progressive carries, but he often relies on his physicality rather than guile to beat opponents. Al Mousa's inability to hit a decent volley is also a concern, and he will need to work on his overall technical game to become a more well-rounded defender.
