## Scouting Notes


ClubNordsjælland

PositionAttacking midfielder

Born1 June 2004

Born in Bodø, north of the Arctic circle, Schjelderup attracted enormous interest from scouts very early on. They came to Norway to watch his talents, and Schjelderup visited Ajax and PSV Eindhoven academies four times each, as well as having trials at Liverpool, Tottenham, Juventus and Bayern Munich. Eventually, though, he chose to join Nordsjælland, the Danish club famous for nurturing youngsters – and the move has already paid off, the prodigy promoted to the first-team squad at the beginning of 2021. He immediately became a key performer thanks to his imagination, vision, audacity, dribbling skills and versatility – Schjelderup can be used as a central midfielder, left winger, second striker and even at centre-forward. He scored his first goal in the Superliga in March, added a brace a week later, and had three goals to his name in the new season by the end of September. He is already a star by Danish standards and – having recently signed a contract extension until 2024 – looks certain to be sold for a Nordsjælland record fee when he eventually goes. **[Michael Yokhin](https://twitter.com/Yokhin)**  
[Watch Andreas Schjelderup in action](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yAgIyTrfXQI)  
  
**September 2022 update** Has secured a spot in the starting XI and has started the 2022-23 season magnificently with five goals in 10 league appearances. The 2021-22 season was tough as Nordsjælland struggled at the wrong end of the table. “It was testing, especially for me as a young player,” Schjelderup admitted.  
  
**October 2023 update** Joined Benfica in January 2023 for £9m, the Portuguese club recognising the talent the young forward possesses. Now, he may not have got many minutes - two, in fact – but by the end of the 2022-23 he had featured in both the Portuguese top-flight and the Champions League. Has rejoined Nordsjælland on loan for the 2023-24 season.
