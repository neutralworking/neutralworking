## Snapshot

A defensive midfielder with exceptional reading of the game and the positional intelligence to make twenty interceptions look inevitable rather than fortunate, coupled with genuinely polished footwork for someone in his role. His pressing is enthusiastic but occasionally clumsy, and he can be undone by sharp passes into tight spaces because his recovery pace isn't remarkable, but he's a serious footballer who elevates any midfield he anchors. A key player for Beşiktaş if they want control of the centre.
