## Snapshot

Argentine playmaker who operates in the half-spaces with genuine technical intelligence and an eye for the killer pass; he's progressive in possession and can thread through-balls that split lines. The issue is decisiveness under pressure—he'll occasionally overthink rather than commit, and defensively he's a passenger who'll cost you in the middle third. Still, on his day he's a genuine creator for a mid-table side.
