## Scouting Notes

A left-winger with genuinely dangerous acceleration and the ability to create space on the ball through explosive dribbling; he's direct, aggressive off the ball, and capable of beating a full-back in isolation. His decision-making in the final third can be rushed, and his crossing is inconsistent—he beats defenders with his feet but doesn't always convert the chance into a clear opportunity. A good Bundesliga starter with high upside, provided he sharpens his end product.
