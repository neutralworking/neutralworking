## Snapshot

A. Vogt embodies the Marksman archetype, a direct Poacher with strong finishing ability from both close and long range, complemented by surprising technical skill and guile in take-ons. His tactical strength allows him to position effectively, but his limited mental and physical pillars, combined with weaknesses in creativity, pass accuracy, and duels, pigeonhole him as a pure penalty-box operator. He is a specialist who thrives on service, recalling traditional fox-in-the-box strikers, and would be a sensible, cost-effective acquisition for a club that creates numerous chances and requires a dedicated finisher rather than a link-up forward.
