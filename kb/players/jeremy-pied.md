His athleticism has predictably declined with age, making him vulnerable to explosive wingers in one-on-one situations, and his attacking output has become genuinely negligible—he's a passenger in the final third now. Still, the football intelligence is there: he understands angles, communicates well, and won't embarrass himself against mid-table opposition.
