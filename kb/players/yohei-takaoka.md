## Snapshot

A composed, distributing goalkeeper who plays a sweeper role and isn't afraid to venture into dangerous territory with the ball at his feet—his range of passing and willingness to progress play from deep is well above J1 average. The problem is his shot-stopping and decision-making under pressure can be erratic; he's given away possession in critical moments and his positioning on crosses asks serious questions about his concentration. At 29, he's likely peaked as a squad-rotation option at a legitimate top-five league club, but in the Japanese league he's a capable starter with enough technical skill to warrant European interest were his fundamentals more reliable.
