## Snapshot

Central midfielder with good energy and a tidy first touch, capable of arriving late in the box and finishing; he works hard off the ball and contributes defensively. His range is limited and he can be predictable in possession—he doesn't progress the ball with real vision or drive from deep. A reliable squad player who gives you eight or nine out of ten every week, but nothing that makes you sit forward in your seat.
