Crysencio Summerville: Player Research Brief

Recent Form

Crysencio Summerville has shown a significant upturn in performance for West Ham United since the beginning of 2026. After recording only one assist and no goals in the first half of the 2025/2026 season, he has scored five league goals since the turn of the year, averaging 0.5 goals per 90 minutes in the Premier League. This impressive output has made him one of the division's most in-form players and a crucial figure in West Ham's battle against relegation.

As of March 11, 2026, Summerville had made 27 appearances across all competitions, contributing 7 goals and 2 assists, maintaining a Sofascore Rating of 7.14. He averages 78 minutes per game and has started 23 matches, demonstrating the coaching staff's trust in his abilities. His creative output has also increased, with a notable rise in crossing attempts (3.01 per 90 minutes in 2026 compared to 0.93 in 2025) and chances created, though this has not always translated into assists due to his teammates' finishing.

Tactical Profile

Operating primarily as an inverted winger on the left flank, Summerville is a right-footed player who excels at cutting inside to create scoring and shooting opportunities. He is known for his exceptional dribbling ability, pace, and acceleration, frequently running at defenders and utilizing quick one-two passes in the final third. His playing style is characterized by a desire to move freely across the attacking third, making him difficult for opponents to mark.

Summerville's strengths lie in his ability to take on defenders and carry the ball effectively, rather than engaging in hold-up play. He possesses a low center of gravity, agility, and quick feet, allowing him to use body feints and rapid reactions to beat opponents. While he creates significant danger, his role as a final-ball supplier is still developing, averaging 0.85 key passes per game. His heatmap indicates a strong presence on the left touchline and half-space, often receiving the ball high and wide before attacking the penalty area.

Career Highlights

Crysencio Summerville's football journey began at the renowned Feyenoord academy, which he joined at the age of seven. He signed his first professional contract with Feyenoord in 2018. Internationally, he was part of the Netherlands U17 squad that won the 2018 UEFA European Under-17 Championship, even scoring a goal against Germany in the group stage.

After loan spells at FC Dordrecht and ADO Den Haag, where he became ADO Den Haag's youngest Eredivisie goalscorer, Summerville moved to Leeds United in September 2020. He made his Premier League debut for Leeds in September 2021 and gained significant attention in October 2022 by scoring his first Premier League goal against Fulham, followed by a memorable winning goal against Liverpool at Anfield, ending their 29-game unbeaten home streak. His standout season came in the 2023/24 Championship, where he scored 19 league goals and provided 10 assists, earning him accolades such as the EFL Championship Player of the Season and a spot in the Championship Team of the Season.

Crysencio Summerville is described as an exciting and confident player who consistently seeks to influence the game, even when facing challenges. He is known for his persistence, always asking for the ball and striving to make an impact. Summerville comes from humble beginnings in Rotterdam South, growing up as the second-youngest of eight children in a family that instilled strong values, with his parents emphasizing education and providing unwavering support for his football aspirations.
