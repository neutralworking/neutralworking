## Snapshot

A cultured, left-footed 8 who arrives at Hamburg with genuine press-resistance and an eye for the half-space—he'll drift into pockets and curl passes onto the back foot of runners with real precision. His technical ceiling is higher than most in this division; he can manipulate the ball away from traffic and progress play via dribble or the through-ball. The issue is he's a touch slow physically and can get overwhelmed by high-intensity pressing; in a battle of the midfield, he needs shield and space, not chaos.
