## Scouting Notes

A nuisance to defend — chest-on striker with real instinct for occupying defenders and creating half-yard advantages in the box. Still has the nous to find pockets in crowded areas, and his positioning remains better than his athleticism would suggest. The problem is speed; he's a half-second late to everything now, and against anyone with genuine pace across the back four, he'll struggle to impose himself.
