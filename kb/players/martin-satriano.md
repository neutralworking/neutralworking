## Snapshot

Rangy number-nine who uses his physical presence to hold play and create space for others in tight areas, with a nose for half-space movement that suits counter-pressing systems. Limited technical security on the ball and his finishing is streaky; he won't carve open defenses alone. Useful bench option in a team that plays direct football, less valuable if you demand your striker to be a fulcrum in possession.
