## Snapshot

A left-sided playmaker of rare technical refinement with exceptional passing range and the ability to find teammates in improbable spaces through weighted through balls and crossfield switches. His movement in tight spaces remains exemplary, and he's still capable of moments of creative brilliance that unlock defences. The trade-off is stamina and consistency—at 33, he's less of a fixture in the midfield three than he was five years ago, and his work-rate in pressing is no longer reliable.
