## Snapshot

Haitham Asiri is a raw, pacey centre-forward in the mould of a traditional Sprinter, capable of making runs in behind the opposition defence with his exceptional acceleration and pace. However, his lack of awareness often sees him caught out of position, and his poor tackling and marking abilities make him a liability in defensive phases. Asiri's game is built on making progressive carries into the channels, but he struggles to link play with his teammates due to his limited awareness, making him a frustrating watch at times.
