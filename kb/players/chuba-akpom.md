## Snapshot

A centre-forward with excellent movement in the box and a genuine finishing ability that has proven itself across multiple seasons; his work rate off the ball is commendable. However, Akpom's first touch can be heavy in congested spaces and he doesn't have the physical dominance some Premier League defenses will impose on him. At 30, he's a reliable, proven goalscorer—a solid addition for a club his level.
