## Snapshot

A ball-playing centre-back with better technical range than Ekpiteta and genuine intent in possession, Casey can carry the ball into the middle third and isn't afraid of pressure—a modern touch for a League One defender. His weakness is his physicality; he lacks the natural dominance in the air and one-on-one defending that compensates for slighter frames at this level, and he'll be exposed against more direct opposition.
