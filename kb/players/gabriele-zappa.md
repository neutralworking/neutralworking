## Scouting Notes

A right-back who attacks the flank with genuine pace and provides consistent width, though his defensive fundamentals—particularly footwork against tricky wingers—can be suspect. He's more comfortable when his side has the ball, as he covers ground better going forward than recovering laterally. Useful in a high-press system but exploitable on the counter.
