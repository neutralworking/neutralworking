## Snapshot

Right winger with real pace and the ability to strike a ball cleanly from range, which gives opposing fullbacks something to think about. However, his decision-making in the final third is pedestrian—he'll often drift infield predictably and lack the invention to create a yard of space when a lane closes. He's a functional option for Basel's attacking width, but he doesn't have the craft or intelligence to trouble defences at the next level.
