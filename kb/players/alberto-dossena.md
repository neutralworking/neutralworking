## Snapshot

A reliable, unfussy centre-back who reads the game with the calm of a man who's done this a thousand times before. He's positionally sound and moves people around well on set pieces, but he's not quick enough to cover for midfield lapses, and he'll never be dominant in aerial duels against truly physical strikers. Serviceable for a newly promoted side, nothing more.
