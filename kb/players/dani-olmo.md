## Career Highlights

Dani Olmo's career has been marked by significant achievements at both club and international levels. Internationally, Olmo has been a key player for Spain. He won the UEFA European Under-21 Championship in 2019 and earned a silver medal at the 2020 Olympic tournament. He returned to his boyhood club Barcelona in 2024, signing a six-year contract.
