## Snapshot

A Spanish winger capable of delivering useful work in a structured system, with reasonable pace and the ability to cut inside or whip crosses, though he doesn't possess the technical security or in-game variability to be a playmaker. His decision-making in the final third is occasionally wasteful, and he can be predictable, inviting defenders to set traps. Juanmi is a squad winger — productive enough for a team operating with low ambition but not a player who would enhance a team with European aspirations.
