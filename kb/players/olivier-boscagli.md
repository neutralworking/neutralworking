## Snapshot

A left-sided centre-back with genuine composure in possession and the ability to progress the ball through short-range playmaking rather than just hoisting it long. He reads space intelligently, positions himself well to intercept, and has the physicality to handle modern attacking forwards, though he can be caught out by explosive lateral movement and sometimes hesitates when facing inverted wingers. Dependable Premier League performer, not spectacular but architecturally sound.
