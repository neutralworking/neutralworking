## Snapshot

A combative, no-nonsense centre-back who organizes well and reads the game with decent positional sense, though his ball-playing ability is limited—he's not someone you're looking to play out from the back. Strong in the air and dominant in one-on-one situations, but prone to rash decisions when pressed and doesn't have the mobility to cover ground laterally against pacey forwards. Solid mid-table Premier League depth or a reliable starter in the Championship.
