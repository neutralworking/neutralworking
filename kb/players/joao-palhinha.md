## Scouting Notes

A press-resistant midfield anchor who reads the game with maturity and breaks up opposition attacks through positioning rather than reckless lunging. He's comfortable in possession, can turn on the ball in tight spaces, and offers a physical barrier without fouling. His passing range is limited—he's not a creator—and he can occasionally be exposed in one-v-one scenarios against pacey attackers if Bayern's shape fractures.
