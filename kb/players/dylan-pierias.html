Born20 February 2000

A throughly modern-day right-back, Pierias made headlines in February this year when he debuted for City at the age of 16 to become the first player born this millennium to play in the A-League (last year’s Australian Next Generation entry, Sebastian Pasquali, was previously the youngest player to appear in the competition). Ufuk Talay, the Young Socceroos’ coach, believes Pierias, now 17, is one of several youngsters he works with who has what it takes to succeed at the highest level. “He’s a very smart player, technically sound and can play out from the back,” Talay says. “He’s very athletic and has a good engine to go up and down as a full-back.” Further opportunities to play senior football are yet to arrive for Pierias but may not be far away if he continues to develop under the guidance of more experienced first-team squad members. Mike Hytner

October 2018 update Pierias continues to develop in Melbourne and twice featured in City’s pre-season. He was also on the bench for a recent FFA Cup match and is expected to push for another appearance in Warren Joyce’s A-League team this year.

October 2019 update Featuring for Australia Under-20s, Pierias found opportunities limited at club level after Melbourne City signed the veteran Ritchie De Laet as a surprise marquee right-back. Signed alongside another Next Generation player, Sebastian Pasquali, for the new A-League club Western United, where he will face competition from the Socceroo Josh Risdon at right-back.

October 2020 update Has struggled to establish himself as first choice so far having been used mainly as a substitute last season.

October 2021 update Enjoyed a breakout season with Western United, growing in confidence en route to six goals from his 26 appearances including 20 starts. In May he signed with the club for a further two years.

September 2022 update Represented Australia at the Tokyo Olympics before, playing as a wide attacker, he made 11 starts across 27 appearances, scoring two goals in the process, as Western United became A-League Men champions for the first time.
