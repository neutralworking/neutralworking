## Scouting Notes

A physical centre-forward with decent movement in the box and an obvious willingness to work the channels; he holds the ball up adequately and isn't afraid of contact. However, his first touch can let him down, and he rarely creates space for himself with intelligent runs—he tends to operate as a target rather than a creator. A useful squad striker for a side that wants to play long, but limited as a sole attacking reference point.
