## Snapshot

Defensive midfielder with solid positional instinct and an ability to shield the backline with reasonable positioning awareness. He is not a progressive passer and lacks the dynamism to support transitions effectively; his pace in open play is limited, and he can be exposed if caught on the ball in dangerous areas. Squad option for a defensive-minded team, but without the technical range or athleticism for a starting role at a competitive Ligue 1 side.
