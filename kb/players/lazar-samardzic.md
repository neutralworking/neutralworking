## Scouting Notes

A Serbian attacking midfielder with excellent technical ability and a capacity to operate as a No. 8 or No. 10. Samardžić has quick feet, good press resistance, and a dangerous left foot that opens up cutting passes in the half-spaces. The limitation is his intensity off the ball — he's not a hound in midfield, and against more aggressive opponents, he can be overrun; he needs a system with strong ball retention to function.
