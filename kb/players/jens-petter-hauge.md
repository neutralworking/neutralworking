## Snapshot

A left-sided ball carrier with genuine intent in the final third—he'll take a man on and isn't afraid to make something happen in tight spaces. Operates as a winger but has the low center of gravity and quick feet to play inverted into the half-space, where he's most dangerous. The problem is consistency; he'll produce a moment of brilliance then disappear for twenty minutes, and his decision-making in the box remains inconsistent—too often he's shooting when a cutback is there, or vice versa. Defensively, he's a passenger, which limits his ceiling at a genuinely elite club. A very good squad option in Scandinavian football, but the step up to a top-five league as a regular starter hasn't materialized, and at 26, you're running out of time to believe it will.
