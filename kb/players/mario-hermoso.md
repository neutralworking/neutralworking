## Snapshot

Spanish centre-back with exemplary positioning and an understated ability to progress play through short passing and intelligent distribution; he's aerially strong and rarely beaten one-on-one. His pace isn't elite and against direct, explosive attackers he can occasionally be isolated, but his consistency and reading of the game are genuinely excellent. A cornerstone of a well-organised defence.
