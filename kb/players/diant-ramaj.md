## Snapshot

A young goalkeeper with commanding presence and decent shot-stopping reflexes, though his distribution is still pedestrian and his decision-making on whether to leave his line leaves Heidenheim vulnerable to long balls. Ramaj has legitimate upside if he develops the sweeper-keeper instincts modern football demands, but right now he's a functional stopper rather than an architect of play from the back.
