## Snapshot

A winger with legitimate pace and dribbling ability who operates with real directness down the flank, but his end product remains maddeningly inconsistent and his decision-making in the box—when to shoot, when to cross—is erratic. He's a useful option for Marseille's attack, capable of creating chaos, but he's not yet a reliable finisher or creator at the level a title contender demands.
