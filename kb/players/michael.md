## Scouting Notes

Michael is an explosive, direct winger, with blistering pace and exceptional balance, but his lack of guile and intensity means he can be a frustrating watch, often failing to deliver in the final third. He has the ability to beat his man and deliver a cross, but his decision-making is poor, and he rarely looks to take on a more creative role, instead relying on his physical attributes to get him out of trouble. His carries are also a major weakness, as he often loses possession in dangerous areas.
