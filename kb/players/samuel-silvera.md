## Snapshot

A willing runner with decent athleticism who occupies space on the wing, but lacks the technical security and positional intelligence to consistently trouble Premier League defences. He's quick enough to get past a tired fullback and has the temperament to hassle opponents, yet his decision-making in the final third is clumsy—too often choosing the wild pass over the simple one, and his left foot is genuinely a weakness despite nominally being left-footed. For a player at this level, you need either exceptional dribbling, elite finishing, or the intelligence to find soft spaces; Silvera has none of these in reliable measure.
