## Snapshot

A wide forward with genuine directness and a hunger to get at defenders, Forson has the raw ingredients—quick feet, aggressive running angles, decent left foot—but he's still learning the art of finishing and defensive positioning at senior level. He'll ghost out of games defensively and his decision-making in the final third remains hit-and-miss, which is why he's operating at the margins of United's squad rather than anchoring the left flank. The profile suggests a player who could become useful depth, but asking him to be a starter right now would be optimistic.
