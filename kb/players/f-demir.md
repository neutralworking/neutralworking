## Snapshot

Demir is a combative winger with genuine duel success and guile in tight moments, armed with reasonable composure and vision to progress play in the half-spaces. His technical ceiling is constrained by weaker long-range execution and a hot-headed edge that regularly costs possession and disciplinary sanity; he'll never be a crosser or distance-shooter. Ideally suited to possession-oriented teams needing a pressure-resistant, close-control operator on the wing who feeds interior runners rather than demanding space to operate, though his discipline issues make him a medium-term project rather than a plug-and-play solution.
