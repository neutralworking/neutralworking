## Snapshot

Still a useful focal point in transition and a cerebral operator in the half-spaces, though his sprint speed has visibly diminished and he can't sustain the relentless pressing that defined his Liverpool years. His link play and spatial intelligence remain excellent, and he understands how to move a defense, but he's now more of a 70-minute intelligence player than a 90-minute workhorse—the Saudi league suits this version of him perfectly.
