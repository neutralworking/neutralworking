## Scouting Notes


ClubSporting

PositionWinger

Born28 February 2003

Despite being linked with some of Europe’s biggest clubs Joelson has just signed a new contract with Sporting, with a €60m buy-out clause. This came as a relief for Sporting fans, who look at him as one of the best talents in the club’s youth system, or the Cristiano Ronaldo Academy as it is now called. Born in Guinea-Bissau, the winger moved to Portugal when he was 11, and first trained with Sporting’s first team at 16. Last July he became the third youngest player to play for Sporting’s first team. A bright and creative winger, who takes on opponents with confidence even when they have a significant physical advantage – Joelson is 5ft 6in tall. He made his Portugal Under-21 debut in September, still aged 17. **[Nuno Travassos](https://twitter.com/nunotravassos?lang=en)**  
[Watch Joelson Fernandes in action](https://youtu.be/6gi59ZfX3G4)  
  
**October 2021 update** Having played four times for the first team in 2019-20 he did not add to that tally last season and was loaned to Basel in the summer.  
  
**September 2022 update** Back at Sporting after his loan deal at Basel was cut from two seasons to one and it is unclear where he goes from here.  
  
**October 2023 update** Having played for the B team and the Under-23s at Sporting he joined Hatayspor in Turkey, where he has got off to a good start.
