A centre-back shaped by years of Premier League football who retains good athleticism and an organised approach to defending, but whose career trajectory has been decidedly horizontal since leaving Everton. He's steady in one-v-one situations and reasonably composed under pressure, yet he lacks the progressive passing range or ball-carrying ability that modern top-flight sides demand from their defensive anchors. For a Gulf club, he represents a safe, experienced option; for a European side, he's now a rotational piece.
