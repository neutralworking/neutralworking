## Snapshot

A central midfielder with excellent technical ability — he receives the ball cleanly, progresses it intelligently, and rarely misplaces a pass. He's comfortable in possession and doesn't panic under pressure. The concern is that he can be passive; there are moments where he should impose himself more, drive forward more aggressively, or take a risk. He's not a dynamic midfielder who changes games through intensity. He's a very good footballer in the right system, particularly one built around possession and control, but he needs structure.
