## Snapshot

Swedish centre-half with excellent positioning and an underrated reading of the game—he's rarely beaten to space because he anticipates rather than reacts. On the ball, he's comfortable and can pass out from the back, though not with the range of elite progressive defenders. The limitation is his physical engagement; he doesn't relish contact and can be bullied by aggressive, direct centre-forwards, which makes him a player who needs the right system around him rather than someone who imposes himself. Very good in a structured defensive unit, ordinary when chaos breaks out.
