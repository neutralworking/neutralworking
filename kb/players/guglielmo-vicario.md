## Recent Form

Guglielmo Vicario has established himself as a key player for Tottenham Hotspur since his arrival in 2023. During the 2023-2024 season, he made his debut on August 14, 2023, and secured his first clean sheet just five days later against Manchester United. His impressive performances led to nominations for the Premier League Save of the Month in five consecutive months, from August to December 2023, and again in February 2024. He was an ever-present figure in the Premier League, playing all 38 matches as Tottenham finished fifth, and was recognized with the Goalkeeper of the Year award at the London Football Awards in February 2024.

The 2024-2025 season saw Vicario achieve significant success, winning his first major trophy, the UEFA Europa League, with Tottenham. He was also named in the UEFA Europa League Team of the Season for 2024-25 and received the Premier League Save of the Month award in April 2025. As of the current 2025-2026 season, Vicario has played 31 Premier League matches, recording 7 clean sheets and a save percentage of 62.41%. He has made 2.7 saves per game in the Premier League and has featured in 16 Premier League and 6 Champions League matches, conceding 21 and 7 goals respectively.

## Tactical Profile

Vicario is characterized as a blend of a modern sweeper-keeper and a traditional shot-stopper, demonstrating strong abilities in both aspects of the game. He is particularly noted for his elite shot-stopping skills, reflexes, and proficiency in saving penalties, having saved three out of four in the 2024-25 season. His positional awareness and decision-making contribute significantly to his team's defensive stability and ability to initiate counterattacks.

While possessing the capability to play out from the back and execute probing passes, his distribution, especially with his feet, has occasionally drawn criticism, with a mean passing accuracy of 82%. His long ball accuracy in the 2025-2026 season stands at 30.67%, which is among the lower percentages for Premier League goalkeepers. Despite this, he commands his box effectively on crosses and has shown improvement in his overall distribution.

## Career Highlights

Guglielmo Vicario's career path has seen him progress through all levels of Italian football, from Serie D to Serie A. He played a crucial role in Venezia's back-to-back promotions from Serie D to Serie B. His performances at Empoli, where he made 71 appearances over two seasons and famously executed a triple save against Roma in February 2023, earned him a move to Tottenham Hotspur in 2023.

At Tottenham, Vicario has accumulated over 100 appearances and secured his first major trophy, the UEFA Europa League, in the 2024-2025 season. His individual accolades include the London Football Awards Goalkeeper of the Year in 2024 and being named in the UEFA Europa League Team of the Season for 2024–25. On the international stage, Vicario received his first call-up to the Italian national team in September 2022 and made his debut on March 24, 2024, keeping a clean sheet against Ecuador. He was also part of Italy's squad for Euro 2024.

Vicario is known for his focus, emotional investment, and unwavering composure between the posts. He is a passionate individual, a key figure within the Tottenham dressing room, and a member of the club's leadership group, actively supporting the captain and vice-captains.
