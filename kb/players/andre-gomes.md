## Snapshot

ClubBenfica

PositionGoalkeeper

Born20 October 2004

****

Has all the key attributes to become an excellent goalkeeper: he is tall, with an impressive physical stature that makes him look “big” underneath the crossbar. Make no mistake, he is still agile with good reflexes. He has also already shown how good he is with his feet, something increasingly important for goalkeepers. If he manages to improve his consistency over the next few years, and if he overcomes the difficulties of the transition into professional football, which is particularly hard for goalkeepers, Gomes can be an important keeper for Portuguese football in the future. **[Nuno Travassos](https://twitter.com/nunotravassos?lang=en)  
  
**September 2022 update** A key figure as Benfica won the Uefa Youth League, being a penalty hero in the semis against Juventus and then kept a clean sheet in the final against Salzburg. Joined the first team for pre-season in the summer.  
  
**October 2023 update** Suffered a serious injury at the end of last season and lost the opportunity to establish himself as a No 2 for Benfica. It meant that Samuel Soares were given more opportunities but Gomes is still young and is likely to get his chance, at Benfica or elsewhere.**
