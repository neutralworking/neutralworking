Career Highlights

Hákon Arnar Haraldsson began his senior career with Icelandic club ÍA, making his debut in February 2019. He then moved to FC Copenhagen's youth academy in June 2019 and signed a new contract with the club in May 2021 before making his senior debut in July 2021. During his tenure with FC Copenhagen, Haraldsson achieved significant success, winning the Danish Superliga twice (2021–22, 2022–23) and the Danish Cup once (2022–23). He also contributed to Iceland's Baltic Cup victory in 2022.

On July 17, 2023, Haraldsson completed a transfer to French Ligue 1 club Lille, signing a five-year contract until June 30, 2028, for an initial fee of €12 million plus up to €5 million in add-ons. He has also represented Iceland at various youth levels and made his senior international debut for the national team on June 2, 2022.
