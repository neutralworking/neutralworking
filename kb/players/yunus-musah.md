## Snapshot

A central midfielder operating at an elite level for a top Serie A side, with exceptional technical security, range of passing, and the ability to control matches through intelligent ball progression and positioning. He operates beautifully in the half-spaces and breaks lines with genuine regularity, though he can occasionally lack the defensive intensity required against the most aggressive pressing systems. A cornerstone midfielder and one of the most complete players in Serie A right now.
