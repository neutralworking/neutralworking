## Scouting Notes


ClubParis St-Germain

PositionMidfielder

Born4 March 2003

Having been rocked by the departures of Adil Aouchiche and Tanguy Nianzou Kouassi – their two representatives in last year’s NextGen – PSG were determined to hold on to the elegant midfielder in the face of serious interest from Barcelona and Manchester City. Michut has been at the club since January 2016 and signed a three-year contract in July having played more minutes for Stéphane Moreau’s under-17s side last season than any other player. Born in Aix-les-Bains in France’s Savoie region, he has been called “the new Marco Verratti” in tribute to the Italian midfielder and recently scored on his debut for the club’s under-19s. Michut has also trained with Thomas Tuchel’s senior squad on several occasions and featured in the pre-season friendly against Sochaux in August. **[Ed Aarons](https://twitter.com/ed_aarons)**  
[Watch Édouard Michut in action](https://twitter.com/edouard_michut/status/1275844101955608576)  
  
**October 2021 update** Made his first team debut as a substitute in February and extended his contract with PSG to 2025 in the summer. But Michut’s meteoric rise has been slowed by his return to the Under-19s so far this campaign, although he is still one to look out for.  
  
**September 2022 update** Became PSG's youngest player to contribute an assist since Kylian Mbappé in January but was sent off against Angers and then sent on loan to Sunderland.  
  
**October 2023 update** Made 24 appearances on loan as Sunderland reached the play-off semi-finals but they opted not to sign him on a permanent deal. Recently linked with a loan move to Turkish side Adana Demirspor.
