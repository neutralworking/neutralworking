## Snapshot

A workaday midfielder who's been the engine of Heidenheim's rise without ever being the most technically gifted player on the pitch. Beck covers ground reliably, makes the straightforward pass, and understands positioning well enough to shield the back four. The problem is he has no invention—no ability to break lines with a killer ball or drive a progressive carry through the middle when traffic gets congested.
