## Snapshot

A press-resistant ball carrier with genuine technical composure in tight spaces — the kind of midfielder who turns defenders inside out with subtle weight shifts rather than explosive athleticism. Bénes operates best in the half-spaces where he can receive on the back foot and progress play through incisive short passing or the occasional driving run; his range isn't vast, but his decision-making in congested areas is genuinely sound. The limitation is structural: he lacks the legs to dominate a midfield three at this level, tends to get caught on the ball under genuine pressure, and doesn't offer the defensive security or athleticism that modern Süper Lig sides demand from their engine-room players. At 28, he's a solid complementary piece at a club like Kayserispor — useful in possession, dangerous in transition — but he's not the answer if you're building around midfield dominance.
