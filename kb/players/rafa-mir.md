## Snapshot

Spanish striker with real predatory instinct in the box and an eye for half-chances, possessing genuine clinical finishing when the ball arrives at pace. However, his overall movement is somewhat predictable, he doesn't create much for teammates, and his hold-up play is limited; he operates best when fed consistently by a creative midfield. Decent La Liga starter for a mid-table side but relies heavily on service.
