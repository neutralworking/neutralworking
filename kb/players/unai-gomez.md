## Scouting Notes

A press-resistant central midfielder with the technical security to play out of tight spaces; he's tidy in possession and reads the game with the kind of understated intelligence that doesn't jump off the screen but keeps Bilbao's engine ticking over. The weakness is lateral—he's not a playmaker in the creative sense, lacks the range or ambition in the final third to unlock defences, and his physical profile means he'll never be a ball-winning midfielder at the highest level. Serviceable, reliable, the exact type of player that doesn't win headlines but wins you matches.
