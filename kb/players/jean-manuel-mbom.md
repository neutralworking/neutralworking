## Scouting Notes


CountryGermany

ClubWerder Bremen

PositionMidfielder

Born24 February 2000







****

Mbom joined the football boarding school of Werder Bremen aged 13. Usually Bundesliga sides don’t take in youth players before they are 14, but the son of a Cameroonian father and German mother has impressed with his great vision. He reads the game and his opponents so well and as captain he guided Werder’s under-17 side to the runners-up spot last season behind Bayern Munich with 18 goals in 20 games from central midfield. “I can play my best when I have the game in front of me and can distribute balls,” Mbom has said. Manchester City, who are the prospect’s favourite team because his idol Yaya Touré is playing there, could do worse than take a look. **[Michael Reis](https://twitter.com/donreisino?lang=en)**
 
**October 2018 update** Was part of the first-team photo for the new season and has been training with the senior squad for most of the summer, although he is mainly playing for the under-23s. Made his debut for the Germany Under-19 team in September 2018.
 
**October 2019 update** Another strong year for the Germany Under-19 captain in which he played 23 Regionalliga games and was once included in the squad for a Bundesliga game. Has joined KFC Uerdingen in the third league on loan for the season and both clubs have high hopes for the 2019-20 campaign.
 
**October 2020 update** Made 28 appearances in 3. Liga for Uerdingen and was then thrust into action for Bremen in the Bundesliga on the second day of the season against Schalke. His manager, Florian Kohfeldt said: “It was nice to see the way he presented himself on Sunday. He was excellent. He has worked very hard to get to this moment.”
 
**October 2021 update** Steady and impressive progress, the youngster made 21 league appearances as Werder were relegated. Now a certain starter in 2. Bundesliga and has won his first two under-21 caps.
 
**September 2022 update** Bremen bounced back straight away, winning promotion to the Bundesliga at the first attempt but Mbom ruptured an achilles towards the end of April and now faces a long time on the sidelines.
