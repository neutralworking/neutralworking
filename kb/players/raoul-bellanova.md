## Snapshot

A right-back operating at an excellent level for a club of Atalanta's stature, combining defensive reliability with genuine attacking intent and the ability to drive the ball forward into dangerous areas. His positioning is sharp and he reads defensive triggers well, though he's not a one-v-one genius and can occasionally be caught in transition. An essential player for Gasperini's system and a real asset in both directions.
