## Snapshot

A seasoned Swedish stopper who reads the game with the composure you'd expect from someone who's seen it all—distributes from the back with genuine intelligence and rarely panics under pressure. His reflexes have dulled with age, and against modern forwards playing on the shoulder, he can be exposed by sharp movement and tempo; positioning is increasingly doing the heavy lifting rather than athleticism. Still the kind of presence you want in a dressing room and on a resume at this level, but he's a caretaker now rather than a long-term solution.
