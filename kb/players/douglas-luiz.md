## Tactical Profile

He is recognized for his technical quality, defensive awareness, athleticism, and attacking flair. Luiz excels in breaking up opposition play through good defensive positioning and vision, often sitting on the right of a double pivot in midfield. His offensive contributions include driving forward with the ball, delivering accurate long passes, and utilizing his powerful long shot, with a notable ability to score directly from corners.

## Career Highlights
Douglas Luiz has achieved significant milestones in his career, including winning a gold medal with the Brazil U23 team at the 2020 Tokyo Summer Olympics. He was also part of the Brazil squad that finished as runners-up in the 2021 Copa América. At club level, while with Aston Villa, he was recognized as both the Supporter's Player of the Season and Player's Player of the Season for the 2022-23 campaign. His career has seen him play for Vasco da Gama, Manchester City (with loan spells at Girona), Aston Villa, Juventus, and Nottingham Forest.
