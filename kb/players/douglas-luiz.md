## Snapshot

A ball-carrying midfielder with the kind of technical security that keeps things ticking over. Luiz glides through traffic, shifts the ball left and right, and generally keeps possession moving—but there's a passivity to him that limits his ceiling. He's not a presser, not a disruptor, and he can go missing in games that demand intensity; he's best deployed in midfields where someone else provides the grit.
