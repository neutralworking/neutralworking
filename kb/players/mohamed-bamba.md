## Snapshot

A physical, direct center-forward operating in the Portuguese second tier who relies on aerial dominance and off-the-ball positioning rather than technical sophistication. He's the type who'll win you headers in the box and troubles defenders with his aggression, but lacks the movement patterns and positional intelligence to function effectively against organized defensive schemes. Without meaningful Primeira Liga or European experience, he remains a functional asset at this level rather than an emerging prospect with genuine upside.
