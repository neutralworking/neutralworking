A steady, reliable custodian who reads the game with the experience you'd expect from a 35-year-old Austrian international—his positioning is generally sound and he distributes competently for a goalkeeper operating in the Swiss Super League. However, he's no longer a commanding presence; his reflexes have dulled perceptibly, and on the rare occasions Sion are exposed to elite attacking intensity, he can look slow off his line. At this stage of his career, he's a functional, experienced option—fine for a mid-table Swiss side, but not someone you'd trust in a genuine pressure situation.
