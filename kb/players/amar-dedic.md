## Snapshot

Amar Dedic truly embodies the Creator-Sprinter archetype, bombing down the flank with impressive pace and acceleration, coupled with the stamina to go all day. While he offers excellent balance and shielding in possession, his actual defensive duels and blocking leave a lot to be desired, making him a liability when truly tested. He's also prone to lapses in discipline, suggesting he's perhaps better suited to a more advanced Carrilero role where his defensive frailties are less exposed.
