## Scouting Notes

A winger with real craft in his left foot and the ability to find space in the half-spaces that most wide players miss. He's intelligent about his positioning and genuinely tries to do the defensive work, which is refreshing. However, he lacks the explosive acceleration to consistently beat a man at pace, and when the play slows down and becomes positional, he can disappear from matches.
