## Snapshot

ClubSporting CP

PositionDefender

Born27 February 2004

A right-back like his older brother Tomás, who played for Reading last season and is now back at Porto in the reserves. Gonçalo also played for Porto from the age of seven to 17 but decided to sign for their rivals Sporting this summer. He is a very attack-minded full-back, technically gifted and good one-on-one. He needs to become a bit more consistent and mature in his decision-making but for now he is a very interesting player for a team that likes going forward. “I’m good at one-v-ones and going forward and I also think I’m strong enough to be a good defender,” he said on his first day at Sporting, before adding: “but I know I can still improve a lot.” His coach, Rúben Amorim, said during pre-season: “It was a great deal for the club. He’s a kid full of talent.” **[Nuno Travassos](https://twitter.com/nunotravassos?lang=en)  
  
**September 2022 update** Played 10 games for Sporting last season and has now been loaned to neighbours Estoril to get more playing time in the Portuguese top-flight.  
  
**October 2023 update** The loan move to Estoril did not go quite to plan as he found himself behind to the new revelation Tiago Santos (who is now at Lille) so Esteves returned to the Lions midway through the season. He is still not playing regularly for the first team but went to the Under-19 Euros, where he helped Portugal reach the final.**
