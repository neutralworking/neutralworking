## Snapshot

Young Norwegian left-back with genuine pace and vertical recovery speed, allowing him to cover ground quickly and bail himself out of positional mistakes. His attacking instincts are promising, but he's too loose defensively and struggles with intelligent runners in the half-space—he gets dragged out of position and then can't recover because he's overcommitted.
