## Snapshot

A Belgian centre-back still in the consolidation phase of his career; he's physically imposing and capable of decent positioning, but his decision-making in critical moments can be loose, and his ball retention isn't precise enough for a team that wants to play out from the back. He functions as a squad option and will likely become a respectable league defender, but he's not yet at the level to be a long-term starter for a title contender. Growing, not yet arrived.
