## Scouting Notes

Ramazani profiles as a direct Complete Forward, showcasing strong technical attributes with blistering acceleration, pace, and take-on ability that make him a constant threat in transition, further aided by excellent balance and shielding. However, he struggles in duels and exhibits a limited mental pillar, characterized by tactical lapses and a hot-headed disciplinary record. This individualistic striker thrives in high-transition or counter-attacking systems, exploiting space behind defenses with bursts of pace rather than intricate tactical understanding. For clubs needing a raw, pacey forward, Ramazani could be a valuable asset, though his significant risk profile demands careful management and a conducive environment to nurture his raw talent.
