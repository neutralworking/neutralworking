## Snapshot

Ritzka is a physically dominant holding midfielder who excels at winning aerial battles and using his stamina to cover a lot of ground, but his limited mental attributes and lack of creativity make him a largely defensive-minded player. Tactically, he fits into a traditional 4-4-2 or 4-2-3-1 system as an anchor, providing defensive cover and allowing more creative players to flourish. A team seeking a rugged, athletic midfielder to break up opposition attacks would benefit from Ritzka's services, but his limitations mean he may not be the best fit for a side that relies on intricate passing sequences.
