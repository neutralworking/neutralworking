## Scouting Notes

A composed, left-footed centre-back who reads the game with maturity and can pick a pass from deep, functioning as a legitimate ball-progressor for Atlético's build-up. His positional discipline is excellent, and he's rarely caught flat-footed, but he's not a particularly dominant aerial defender and can look vulnerable against direct, physical strikers who get a yard of space. His best work is on the ground, orchestrating from the back.
