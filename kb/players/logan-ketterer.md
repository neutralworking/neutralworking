## Snapshot

A competent MLS goalkeeper who reads the game with reasonable intelligence and distributes adequately from the back, but lacks the shot-stopping authority or reflexive brilliance you'd want from a top-six club's first choice. He's the sort of keeper who won't lose you matches through egregious error—he's reliable in that unglamorous sense—but he won't win them either, and his positioning can be vulnerable to the modern trend of cutback play and second-phase chaos. At 32, his ceiling is permanent MLS journeyman, and that's without any particular weakness that screams for improvement; he's simply a capable depth option masquerading as a starter.
