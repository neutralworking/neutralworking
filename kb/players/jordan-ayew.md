## Scouting Notes

Veteran striker who's lost the explosive athleticism that defined his early career but retains good positioning instincts and a professional's understanding of how to operate in limited space. His hold-up play is respectable and he'll occasionally produce a flash of quality, but he's no longer a threat to defenders in terms of direct pace. A squad rotation option who'd be better suited to a Championship side where his experience carries more weight.
