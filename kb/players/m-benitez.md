A balanced marshal whose technical and tactical competencies hover at the midpoint, allowing him to function across multiple midfield roles without excelling in any. His awareness and interception work create a defensive foundation, though gaps in blocking and mid-range passing prevent him from anchoring genuinely, while his lack of vision limits him as a true play-maker. The high-press trait suggests he's most useful in aggressive systems where his close-range tackling and quick reading compensate for his inability to orchestrate play at distance. For squad-building, he's a sensible journeyman rather than a starting caliber player.
