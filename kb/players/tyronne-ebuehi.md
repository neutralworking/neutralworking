## Scouting Notes

A left-back of athleticism and aggression who defends with genuine intensity and physicality, comfortable driving into the half-space with the ball at his feet. His progressive carrying is a genuine asset in transition, and he rarely gets turned. However, his positional awareness can be sloppy—he's prone to leaving gaps when ball-watching—and he's not a technically sophisticated defender; against polished attacking play in confined spaces, he tends to rely on athleticism rather than positional craft, which becomes a liability as defenders age.
