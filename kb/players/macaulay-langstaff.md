A genuine poacher with sharp instincts inside the box and the predatory movement that suggests he's studied countless hours of strikers at work. His finishing is reliable and his positioning excellent, but he's entirely dependent on service; he won't turn and face defenders, won't operate in the channels, and his link-up play is functional at best. A specialist, effective within narrow parameters.
