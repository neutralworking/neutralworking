Recent Form

Dilane Bakwa joined Nottingham Forest in September 2025, and his initial contributions to the club have seen him feature across multiple competitions. In the 2025/2026 Premier League season, Bakwa has played 343 minutes across 10 matches, registering one assist and receiving one yellow card, with an average FotMob rating of 6.42. He has also participated in five UEFA Europa League matches, two UEFA Conference League Qualifiers, one FA Cup tie, and one EFL Cup game for Forest, accumulating further minutes on the pitch.

Prior to his move to Nottingham Forest, Bakwa started the 2025/2026 season strongly with Strasbourg in Ligue 1, contributing one goal and three assists in just three appearances, playing 105 minutes. His recent appearances for Nottingham Forest in April 2026 include an 18-minute cameo in a 1-0 home win against FC Porto, earning a 6.6 FotMob rating, and playing 61 minutes in a 1-1 away draw against the same opponent, where he achieved a 7.0 FotMob rating. He was an unused substitute in a Premier League draw against Aston Villa in April 2026.

Tactical Profile

Dilane Bakwa operates primarily as a right winger, though he demonstrates tactical versatility by also being able to play on the left flank and occasionally in central attacking roles. As a left-footed player, he frequently cuts inside from the right wing, a characteristic that creates space for other attackers. His playing style is marked by flair, confidence, and a strong ability to beat defenders in one-on-one situations, often employing a "stand-start" dribbling technique and close control to navigate past opponents.

Bakwa is known for his effective ball-carrying, averaging 2.74 carries into the final third per 90 minutes, and is less reliant on blistering pace, preferring to receive the ball to feet rather than exploiting space in behind defenses. He is adept at both hugging the byline to deliver crosses (averaging 5.87 crosses per game) and cutting inside to provide passes from the half-space, making him an unpredictable threat. His strengths include key passes, dribbling, crossing, taking set-pieces, and long-range shooting, while his tackling is identified as a weakness. He frequently draws fouls, averaging 3.13 per 90 minutes, indicating his effectiveness in disrupting opposition play and winning advantageous set-piece opportunities.

Career Highlights

Born on August 26, 2002, Dilane Bakwa developed through the youth systems of Boissy, Lusitanos Saint-Maur, and Bordeaux. He signed his first professional contract with Bordeaux in May 2019 and made his senior debut for the club in a Ligue 1 match against Nice on September 27, 2020. During his time at Bordeaux, Bakwa made 36 appearances across all competitions, contributing five goals and eight assists. His breakthrough season came in Ligue 2 during the 2022/23 campaign, where he was a consistent presence, missing only three league fixtures.

In August 2023, Bakwa transferred to Strasbourg, signing a four-year deal. In his inaugural season with Strasbourg (2023/24), he featured in 36 matches, scoring five goals, including one against Paris Saint-Germain, and providing nine assists. The 2024/25 season saw him further improve his output, with six goals and nine assists in 30 Ligue 1 appearances, which helped Strasbourg secure a seventh-place finish and qualify for the UEFA Conference League. His impressive performances led to a move to Nottingham Forest on September 1, 2025, in a deal reportedly worth €35 million including add-ons, where he signed a five-year contract extending until 2030. Bakwa has also represented France at various youth levels, including U16, U17, and U21, making his U21 debut in September 2024.
