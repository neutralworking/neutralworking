## Snapshot

A technically gifted attacking midfielder who operates with confidence in possession and can find clever passes through congestion. He's got excellent close control and can press intelligently. The knock is that his decision-making can be slow — he sometimes overcomplicates in the final third when a simple pass is required — and his workload off the ball is inconsistent. Against a pressing team, he can become a liability rather than an asset. He's a good player in the right system, not a top-tier one.
