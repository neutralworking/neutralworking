## Snapshot

Crafty winger with excellent close control and the intelligence to work half-spaces effectively, finding pockets of space that others miss. However, his final ball is often rushed or overhit, and his work rate off the ball doesn't match his technical ability. Better as a super-sub or in matches where Huddersfield can control possession—he'll be exposed if asked to cover defensively against athletic opponents.
