## Snapshot

A journeyman 'keeper now in his mid-thirties who has knitted together a career in MLS and lower-tier American competitions without ever truly settling as a number-one at the elite level. He's a functional, reliable presence between the sticks — organizes his line competently, deals with crosses at a decent clip, and won't gift you howlers on distribution. That said, he lacks the reactive athleticism and commanding presence of a top-tier MLS starter; his footwork under pressure is workmanlike rather than crisp, and he's the sort of goalkeeper you win with through a brilliant defense in front of him, not because of any individual brilliance. At this stage of his career, he's squad depth or a starter at a mid-table franchise, nothing more.
