## Snapshot

A robust, physical centre-forward with decent hold-up play and a genuine aerial presence in the box—the kind of target man who makes life easier for wider creators and can finish clinical chances when they arrive. However, his touch can be heavy in transition, he lacks the explosive acceleration to hurt defences in space, and his off-the-ball movement is pedestrian; he's not the type to create separation through intelligent positioning. In Serie A, even at a club like Bari, he's functional depth rather than a solution—serviceable on his day, but limited upside at 29.
