## Snapshot

Güven Yalçın is a pacey and agile striker, with impressive jumping and acceleration abilities. He excels at making runs in behind the opposition defense, but his lack of discipline and composure can be a major issue. He often struggles to finish chances, and his lack of creativity makes him reliant on service from his teammates. His dueling ability is also a concern, as he can be easily muscled off the ball by physical defenders.
