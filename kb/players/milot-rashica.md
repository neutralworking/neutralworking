## Snapshot

A winger-forward capable of genuine directness in the final third, with sharp acceleration and the spatial awareness to find pockets of space in congested areas. His final ball is inconsistent and he can be a touch selfish in transition, choosing the low-percentage shot over the simpler square pass, and his press isn't relentless enough to compensate. A useful contributor to Beşiktaş's attack who works better as a changer of tempo than an orchestrator.
