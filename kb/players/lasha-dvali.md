## Scouting Notes

A composed, left-footed centre-back who reads the game intelligently and carries the ball out from the back with genuine purpose—his progressive passes regularly unlock transitions. He's aerially commanding and positionally sound, the sort of defender who makes the difficult look routine. However, he lacks explosive athleticism to recover against genuinely quick strikers, and his passing range, while reliable, rarely stretches beyond 40 yards with real conviction.
