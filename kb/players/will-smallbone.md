## Scouting Notes

A technically sound midfielder with good footwork in confined spaces and the ability to execute a genuine range of passes—short, long, diagonal, and progressive carries all feature in his repertoire. However, he lacks the physicality to dominate English football's midfield transitions and can be bypassed too easily; there's also a consistency issue, as he occasionally disappears from matches entirely. Talented but incomplete.
