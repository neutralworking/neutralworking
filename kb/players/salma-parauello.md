## Snapshot

A dynamic right-winger with genuine explosive pace and a direct running threat that forces defences to respect verticality in a way many Barcelona attackers now don't. She combines pace with surprising technical security and has the intelligence to work back defensively, which has become increasingly vital for Barcelona's modern 4-3-3. The limitation is that her decision-making in the final third remains inconsistent—she often opts for the low-percentage shot when simpler, higher-value passes exist—and her left foot is a weakness when forced onto it.
