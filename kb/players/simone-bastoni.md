## Snapshot

A central midfielder-cum-deep playmaker who compensates for limited athleticism with genuine technical security and an almost obsessive tidiness in possession. He moves the ball quickly and progressively through the thirds, rarely hurries decisions, and has the intelligence to position himself in useful spaces for receiving between the lines. The fundamental weakness is pace of thought against genuinely high-intensity pressing; he's not a player who accelerates proceedings when the tempo needs lifting, and his left-foot range is reliable but unremarkable.
