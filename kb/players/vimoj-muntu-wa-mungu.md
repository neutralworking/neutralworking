A young left-back with genuine pace and athleticism who shows promise in transition and possesses the physical tools to develop into a solid Ligue 1 starter, though his decision-making in wide areas remains raw and his defensive positioning is still maturing. He can be caught too square to goal when pressed, and his crossing—while enthusiastic—lacks consistency and disguise. At 21, he's very much a work in progress, but the profile is encouraging: he'll likely become a functional starter within 18-24 months if given consistent minutes and proper coaching.
