## Scouting Notes

A composed, left-footed centre-back who reads the game with genuine intelligence and uses his body well to shepherd attackers into uncomfortable spaces. His progressive passing from the back is tidy rather than spectacular, but he's reliable in possession and rarely panics under pressure. The weakness is pace—he'll struggle against genuinely explosive runners, and Brighton's high line occasionally exposes his lack of explosive recovery pace.
