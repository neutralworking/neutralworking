## Snapshot

CountryNetherlands

ClubFeyenoord

PositionDefender

Born18 July 2000

****

Geertruida is yet another left-footed defender who is developing fast at Feyenoord’s youth centre Varkenoord. Recent years have seen several talented defenders pass through the reigning Dutch champions (Nathan Aké, Stefan De Vrij, Daryl Janmaat, Bruno Martins Indi, Jeffrey Bruma, Karim Rekik, Rick Karsdorp and Terence Kongolo) and make it to big leagues. Geertruida, born near Feyenoord’s De Kuip stadium, was on the bench against Manchester City in the Champions League defeat in September. “A modern and physically strong defender,” said the technical director, Martin van Geel, when Geertruida signed his first contract. **[Bart Vlietstra](https://twitter.com/BVlietstra?lang=en)**
 
**October 2018 update** Another year of progress for a player knocking on the door to Feyenoord’s first team. Has made one appearance and plenty more will surely come.
 
**October 2019 update** Is getting more and more minutes every season but it is not guaranteed that he will become a regular starter, especially after Feyenoord signed the Argentinian defender Marcos Senesi.
 
**October 2020 update** What a season for the young defender: he made 17 Eredivisie appearances and played six times in the Europa League, gaining valuable experience throughout the season. Appears equally adept at right-back and centre-back he has started all three games so far this season and even scored against ADO Den Haag.
 
**October 2021 update** Was a regular last season at Feyenoord but a knee injury has meant that he is having to convince the new coach, Arne Slot, of his worth. Extended his contract after months of negotiations.
 
**September 2022 update** Played a big part as Feyenoord reached the Europa Conference League final in 2021-22. Mainly plays at right-back, despite strong competition for the position, but often drifts into central areas to be a “secret” midfielder.
