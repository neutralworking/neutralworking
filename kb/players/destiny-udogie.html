Destiny Udogie is a 23-year-old Italian left-footed fullback who currently plays for Tottenham Hotspur. Standing at 187 cm, Udogie is known for his utility and fullback capabilities.

Recent Form

In the 2025/2026 season, Destiny Udogie has made 22 appearances across all competitions, including the Premier League, EFL Cup, and Champions League, contributing one assist but no goals. In the Premier League specifically, he has played 15 matches, accumulating 932 minutes with an average FotMob rating of 6.65, and has received three yellow cards. His passing accuracy from central positions stands at 73.08%, and his build-up effectiveness rating is 244, placing him 167th in the league for this metric.

Recent match performances in early 2026 include playing 90 minutes against Sunderland (6.9 FotMob rating), 45 minutes against Nottingham Forest (6.2 rating), 24 minutes against Atletico Madrid (6.0 rating), and 90 minutes against Borussia Dortmund (8.0 rating). He has also featured in matches against Manchester United, Manchester City, Eintracht Frankfurt, and Burnley during this period. Udogie has faced some disruptions to his season, having missed games due to injury.

Tactical Profile

Udogie operates primarily as a dynamic and aggressive attacking left-back or wing-back, a role he has embraced effectively within Tottenham's high-tempo system under Ange Postecoglou. His playing style is characterized by significant pace, strength, and a strong left foot, allowing him to make explosive overlapping runs and actively join attacks like a winger. He is comfortable in possession and adept at driving forward with the ball, often inverting into midfield spaces to aid build-up play and create numerical advantages.

Defensively, Udogie is aggressive in duels and demonstrates good ball recovery, tracking back effectively. While his physical attributes are considered elite, some analyses suggest his positional awareness can occasionally be exposed, particularly against more experienced attackers, as he continues to adapt to the defensive demands of the Premier League. He has previously shown versatility, having been deployed as a central midfielder in his early career. Udogie himself has cited Marcelo as his inspiration, reflecting his attacking mindset as a full-back.

Career Highlights

Destiny Udogie made his professional debut for Hellas Verona in 2020 at the age of 17. He then moved to Udinese in 2021 on a loan deal with an obligation to buy, where he made 37 appearances and scored five goals during the 2021/2022 season. Tottenham Hotspur secured his permanent transfer in August 2022 for approximately €18 million, immediately loaning him back to Udinese for the 2022/2023 season.

Udogie joined the Spurs first team in the summer of 2023, making his Premier League debut in August of that year. He scored his first Premier League goal for Tottenham in December 2023 against Newcastle and subsequently signed a new contract with the club, extending until 2030. His impressive performances in the 2023/2024 season earned him a nomination for the Premier League Young Player of the Season award. On the international stage, Udogie earned his first senior cap for Italy in October 2023. He was also part of the Tottenham squad that won the Europa League in 2025 and the J. League World Challenge in 2024.

Condition / Medical History

Destiny Udogie has experienced several injury setbacks throughout his career, particularly in recent seasons. In April 2024, he underwent thigh surgery for a quadriceps issue, which prematurely ended his 2023/2024 season. The 2025/2026 season has also seen him contend with injuries, including a knee issue that caused him to miss the initial games of the campaign and a minor knee irritation in October 2025.

He has also dealt with muscle injuries in December 2025 and February 2026. Tottenham has adopted a cautious approach with Udogie due to his recent injury history, managing his return to play carefully. His injury record also includes a strain injury in 2025 and a hamstring injury in 2024.
