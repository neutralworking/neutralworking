A wide forward masquerading as a center-forward—quick, energetic, and capable of inverted runs that create half-space opportunities for teammates. His pressing is genuine and his movement off the ball shows football intelligence, but he's not a 15-goal-a-season striker; he's a functional auxiliary attacker who works best in a system that doesn't ask him to be the focal point. At 29, he's a squad stabilizer for a Süper Lig club, not a breakout talent or a player built to lead a line.
