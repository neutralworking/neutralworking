## Snapshot

A functional, intelligent forward whose main currency is positioning and movement in tight spaces rather than athleticism or technical brilliance. He's decent at finding half-space pockets and has a reasonable first touch for a number nine, but at 32 he's lost the explosive step that might have made him genuinely dangerous in the Bundesliga. His best work comes from understanding defensive shape and slipping into gaps, which is fine for a mid-table Köln side but he won't do the pressing or recovery work a top-four team demands.
