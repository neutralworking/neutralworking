## Snapshot

A left-footed number 10 who still shows flashes of the technical quality that made him promising—tidy first touch, willingness to operate in the half-spaces, capable of a defence-splitting pass—but at 33 he's become a player who operates in short bursts rather than dictating tempo. His decision-making has grown increasingly ponderous, and he lacks the athleticism to press or cover ground consistently over 90 minutes, making him vulnerable to the intensity of modern pressing systems. He remains a reliable squad depth option in Argentine football, but the gap between his peak self and his current utility has become quite pronounced.
