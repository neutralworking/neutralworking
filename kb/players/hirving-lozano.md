## Snapshot

A 30-year-old mezzala virtuoso at his peak, Lozano combines electrifying pace and acceleration with rare balance and sharp first touch, making him immediately dangerous on the dribble and in half-space manipulation. His mental fragility—reflected in weak blocking, poor discipline, and duel inconsistency—is the counterweight: he is unreliable when asked to defend or resist pressure, and his directness can squander possession architecture. For a Dutch or elite European club seeking a creative catalyst in advanced midfield, his availability and on-ball durability justify investment, though he demands tactical protection and systemically-enforced discipline.
