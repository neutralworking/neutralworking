## Scouting Notes

An English central midfielder with good press resistance and a tidy range of passing, capable of shuttling effectively and breaking up play with reasonable timing, though he lacks the dynamism or creative spark to genuinely progress the ball in dangerous areas. He'll win his duels and rarely loses the ball cheaply, but the lack of vertical threat or half-space intelligence means he's a functional squad player rather than a creator. A hard-working cog, not an engine.
