## Snapshot

Aerially commanding centre-back who dominates his area in both phases and reads set-play situations with admirable clarity. A leader by temperament with good positioning instincts, he makes himself big and difficult to play through. On the ground, however, his ball-playing range is limited and he struggles with press resistance — ask him to progress play out from the back and you'll see him resort to long clearances rather swiftly.
