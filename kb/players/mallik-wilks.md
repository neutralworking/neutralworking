## Scouting Notes

Mallik Wilks is a quintessential 'Sprinter' and direct Extremo, whose blistering acceleration and pace allow him to terrorize defenders and make devastating inverted runs from wide positions. While he possesses fantastic balance and shielding to protect the ball, his abysmal defensive attributes – tackling, interceptions, and awareness – mean he offers absolutely nothing off the ball, making him a significant tactical burden for any team wanting to press or defend cohesively.
