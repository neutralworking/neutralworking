## Scouting Notes

A Venezuelan winger operating at a level well below where his technical gifts might suggest — capable of finding dangerous half-space pockets and delivering incisive crosses from the byline, but at 33 he's become more functional than vital. His left foot remains his weapon, and he understands how to manipulate space in transition, yet the athleticism that once made him a genuine threat has drained away; he'll beat a man once per match but won't do it three times. Defensively he remains a liability, and in the modern game, that's become an unaffordable luxury for a player whose creative output no longer compensates.
