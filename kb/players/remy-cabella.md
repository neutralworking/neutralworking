A journeyman attacking midfielder still offering craft and press resistance in possession—he threads passes through congested spaces and has the experience to manage a game's tempo without panicking. At 36, though, he's a pure squad depth option: the legs have gone, he won't press effectively in modern football's intensity, and he's useful only in matches where Olympiacos will dominate the ball without serious defensive demands. A capable older pro for cup games and rotating heavy European weeks, nothing more.
