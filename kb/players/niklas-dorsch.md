## Scouting Notes

A centre-back with excellent reading of the game and real composure on the ball. Dorsch's distribution is a genuine strength—he can play long switches and isn't content to simply clear—but he's not particularly quick and can be exposed by direct runners if the midfield doesn't shield adequately. A thinking defender in a club that needs to think.
