## Snapshot

A quick, direct right-winger with genuine pace in behind and the willingness to take people on, but his technical range is narrow—he's at his best running in a straight line, and his passing game, crossing precision, and ability to operate in tighter spaces are all decidedly average. He's a useful depth option or a game-changer from the bench if you need to stretch a tired defence, but he lacks the creative intelligence or consistency to be a first-choice winger at an ambitious club. More effective as chaos than craft.
