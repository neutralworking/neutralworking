## Scouting Notes

A 27-year-old right-sided mezzala with athleticism and reactive sharpness, Carrasquilla compensates for limited tactical range through direct, flamboyant running and decent ball-carrying ability—he exploits space through take-ons rather than positioning intelligence. His hot-headedness and discipline problems make him a liability in possession-heavy systems; he works best in transitions where his acceleration and balance flourish and his tactical gaps matter less. Rising status suggests upside, but the tactical ceiling remains real and a move to a more positionally demanding league could expose him quickly.
