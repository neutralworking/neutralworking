Guilherme joined Konyaspor in January 2020 and became a significant figure for the club, playing 226 official matches, scoring 13 goals, and providing 38 assists, setting a club record for assists. His career before Konyaspor included stints with several clubs across Europe, such as Jagiellonia, Termalica Nieciecza, and FCSB. During his time in Romania with FCSB and Petrolul, he won multiple domestic trophies, including one Liga 1 title and two Cupa Romaniei titles. He also gained experience in European competitions, making appearances in the Europa League with various clubs.
