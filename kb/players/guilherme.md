## Scouting Notes

A relayeur built on physical attributes—stamina, jumping, balance—and crossing threat rather than midfield control, offering value as a dynamic interior wing-back or inverted fullback. Discipline and tactical inconsistency betray his mental profile; while he carries the ball and launches set pieces effectively, he neither shields nor distributes with the consistency demanded of a true metronome. Best deployed in systems where his crossing and aerial presence offset his lack of positional anchoring, ideally alongside a more structured midfield partner.
