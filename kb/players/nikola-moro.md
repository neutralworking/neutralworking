## Snapshot

A Croatian midfielder who brings defensive cover and the capacity to shield the back line; he is tidy on the ball and rarely loses possession cheaply. His limitation is his lack of penetration—he does not create, does not carry the ball with particular conviction, and does not burst forward with timing. A functional water-carrier, useful in structured midfields but uninspiring as a starter in ambitious teams.
