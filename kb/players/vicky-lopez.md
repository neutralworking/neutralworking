## Snapshot

A technically clean left-winger with assured ball-carrying ability and the kind of rhythmic, flowing movement through congested spaces that suggests she understands Barcelona's passing sequences intuitively. She's comfortable inverting into midfield, her crossing is weighted properly, and she works back defensively without protest. The issue is that she lacks a truly devastating end product—her shot selection is safe rather than adventurous—and against elite right-backs who press her high and tight, she can become a sideways passer rather than a creator.
