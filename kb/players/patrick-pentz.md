## Scouting Notes

A capable shot-stopper with decent footwork for a Austrian Bundesliga starter, Pentz has the fundamentals down but lacks the elite distribution and sweeping instincts you need at the highest level. He's decent on set pieces and commands his box adequately, but he's prone to the occasional unforced error when trying to play out from the back—you won't see him threading progressive passes or acting as a genuine sweeper-keeper. Solid enough for a mid-table Austrian side, but not the sort who elevates a team into European contention.
