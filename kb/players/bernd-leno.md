## Scouting Notes

Still a genuinely composed distributor with excellent reading of space and a willingness to play out from the back with technique that belies his age. His shot-stopping remains reliable, his positioning is intelligent, and he's the sort of experienced goalkeeper who earns playing time through consistency rather than charisma. The decline in his athleticism is evident on long-range efforts and quick reflexes, but he remains a legitimate top-flight operator.
