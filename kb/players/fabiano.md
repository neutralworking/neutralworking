## Snapshot

A physically imposing tuttocampista who thrives in direct, competitive midfield exchanges—his aerial prowess and reactions make him reliable in transition and set-piece scenarios. His technical foundation is solid across ranges, but he lacks the tactical discipline and creative range to drive sequences himself; he's most effective as a supporting midfielder in structured systems where his aggression and build-up contributions are channeled rather than improvised. At 26 with strong mental resilience, he offers immediate midfield ballast for clubs seeking a combative connector, though his limited vision and blocking weaknesses mean he cannot anchor a midfield alone or orchestrate possession recovery.
