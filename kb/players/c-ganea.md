## Snapshot

C. Ganea operates as a Passer-Powerhouse Tornante, contributing offensively with strong long and mid-range passing, effective crossing, and good acceleration and stamina for wide areas, all while pressing high. However, his general technical ability is a noted weakness, as is his defensive blocking and overall guile, limiting his capacity for intricate play. He fits the mould of a modern Tornante, a winger who contributes extensively to possession with his passing range and delivers crosses, rather than an inverted dribbler, ideal for sides that build through wide channels and require high work rates off the ball. Ganea's moderate physical and mental attributes make him a dependable option for clubs needing a structured, high-pressing wide midfielder with good distribution from the flanks; his technical limitations mean he's unlikely to thrive in possession-heavy, intricate systems, but he offers good squad depth for teams valuing work rate and direct play, providing solid market value for a mid-table side.
