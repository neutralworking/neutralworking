## Snapshot

A classic Prima Punta, Acquah uses his immense physical strength and superb jumping ability to dominate defenders in aerial contests, making him an ideal focal point for direct play. While he possesses decent pace, his near-total absence of defensive attributes like marking and tackling means he offers absolutely nothing out of possession, making him a very one-dimensional forward. He's a bulldozer, but only when attacking.
