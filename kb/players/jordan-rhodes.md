## Scouting Notes

Veteran striker with good movement inside the box and a lethal finishing record, understanding how to steal space in tight areas that younger forwards miss. But at 36 he's immobile and can't hold play up or press effectively, making him a liability outside the box. Works only as a poacher in a structured team—absolutely requires supply and can't function in a system asking him to work in deeper territory.
