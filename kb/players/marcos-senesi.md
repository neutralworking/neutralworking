## Snapshot

Argentine centre-back with excellent reading of the game and a proactive approach to defending; he steps up well, his distribution is confident, and he's genuinely comfortable in possession. The physical side is adequate rather than exceptional — he's not the quickest in a footrace, and if a forward can get separation early, Senesi is susceptible. Very good defender in a structured system.
