Recent Form

Diogo Dalot has maintained a consistent presence in Manchester United's lineup during the 2025/2026 season, featuring in 26 league games with 2256 minutes played, contributing one goal and three assists in the Premier League. Across all competitions, he has played 36 matches, scoring one goal and providing three assists, accumulating 2675 minutes on the pitch. His performance has been noted for reliability, with a FotMob rating of 7.11 in the Premier League, and he has been recognized as one of Manchester United's more technically proficient players.

Dalot's defensive contributions have also been significant this season, ranking 29th in tackles won, 39th in interceptions, and 60th in clearances among league players. He has been highlighted as one of the team's best in tackles completed and aerial duels. Despite some past injury concerns, he has demonstrated strong availability, playing a high volume of matches and minutes for Manchester United.

Tactical Profile

Diogo Dalot is a versatile full-back or wing-back, primarily operating on the right flank but capable of playing on the left. He is known for his speed, technical ability, and offensive contributions, which include effective dribbling, crossing, and long-range passing. Dalot's tactical intelligence allows him to adapt his role within the team's system, either inverting into midfield to aid ball progression or maintaining width to stretch the opposition.

His defensive game has seen considerable improvement, particularly in aerial duels where he excels, and in his tackling and interception abilities. While he possesses good ball control and is a progressive passer, some analyses suggest occasional inefficiencies in his dribbling and decision-making in the final third. Nevertheless, his ability to carry the ball under pressure and his consistent passing accuracy make him a dependable asset in various tactical setups.

Career Highlights

Dalot has achieved significant success at both club and international levels. With Manchester United, he has won the FA Cup in the 2023-24 season and the EFL Cup in 2022-23. His international honors with Portugal include winning the UEFA Nations League in 2025 and the UEFA European Under-17 Championship in 2016.

He also contributed to Portugal's youth teams reaching the UEFA European U21 Championship final in 2021 and the UEFA European U19 Championship final in 2017. Dalot made his senior international debut for Portugal at UEFA Euro 2020 and scored his first two international goals during the 2022–23 UEFA Nations League. A loan spell at AC Milan from 2020 to 2021 was instrumental in his development, particularly in refining his defensive skills while maintaining his attacking prowess.

Personality

Diogo Dalot is regarded as a supportive and dedicated individual within the team. He was noted for helping Bruno Fernandes settle into Manchester United, driving him to training and fostering a strong bond. His commitment to the club is evident in his consistent effort, with reports indicating he "always gives 100%" and "doesn't complain when dropped." His positive influence and contributions were recognized by his peers, as he was named Manchester United's Players' Player of the Year in the 2023/24 season.

Condition / Medical History

While Dalot has demonstrated high availability in recent seasons, playing a substantial number of minutes for Manchester United, he has experienced several minor injuries in 2025. These include a knock injury, a strain injury, a muscle injury, and a calf injury. Throughout his career, he has also dealt with various other issues such as hamstring, hip, groin, and meniscus injuries. Despite these setbacks, he has often returned to play quickly, showcasing his resilience and commitment to fitness.
