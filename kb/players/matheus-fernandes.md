## Snapshot

Matheus Fernandes operates as a Terrier Tuttocampista, excelling in defensive midfield with strong tackling, awareness, and interceptions, showing composure under pressure and contributing occasionally with close-range finishing. His tendency to engage in a high press is notable, though a hot-headed nature frequently leads to discipline problems, and his vision for progressive passes is limited, particularly from mid-range. This player fits the mould of a ball-winning midfielder, an essential component in high-intensity, counter-pressing systems that require constant disruption of opposition play. A solid option for clubs seeking a robust, industrious presence in midfield to break up play and maintain possession, Fernandes offers a good blend of technical and mental attributes; however, his recurring discipline issues and restricted passing range could become a liability for teams aspiring to intricate build-up play, marking him as a moderate risk acquisition.
