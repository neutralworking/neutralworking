## Snapshot

A pace-driven defensive midfielder with strong technical foundation and stamina, but tactical lapses and occasional indiscipline undermine his reliability as a primary pivote—he requires clear structure and instruction rather than autonomous decision-making. His first touch and mid-range distribution suggest he can function as a pressing-oriented ball-winner in high-tempo systems, yet his hot-headedness and weak tactical reading create risk in high-stakes moments. Best positioned as a squad rotation option for clubs emphasizing gegenpressing rather than as an uncompromising defensive leader.
