Snapshot

An English winger with directness and dribbling ability that troubles defenders in the half-spaces; he can operate on either flank and creates problems through movement and press resistance. His finishing is the concern—he shoots reasonably but lacks the clinical precision of truly elite wingers—and defensively, he can be caught out of position against sustained pressing. A young player developing well, not yet complete.

Jonathan Rowe, an English winger, is currently playing for Serie A club Bologna, having transferred from Olympique Marseille in August 2025 for a fee of €17 million. He was born on April 30, 2003, making him 22 years old, and primarily uses his right foot. Standing at 178 cm, Rowe is known for his role as a winger.

Recent Form

Jonathan Rowe has shown promising form for Bologna in the 2025/2026 Serie A season, recording 2 goals and 1 assist across 1,250 minutes played, with an average FotMob rating of 6.76. He has experienced a "purple patch" in front of goal during early 2026, contributing six goals in total, with five of those coming in a frantic start to the year. Rowe has become a vital component of Vincenzo Italiano's side, despite initially struggling to adapt to the tactical rigours of Italian football after his move from Marseille. His performances have led him to describe this period as his best since arriving at Bologna.

Tactical Profile

Rowe is a right-footed winger capable of operating across the attack, often deployed on the left side as an inside forward. His dribbling ability is a key attribute, characterized by a low center of gravity, excellent ball control, and speed, making him difficult to dispossess. He excels at progressive carries, ranking in the top 97th percentile for midfielders in Europe's next 14 leagues for this metric, and is also in the 99th percentile for touches in the attacking penalty area per 90 minutes. Rowe's confidence in advanced attacking positions and his ability to change pace at high speed make him a dangerous presence in the final third.

Career Highlights

Jonathan Rowe's career has seen significant progression, including a loan spell at Olympique Marseille in the 2024/2025 season where he made 28 appearances and scored 3 goals, before his permanent transfer to Bologna in August 2025. He made his debut for Marseille on August 25, 2024, and scored his first goal for the club in a 3-2 victory over Lyon on September 22. Prior to his moves abroad, Rowe had a breakthrough season with Norwich City in 2023/2024, scoring five goals in his first five games across all competitions and earning the EFL Young Player of the Month award for August 2023. He also received the Sky Bet Championship Goal of the Month award in January 2024. Internationally, Rowe has represented England at the U21 level, making his debut in October 2023 and scoring in a 9-1 win against Serbia. He was part of the England U21 squad that won the UEFA European Under-21 Championship in 2025, scoring in the final against Germany.

Condition / Medical History

Jonathan Rowe has experienced several injuries throughout his career. In 2025, he suffered a thigh injury from November 10 to November 29. Earlier in 2024, he had a muscle injury that kept him out from February 16 to April 12, and a heel injury from November 12 to December 3, 2023. His medical history also includes ankle injuries in 2023 and 2022, and a knee injury in 2025. These injuries have sometimes restricted his playing time, including a period of two months out during the 2023/2024 season.
