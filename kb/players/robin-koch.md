## Scouting Notes

A centre-back with excellent distribution range and the kind of calm on the ball that allows Frankfurt to progress into midfield with purpose. His one-v-one defending is still solid, but his aerial dominance has diminished with age and he can be exploited by aggressive, technical forwards who run between the lines. Cerebral defender who manages space well; no longer a world-beater, but absolutely the kind of steady influence a side fighting for European qualification needs.
