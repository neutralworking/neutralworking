## Snapshot

A willing, energetic forward whose primary currency is his work rate and willingness to compete for second balls rather than technical brilliance or spatial intelligence. He'll press and harry opponents dutifully, which has value, but his link-up play is clumsy and his finishing remains a liability—too often wasteful with the chances he does create for himself. Effective as a squad aggressor against tired defenses late in matches but not a consistent starting solution.
