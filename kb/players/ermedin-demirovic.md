## Snapshot

A centre-forward with excellent technical ability and the capacity to drop into half-spaces to link play or turn and shoot; he's comfortable facing goal and his passing range is genuinely impressive for the position. The limitation is that he lacks the physical dominance or aerial authority to bully defenders in traditional ways, so he's ideally suited to possession-heavy teams where he can operate in tight space. A very good player in the right system, but not universally dominant.
