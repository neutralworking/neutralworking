## Snapshot

William Yarbrough is a solid, dependable goalkeeper who excels at shot-stopping and claiming crosses, providing a sense of security and stability at the back. However, his distribution and ability to play out from the back can be suspect at times, often resorting to long kicks down the field rather than attempting to play shorter passes to his defenders. Yarbrough's decision-making and anticipation are also not always on point, leading to the occasional mistake, but his overall consistency and experience make him a reliable option between the posts.
