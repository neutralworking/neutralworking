## Snapshot

A left-back with more athleticism than technical security, quick to the point of sometimes being reckless in his movement. Edun will recover pace and make aggressive challenges, but his passing is often heavy-footed and he can be caught in possession in dangerous areas because he overcommits. He's the kind of player who'll win you a tackle you didn't know you needed and lose you a game with a casual pass backwards; functional as rotation but unreliable as a regular starter.
