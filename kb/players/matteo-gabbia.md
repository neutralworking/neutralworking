## Snapshot

An Italian centre-back of solid fundamentals — he's positionally sound, wins his aerial duels, and reads the game with typical Serie A discipline. His weakness is in the build-up; his passing is functional rather than creative, which matters when you're trying to progress the ball from deep. He's a perfectly adequate defender for a title-contending team, but not the kind who makes the system more fluid.
