## Snapshot

An inverted left-winger who cuts onto his right foot to create and finish, offering directness and pressing intensity that disrupts opposition build-up play. His acceleration is elite, and he's comfortable dribbling through traffic in the final third. The limitation is his inability to play as a traditional left-back replacement when Bayern invert their shape, and he can be predictable when isolated one-on-one against a fresh full-back.
