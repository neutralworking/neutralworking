## Snapshot

A Brazilian centre-back with excellent physical tools—he's quick, strong, and genuinely committed to winning duels. He's positionally aware enough to slot into a back three or four without major liability. The problem is concentration; he has catastrophic lapses where he switches off entirely, and against technically superior forwards he can be outmaneuvered. A solid wingback alternative, too, given his athleticism.
