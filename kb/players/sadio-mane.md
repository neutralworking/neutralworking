## Snapshot

A winger whose pace and intensity have remained intact, though he's no longer the transcendent figure of his Liverpool peak. He presses relentlessly, dribbles directly, and offers genuine goal threat. His decision-making in the final third has regressed—he takes too long to release the ball sometimes—and he's not as devastating when operating in tight spaces as he was five years ago.
