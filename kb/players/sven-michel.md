## Snapshot

A functional, bread-and-butter centre-forward who knows where the goal is but operates without much finesse or athleticism. Michel is the type who gets his goals through positioning and a decent first touch rather than explosive power or technical brilliance. At 35, he's a reliable depth option for a mid-table Bundesliga side, capable of a decent run of form, but he lacks the mobility to press effectively or the touch to play in transitions, making him increasingly dependent on service in the box.
