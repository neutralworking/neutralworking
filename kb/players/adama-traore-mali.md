## Snapshot

Explosive ball-carrier with the kind of low centre of gravity and change-of-direction ability that leaves defenders grasping at air in transition. Operates best on the wing, where his acceleration and dribbling in tight spaces create chaos and avenue for cut-backs or inverted crosses. His finishing is genuinely poor, though, and he lacks any meaningful left foot — once defenders adjust to his pattern play (always cutting inside), he becomes considerably easier to contain.
