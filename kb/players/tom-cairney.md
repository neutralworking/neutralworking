## Snapshot

A midfielder whose technical security and experience make him invaluable in the Fulham ecosystem, even as his legs have inevitably slowed. He operates most effectively in deeper positions where he can use his excellent range of passing and his ability to receive under pressure to start moves. The elephant in the room is that he cannot cover ground anymore; against high-intensity pressing from elite sides, he'll be found out, and his inability to drive box-to-box means he's best suited to a structured, possession-dominant setup.
