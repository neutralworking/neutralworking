## Snapshot

A twitchy, direct winger who thrives in transition and can manufacture half-chances from nothing with his low centre of gravity and sudden acceleration down the line. His crossing is functional rather than incisive, and he lacks the spatial awareness to consistently operate in the half-spaces where modern football is won. Useful squad depth at a European club, but not a player who'll drive attacking moves from open play.
