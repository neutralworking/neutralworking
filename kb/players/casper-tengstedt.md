## Snapshot

A burly, genuinely mobile number nine who operates with encouraging intelligence in the channels and half-spaces rather than camping on the shoulder. He's press-resistant, can hold the ball through pressure, and has a natural sense of where teammates are moving, which elevates him above the typical bruising forward type. The limitation is obvious: his finishing consistency and his aerial dominance aren't elite, so he's a very good complementary striker rather than a world-beater, but for the Eredivisie he's a proper central forward.
