## Snapshot

An aggressive, ball-playing centre-back who reads the game intelligently and uses his body well in one-v-one situations, particularly adept at cutting out through-balls before they become dangerous. His limitation is aerial dominance—he's not a consistent winner in the air—and occasionally his aggressive positioning leaves him exposed to rapid combinations, but he's a capable cornerstone for a Ligue 1 defence.
