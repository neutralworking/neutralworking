## Scouting Notes

A center-back with reasonable technical security for the position and the capacity to play out from the back in simple, effective ways. He's strong in duels and reads the game better than many in this division, with good anticipation for interceptions. The limiting factor is his mobility on the turn; he can look ponderous when forced to shift laterally quickly, and top-level strikers will exploit that, making him a liability against sides pressing aggressively upfield.
