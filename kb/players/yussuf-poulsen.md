## Snapshot

A street-smart, experienced center-forward who operates as much as a pressure point and link-up player as a pure goal-scorer these days. He's clever with his body, knows how to make space in tight areas, and will carry the ball forward when given time. At 31 and without the explosive athleticism he once had, he'll find Bundesliga defenses challenging—he's slow off the mark and can get isolated if the midfield isn't synchronized, making him a difficult fit for a club needing sustained pressing intensity.
