## Snapshot

A Serbian winger of 30 whose primary asset is his direct running and ability to create chaos in tight spaces through his aggression and low centre of gravity. He'll beat a man on his day, but there's no subtlety to his game—his decision-making in the final third is often rushed, and he doesn't possess the technical execution of a top-flight creator. A useful squad option for Mallorca, but not someone who'll unlock a stubborn defence.
