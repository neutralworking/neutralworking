## Scouting Notes

A Croatian attacking midfielder who operates with genuine technical quality and an understanding of when to accelerate play versus when to recycle. He moves into good areas of the pitch and has the passing range to thread balls into forward areas. The limitation is consistency—he goes missing in difficult matches and doesn't impose himself as a leader. He's a secondary creator, not a primary one.

Brilliant young attacking midfielder, who enjoyed a wonderful first full season as

a Dinamo pro last term, setting up goal upon goal as the Blues won yet another Croat league title, voted Young Player of the Year and earning a spot in the

Outstanding technically, upright and elegant, he boasts a wonderful range of pass, is sound in his decision-making and is very dangerous indeed when breaking forward from deep positions. Diminutive in stature, his low centre of gravity is a major asset, as is the threat he poses from a direct free-kick.
