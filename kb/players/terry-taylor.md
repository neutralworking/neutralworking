## Snapshot

Taylor is a talented young midfielder who is comfortable on the ball and has a good range of passing, but he can be guilty of overplaying at times, taking too many touches and allowing opponents to close him down. He has a good understanding of the game and makes some intelligent runs, but he can struggle with the physicality of the game, often being outmuscled by more experienced opponents. He has a lot of potential, but he needs to develop his strength and athleticism to compete at the highest level.
