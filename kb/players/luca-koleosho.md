## Snapshot

Italian winger with genuine pace and an appetite for direct running, particularly dangerous when asked to attack space down the line rather than cut inside. His decision-making in the final third is erratic—he often chooses a pass when a shot is on, or vice versa—and his defensive application is minimal, making him a one-dimensional offensive outlet. Too raw to be trusted week-in, week-out at this level, but there's enough speed and aggression to warrant continued development.
