## Snapshot

Hart remains a professional save-maker with excellent positioning and reflexes that haven't entirely deserted him at 38, yet the gulf between his 2012 self and this version is instructive—his distribution is pedestrian and his ability to play a high line is gone. Celtic have him as an insurance policy while Engels and Johnston are injured, and that's his honest level: a competent cup-game goalkeeper who can steal a night in Europe but shouldn't be Celtic's first choice in a title race. He's functional, experienced, and past it.
