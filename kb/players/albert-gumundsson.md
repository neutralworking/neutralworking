## Scouting Notes

An inverted winger-turned-attacking midfielder with exceptional close control and a sudden-death left foot; he thrives cutting onto his dominant side and delivering crosses or shooting from marginal angles. His press resistance is genuinely impressive for a player his age, and he can carry the ball vertically through traffic. The trade-off is positional discipline — he drifts inboard and sometimes forgets his defensive responsibilities, leaving fullbacks exposed.
