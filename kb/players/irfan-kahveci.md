## Recent Form

İrfan Kahveci has experienced a dynamic period in his career, marked by a strong performance in the 2023-2024 season with Fenerbahçe and a recent loan spell. During the 2023-2024 campaign, he emerged as a key player under coach İsmail Kartal, contributing significantly with 12 goals in 31 Süper Lig matches and adding 6 goals in 14 UEFA Conference League appearances. He also marked his 100th appearance for Fenerbahçe in October 2023.

For the 2024-2025 season, Kahveci recorded 2 goals and 3 assists in 26 Süper Lig matches for Fenerbahçe. In January 2025, Fenerbahçe extended his contract until June 2028, signaling their long-term commitment to the player.

Currently, for the 2025-2026 season, Kahveci is on loan at Kasımpaşa, having joined them on January 8, 2026, with an expected return to Fenerbahçe on June 30, 2026. While at Kasımpaşa, he has registered 1 goal and 3 assists in 11 Süper Lig matches. Prior to his loan, he played 8 Süper Lig matches for Fenerbahçe in the same season.

## Tactical Profile

İrfan Kahveci is primarily a central midfielder, known for his technical prowess and attacking attributes. He is left-footed and excels in a playmaking role, often contributing from set pieces and posing a threat with long-range shots. His versatility allows him to operate effectively as an attacking midfielder or on the wing, particularly on the right side.

Throughout his career, Kahveci's role has evolved. He initially played as a left-back and later as a deep-lying or wide midfielder. Upon joining İstanbul Başakşehir, former manager Abdullah Avcı deployed him as a playmaker, leveraging his vision, passing ability, technical skill, and understanding of the game. Kahveci himself has expressed a personal preference for playing in central midfield. His close control, speed, dribbling, and crossing abilities further enhance his offensive impact.

## Career Highlights

Kahveci has achieved significant success in Turkish football, including winning the Süper Lig title with İstanbul Başakşehir in the 2019–2020 season. He also secured the Türkiye Kupası (Turkish Cup) with Fenerbahçe in 2022–2023.

Internationally, Kahveci made his debut for the Turkish national team on March 23, 2018. He has represented Turkey in major tournaments, participating in UEFA Euro 2020 and being included in the expanded squad for Euro 2024. A notable individual achievement includes scoring a hat-trick against RB Leipzig in the UEFA Champions League on December 2, 2020, making him one of only three players to achieve this feat in a losing effort in the competition's history. He was also named Footballer of the Year in 2020.
