Recent Form

Igor Paixão has demonstrated a solid attacking presence for Olympique Marseille during the 2025/2026 season. In Ligue 1, he has featured in 26 matches, contributing 6 goals and 5 assists, resulting in a total of 11 goal contributions. His goal-scoring rate stands at 0.31 goals per 90 minutes, with a shooting accuracy of 42.55% from 47 shots taken. He maintains an average FotMob rating of 7.11 in the league, reflecting consistent performances.

Beyond domestic competition, Paixão has also made an impact in the UEFA Champions League during the 2025/2026 campaign. Across 8 matches, he has scored 4 goals and provided 1 assist. Despite sometimes needing to sacrifice his individual attacking output for the collective demands of Roberto De Zerbi's system, particularly due to defensive responsibilities, he remains a heavily utilized and important player for Marseille.

Tactical Profile

Igor Paixão operates primarily as a forward or left winger, showcasing versatility across attacking midfield roles including attacking midfielder (AM), left midfielder (LM), left winger (LW), attacking midfielder central (CAM), and right winger (RW). Standing at 168 cm and favoring his right foot, he is known for his strong characteristics in key passes, finishing, and holding onto the ball.

His playing style is characterized by a tendency to cut inside, dribble past opponents, and deliver crosses, often drawing fouls due to his direct approach. Paixão's dribbling prowess and playmaking abilities are significant, enabling him to break defensive lines and create scoring opportunities for himself and his teammates. He is described as a dynamic winger whose offensive numbers highlight his risk-taking and consistent contribution to goals and assists. Furthermore, he contributes defensively, engaging in duels and tracking back, indicating a strong work rate beyond just attacking flair.

Career Highlights

Igor Paixão began his professional journey at Coritiba, graduating from their academy in 2019 and later having a loan spell at Londrina between 2020 and 2021. He became a key player for Coritiba, notably scoring seven goals during their 2021 Série B campaign, which helped the club return to Brazil's top tier.

His move to Europe saw him join Feyenoord in 2022, where he achieved significant success. With Feyenoord, Paixão secured the Eredivisie title in the 2022/2023 season, followed by the KNVB Cup in 2023/2024, where he scored the decisive goal in the final. He also won the Johan Cruyff Shield in 2024 and was named Dutch Footballer of the Year for the 2024/2025 season. During his time at Feyenoord, he scored a brace in a dominant 6-0 victory over Ajax in April 2024 and received the Eredivisie Goal of the Year award in 2024. Paixão has also represented Brazil at the U23 level in 2023 and was included in the preliminary squad for the senior Brazil national team in February 2025. He transferred to Marseille in the summer of 2025 for a club-record fee.

Condition / Medical History
