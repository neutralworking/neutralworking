## Scouting Notes

A winger of real technical accomplishment who thrives in tight spaces and has the acceleration to make defenders look foolish in the half-space, though his crossing is a genuine weakness and he drifts into anonymity when the chance to dribble isn't there. He's not a sustained presser and he can ghost out of matches when the focus shifts to structural defending, which is the mark of a player who needs the right system and license to express himself. Thrilling on his day, unreliable as a cornerstone.
