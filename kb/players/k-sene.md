## Snapshot

K. Sène is an Assassin Poacher with a strong technical base and impressive finishing ability from both close and long range, complemented by his guile and capacity for take-ons in tight spaces. However, his overall attacking impact is hindered by limited vision and creativity to link play, alongside recurring discipline issues that can lead to unnecessary suspensions or on-field frustration. This profile is a classic number 9, focused almost exclusively on scoring goals and occupying defenders in the box, a tradition that prioritizes individual predatory instincts within structured offensive patterns. Clubs in need of a direct goal threat who can convert chances ruthlessly, particularly within systems that supply rather than demand creative output from their striker, would find value in Sène, but his mental fragility and lack of collective attacking vision present a tangible risk that management must address.
