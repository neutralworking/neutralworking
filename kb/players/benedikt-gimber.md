## Scouting Notes

A compact, left-sided midfielder who understands his lane and works tirelessly to provide defensive cover. Gimber's distribution is tidy and he rarely loses the ball carelessly, but he offers almost nothing in transition or when asked to break the opponent's shape. He's a trusted lieutenant, not a quarterback—useful in a well-drilled system but exposed if the midfield needs creativity.
