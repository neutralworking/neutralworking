## Scouting Notes

A left-back operating in the twilight of his career at Sparta Rotterdam, van Aanholt retains the technical security and positional intelligence that made him a reliable Premier League operator for years, but his explosive first-step acceleration has eroded noticeably. He'll still deliver a decent cross from deep and won't panic under pressure, but he's no longer quick enough to recover when beaten one-on-one by direct wingers—a critical liability in the modern game. At 35, he's a functional depth option in the Eredivisie, but asking him to start regularly against any team with a pacey attacking threat is asking for trouble. His reading of the game remains his saving grace; his legs no longer are.
