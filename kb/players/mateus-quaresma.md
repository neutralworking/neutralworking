## Snapshot

Mateus Quaresma is a classic Destroyer-Powerhouse, bringing immense physicality and tireless stamina to the defensive full-back role, capable of powerful inverted runs. While his acceleration and reactions are top-tier, enabling him to cover ground rapidly and engage in surprisingly effective take-ons, his truly abysmal technical attributes prevent him from contributing meaningful progressive passes. Ultimately, a suspect mental game combined with poor technique means he's often a liability when asked to do anything beyond winning the ball and making simple clearances, lacking the finesse required in advanced areas.
