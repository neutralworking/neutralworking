A full-back still capable of positioning himself well and reading the game defensively, but increasingly reliant on experience rather than athleticism to compensate for diminished recovery pace. He can execute a competent cross and won't get catastrophically exposed in a structured shape, yet he's vulnerable to genuinely quick wingers and lacks the drive to influence play in the half-spaces anymore. A rotation piece or emergency starter in a compact defensive system.
