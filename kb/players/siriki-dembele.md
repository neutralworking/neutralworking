## Snapshot

A Scottish winger with a low centre of gravity and genuine ability to shift defenders in tight spaces, though his end product remains a perennial frustration. He's better at creating the opportunity than the chance itself, and his decision-making in the final third veers between impulsive and laboured. He's talented enough to deliver moments of magic, but not consistent enough to be the difference-maker a top-six club would demand.
