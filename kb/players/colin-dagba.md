## Snapshot

A right-back with decent athleticism and a willingness to press high, though he lacks the technical refinement you'd want for a Ligue 1 regular. He's competent in one-v-one situations and gets forward reasonably, but his reading of the game in transition is pedestrian and his delivery from deep is rarely incisive. At 27, he's a reliable squad option rather than a long-term solution.
