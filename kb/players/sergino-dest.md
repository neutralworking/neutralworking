## Snapshot

A left-back with aggressive pressing intensity and some genuine ball-carrying ability—he's willing and he has the confidence to take players on—but his defensive positioning is genuinely unreliable and he can get spun by intelligent attackers using pace and timing. He's best as an inverted fullback in a structured, possession-dominant setup where he's not exposed to direct play; in an open game, he's a liability. Interesting profile with clear strengths, but currently too inconsistent to trust in a title challenge.
