## Scouting Notes

Marcos Leonardo is a raw, athletic centre-forward, whose pace and jumping ability make him a threat in the box, but his lack of awareness and dueling ability mean he often finds himself isolated and unable to hold up the ball, and his marking is a significant liability. He has the potential to be a prolific poacher, but his all-round game needs significant development. His playing style is that of a traditional runner, always looking to get in behind the defence, but he can be predictable and struggles to create for himself or others.
