## Snapshot

A right-back who remains excellent despite his age, his set-piece delivery still genuinely elite and his ability to read the game from a defensive standpoint still sharp. He's positioned himself as one of the league's best attacking full-backs over the years, and he hasn't truly lost that edge. However, he can look pedestrian against fresh, pacey wingers, and his physical decline is occasionally exposed when pressed to recover ground.
