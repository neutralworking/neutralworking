## Snapshot

A right-back whose stock has risen considerably since moving to Fulham; he's energetic, genuinely two-footed, and has that rare ability to operate as a quasi-winger, timing his arrivals in the box and delivering from deep with both feet. The weakness, paradoxically, lies in his reliability in one-v-one defending when isolated — he's occasionally sloppy reading the game's shape and can be beaten by pacey, direct runners. He's best deployed in a system where he's asked to be an attacking outlet rather than a pure defensive anchor.
