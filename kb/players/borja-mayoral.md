## Scouting Notes

A proper centre-forward who understands positioning and movement in and around the box, making intelligent runs and occupying defenders effectively, but he's not a particularly dynamic presser and offers little in build-up play beyond being a outlet. He thrives on service and finishing chances from predictable situations; you wouldn't describe him as someone who creates chaos or operates effectively in asymmetrical systems. For a side like Getafe that relies on structure and set pieces, he's a functional option, though he's never going to drag a team over the line through sheer individual brilliance.
