A left winger of decent athleticism whose direct running and willingness to take people on offer functional attacking thrust down the flank. His decision-making in the final third is ordinary—he often holds the ball a touch too long or plays the predictable pass—and he lacks the elite pace to consistently beat defenders on pure speed. A solid depth option for a Bundesliga side, but not someone who transforms matches.
