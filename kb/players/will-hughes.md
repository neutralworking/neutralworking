## Scouting Notes

A crafty midfielder whose experience and off-the-ball intelligence provide ballast in midfield, though his legs have largely gone and he's now a conductor rather than a driver. Hughes excels at finding space in congested areas and his passing range is underrated, but he cannot press with intensity or cover significant ground, making him dependent on Palace sitting deep and compact. He's serviceable as a rotation option or starter in a low-block system, but represents tactical limitation rather than possibility.
