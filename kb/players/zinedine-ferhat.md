## Snapshot

Zinedine Ferhat is a skilled and creative midfielder, with impressive balance and acceleration abilities. He often struggles to track back and support his defense, and his inability to read the game and anticipate opposition attacks is a concern. His composure is decent, but he can be prone to errors when attempting to play in high-pressure situations.
