## Scouting Notes

A veteran poacher who's lived off instinct and positioning rather than athleticism for years now, and at 35 that calculus has shifted decisively against him. His movement in the box remains intelligent—he still reads defensive lines and finds half-yard advantages—but the explosive first step that once made those runs dangerous has deserted him. He's a functional outlet in a Saudi league context, but at this stage he's a luxury finisher for a club that needs a CF who can hold play or press with intent; neither is in his toolkit anymore.
