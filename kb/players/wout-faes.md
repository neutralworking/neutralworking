## Snapshot

A centre-back with genuine ball-playing ambition—he'll stride forward with the ball and isn't afraid to find a forward-thinking pass from deep—which marks him out from more conventional defenders. His aerial dominance and physical presence make him a reliable last line, and his left-footed composure allows him to switch play with accuracy. The trade-off is that his aggressive approaching sometimes exposes him to sharp forwards who can exploit the space he vacates, and he can be drawn out of position when tempted by the ball.
