## Scouting Notes

A steady, unremarkable goalkeeper approaching the end of his career with the kind of competence that gets you through domestic Russian football without embarrassing yourself. His distribution is functional but uninspired, and at 35 he's increasingly vulnerable to the modern game's demands for sweeper-keeper involvement and quick decision-making in transition. He's a competent shot-stopper in a conventional sense, but offers nothing special—no exceptional reflexes, no commanding presence that subordinates strikers, no genuine sweeping instinct that modern attackers punish. For a club like Zenit in a weakened domestic league, he's sufficient; anywhere else, he'd be a backup, and rightly so.
