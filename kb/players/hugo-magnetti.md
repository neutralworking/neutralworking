## Snapshot

Tenacious box-to-box midfielder who wins the ball with real intensity and harries opponents into poor decisions — his work-rate is exemplary and he's learned to recycle possession quickly. Limitations emerge in the final third and in tight technical moments; he's a grafter, not a creator, and his limited range means Brest build slowly through him rather than around him. A reliable workhorse in the engine room.
