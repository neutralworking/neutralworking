## Snapshot

A winger with explosive acceleration and genuine dribbling ability in tight spaces; his inverted runs from the right show football intelligence and he can create chaos in the final third. However, Philogene's decision-making in moments of pressure is inconsistent and his defensive positioning needs hardening. At 24, he's a very good attacking prospect who hasn't yet settled into sustained excellence.
