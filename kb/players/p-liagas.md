## Snapshot

P. Liagas is a rugged Connector who excels in the Metodista role, using his strength and discipline to win duels and provide a platform for his team to build from. While his technical ability is moderate, Liagas's positional discipline and build-up contribution make him a valuable asset in midfield, and his ability to win close-range battles is a notable strength. However, his limited creativity, mid-range ability, and take-on skills may make him a predictable presence in the opposition's half.
