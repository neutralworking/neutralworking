## Snapshot

A seasoned Australian custodian operating at a respectable A-League standard with genuine shot-stopping ability and a calm presence in possession. Arnold distributes adequately for a goalkeeper of his era — no Ederson, but competent with his feet and sensible in his decision-making on the ball. The weakness is a lack of elite athleticism or reflexive speed that would allow him to compete at a genuine continental level; he's a reliable, intelligent keeper who'll keep you in matches but won't steal them.
