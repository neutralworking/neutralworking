## Scouting Notes

A young left-back at 21 with the physical tools—pace, athleticism, natural defensive instinct—but still raw in the transitions and reading of play that separates the merely quick from the genuinely complete. Small is the kind of prospect you watch with genuine interest because the foundation is there; he's brave in challenges and willing to engage, but he'll have moments where you see the youth in his positioning, the occasional brain lag that comes with experience still to be earned. He needs consistent senior football and a patient coach, not a panacea, and Charlton's likely position—mid-table Championship or thereabouts—is the right environment for his development. The clock isn't ticking yet, but he can't afford another year of fringe minutes.
