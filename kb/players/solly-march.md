## Snapshot

A left winger-cum-fullback hybrid who's endured enough soft-tissue issues to warrant real concern about longevity, yet still possesses that rare gift of explosive acceleration on the ball and the spatial awareness to find teammates in the half-space. His crossing is serviceable rather than elite, and defensively he's found wanting against proper width, but his intelligent running and ability to manipulate a full-back into poor positions makes him more than just a pace merchant. A squad pillar who can deliver on the big occasion if fit.
