## Snapshot

Olise is a Virtuoso operating as an Inventor from the right wing, boasting strong technical skills, vision, and physical resilience. His exceptional balance, first touch, and passing accuracy allow him to dictate play and create opportunities, making him a potent counter-attacking threat and set-piece specialist. While highly effective in direct play, his tactical discipline can waver, and his heading ability is notably weak. This player fits the mould of an inverted winger who comes inside to create, reminiscent of classic playmaking wingers but with added modern athleticism and directness. Currently rising in his career, Olise is a premium asset for clubs seeking a creative wide player and big-game performer who guarantees durability and attacking flair, despite minor tactical inconsistencies and occasional lapses in discipline.
