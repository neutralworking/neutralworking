## Snapshot

A functional box-to-box midfielder who does the unglamorous work of covering ground and breaking up play without much distinction. He's neither creative enough to start transitions nor defensively imposing enough to be a lynchpin, which makes him useful depth in a settled side but unremarkable when called upon. His passing is safe rather than progressive, and he doesn't drive the ball forward with intent.
