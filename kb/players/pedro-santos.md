## Scouting Notes

Pedro Santos is a skilled and composed midfielder who excels at beating defenders with his dribbling ability. His shielding and through balls make him an asset in possession, but his lack of dueling ability and discipline can make him a liability in defensive transitions. Santos' blocking ability is also a concern, often struggling to win the ball back in defensive situations.
