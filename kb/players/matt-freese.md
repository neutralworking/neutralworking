## Snapshot

A technically aware goalkeeper whose passing out of the back is a genuine asset and whose composure in possession elevates NYCFC's press resistance. However, he lacks the reflexive explosiveness you want in a modern shot-stopper, and his tendency to play passively on set pieces—both defending and initiating from—limits his ceiling. A capable squad player who competes for starts in MLS; unlikely to anchor a truly ambitious project.
