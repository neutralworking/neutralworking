## Scouting Notes

A Montenegrin midfielder with fluid dribbling through the half-spaces and a willingness to carry the ball forward, the kind of young player whose touch and awareness suggest there's something interesting to work with. The gulf between his technical ability on the ball and his decision-making — particularly in transition, where he can dawdle — is pronounced; he's not yet a threat in either box with any consistency. At 20, he's a genuine prospect, but Juventus will need patience before he's ready for sustained top-flight action.
