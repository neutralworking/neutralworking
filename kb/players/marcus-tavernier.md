## Snapshot

Right-winger who wants to operate in the half-space and create from there rather than stay wide; he has genuine technical quality and an eye for a pass. The pace is adequate but not explosive, which means he's at his best when having time to operate — press him quickly and he can look uncomfortable. Decent attacking midfielder playing out of position, rather than a truly elite winger.
