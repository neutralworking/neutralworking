## Snapshot

A centre-forward who is physically strong and good in the air, thriving on service into his channels or in a direct style of play. However, he lacks the technical range to be a focal point in a possession-based system; he's not a creative link or a dribbler, and he needs the ball to come to him simply. Useful as a Plan B, functional as a starter in the right setup.
