## Snapshot

A seasoned left-winger-cum-fullback whose best years are genuinely behind him, but who remains a functional, intelligent operator in the Newcastle system. He's lost the explosive athleticism that once made him a genuine threat in the wide areas, and pressing from the front now feels laboured rather than coordinated. What he retains is positional sense, an understanding of when to invert into midfield, and the kind of leadership that counts in a dressing room—but he's a squad rotational option now, not a starter at this level.
