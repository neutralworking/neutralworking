## Snapshot

A young central midfielder with good physical attributes and reasonable ball-carrying ability in straight lines, but his tactical awareness is still developing and he can be ponderous in tight spaces. He shows commitment in the tackle and some nice moments of passing range, yet he doesn't consistently dictate tempo or recognize when to slow play down versus when to accelerate it. Prospect with potential but currently a backup; needs more senior-level reps to understand positioning and timing at pace.
