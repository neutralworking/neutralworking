## Scouting Notes

D. Kruse is an industrious Terrier Tuttocampista, characterized by exceptional stamina, composure, and a reliable first touch, enabling him to excel in a high-press system and effectively shield the ball. While his endurance and reactions are strong, a flamboyant streak is balanced by a consistent lack of creativity and guile in possession, coupled with recurring discipline issues that can cost his team. This player aligns with the robust, ball-winning midfielders of high-energy systems, a workhorse whose primary function is to disrupt and transition, rather than orchestrate intricate attacking movements. Clubs seeking a durable, high-effort midfielder with moderate physical and technical attributes would find Kruse a dependable asset, particularly for his tactical adherence to a high press; however, his limited offensive guile and disciplinary record imply he is best suited for a supporting role where his primary task is regaining possession rather than initiating complex attacking sequences.
