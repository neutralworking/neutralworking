## Snapshot

An experienced, no-nonsense goalkeeper who distributes the ball cleanly and has the reading of the game you'd expect from a 33-year-old journeyman. Koubek is solid on the line and commands his area, but he's not the sweeper-keeper modern Bundesliga demands, and his reflexes have dulled enough that he can look slow to crosses in congested boxes. He's a perfectly adequate insurance policy for a side not challenging for European football.
