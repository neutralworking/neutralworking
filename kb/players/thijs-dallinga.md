## Snapshot

A willing, industrious centre-forward who presses with genuine intent and offers Bologna legitimate physical presence in the box. His movement is straightforward rather than sophisticated — he wins balls through effort rather than positioning — and his left foot is genuinely useful, but he's not a creator, not particularly mobile for a striker his age, and will struggle against elite defences that don't panic under pressure. Functional option in Serie A's middle reaches; a ceiling issue rather than a technical one.
