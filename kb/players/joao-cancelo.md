## Scouting Notes

A full-back who has transcended his position through press resistance and progressive carry—the kind of defender who genuinely affects how his team builds from the back, threading passes into the half-spaces that most defenders wouldn't even see. At 31, he remains a picture of technical composure and intelligence, capable of playing inverted or advanced depending on the system's demand. The vulnerability is real, though: he can be caught out positionally by direct runners, and his physical recovery speed has naturally diminished with age, making him less effective in high-intensity transitions where he'd previously thrive.
