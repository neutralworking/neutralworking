## Snapshot

Edu Expósito, an Artisan Playmaker best suited as a Mezzala, boasts a strong technical base highlighted by excellent shielding, first touch, reactions, and composure, coupled with moderate physical stamina. His flamboyant style and set-piece ability offer creative outlets, but his limited tactical understanding, poor discipline, and inability to effectively block demonstrate a player more focused on offensive craft than defensive solidity or tactical adherence. He embodies the tradition of the midfield artist, a player who dictates tempo and creates chances through technical finesse and vision, thriving in systems that allow him freedom to roam and connect play, rather than adhering to rigid tactical instructions. Espanyol relies on his creative influence, but clubs considering him must be prepared to integrate a player with tactical deficiencies and disciplinary risks, ideally in a system where defensive responsibilities are shared by others.
