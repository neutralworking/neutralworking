## Snapshot

ClubDC United

PositionMidfielder

Born5 November 2003

********

The Liberian-born midfielder is eligible to play for both the US and the country of his birth but has so far worn the stars and stripes at under-16 level. He has impressed for DC United’s reserve team and has also trained with Borussia Dortmund’s under-19 squad. Nyeman is a talented box-to-box midfielder who should thrive once he bulks up, and can also act as an advanced playmaker. Made his MLS debut in September. **[Tom Lutz](https://twitter.com/tom_lutz)  
  
**October 2021 update** Not 18 until next month he already has 29 MLS games to his name at the time of writing. “He just takes the space like he’s a veteran player,” the defender Donovan Pines said.  
  
**September 2022 update** The midfielder made the move to Europe this summer, leaving DC United for SK Beveren in Belgium.  
  
**October 2023 update** Back in the United States, this time on loan with Salt Lake, but has spent most of his time playing in the Next Pro Series rather than for the first team in the MLS.**
