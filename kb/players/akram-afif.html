Recent Form

Akram Afif has maintained exceptional form through the current 2025-2026 season and recently concluded major tournaments. For Al Sadd in the ongoing 2025-2026 season, he has recorded 16 goals and 17 assists across 35 matches as of mid-April 2026, demonstrating significant impact on goal contributions. In the Qatar Stars League specifically, he has registered 14 goals and 12 assists in 1,846 minutes played, earning an average FotMob rating of 8.29. His club, Al Sadd, recently secured qualification to the quarter-finals of the AFC Champions League Elite, with Afif expressing satisfaction with the team's performance.

His outstanding performance in the 2023-2024 season saw him contribute 26 goals and 11 assists in 22 matches for Al Sadd, leading him to win the top goal-scorer award and helping his team clinch their 17th league title. This strong club form was complemented by a stellar showing in the AFC Asian Cup 2023 (played in early 2024), where he was the tournament's top scorer with eight goals, including a hat-trick of penalties in the final against Jordan, and was named the Most Valuable Player. He also provided three assists during the tournament, playing a crucial role in Qatar's successful defense of their title.

Tactical Profile

Akram Afif operates primarily as a left winger, a position from which he showcases his multifaceted attacking abilities for both Al Sadd and the Qatar national team. He is recognized for his creativity, intelligent movement, and composure under pressure, effectively combining dribbling prowess with precise passing to pose a constant threat in the final third. His versatility allows him to adapt his role as needed, functioning as a dribbler, goalscorer, or creator, and he is adept at finding spaces both in relation to teammates and away from the ball to dismantle defenses.

During the 2023 AFC Asian Cup, Afif demonstrated his tactical flexibility, often starting wide on the left in a 4-3-3 formation but also shifting into a second striker role within a 3-5-2 setup. He frequently dropped deep into his own half to collect the ball, highlighting his involvement in build-up play. Advanced statistics from the tournament underscore his impact, ranking him joint first for shot-ending carries, goal-ending carries, and chance-creating carries, while also placing him high in open-play expected assists and non-penalty expected goals.

Career Highlights

Akram Afif has accumulated an impressive array of individual and team honors throughout his career. He is a two-time AFC Asian Cup winner with Qatar, lifting the trophy in 2019 and again in 2023. His performance in the 2023 edition was particularly notable, earning him the Golden Boot as the top scorer with eight goals and the Most Valuable Player award. Afif has also been named the AFC Player of the Year twice, in 2019 and 2023, making him only the third player in history to achieve this distinction.

At the club level with Al Sadd, Afif has been instrumental in securing multiple Qatar Stars League titles, including in the 2018-19, 2020-21, 2021-22, 2023-24, and 2024-25 seasons. He has also won the Amir Cup, Qatar Cup, and Sheikh Jassim Cup with Al Sadd. Individually, he has been recognized as the Qatar Football Association's Player of the Season on four occasions (2018-19, 2019-20, 2021-22, and 2023-24) and was the top scorer in the Qatar Stars League for the 2023-24 season.

Personality

Akram Afif is characterized by his dedication, talent, and unwavering passion for football, embodying Qatar's growing ambitions on the global stage. He demonstrates composure under pressure, a trait evident in his critical penalty-taking during high-stakes matches, including his hat-trick in the 2023 AFC Asian Cup final. Following his recent AFC Player of the Year award in October 2024, Afif expressed a strong desire to achieve even more, aiming for a third Player of the Year title and an AFC Champions League Elite trophy with Al Sadd.

Afif openly acknowledges the importance of teamwork and a strong support system, publicly dedicating his 2023 AFC Player of the Year award to his wife for her sacrifices and support. He also emphasizes the collective effort required for success, stating that he cannot achieve his goals alone and needs everyone to stand with him. His statements reflect a grounded and ambitious individual who is committed to continuous improvement and making history for his country and club.
