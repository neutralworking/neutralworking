Left-back with serious ambition in the final third; he carries the ball well down the flank and isn't afraid to drive into the half-space or cut inside on his right. Defensively he's functional but occasionally exposed by direct runners—he can be caught high and doesn't have the recovery pace to compensate. At this level, the attacking output justifies the defensive risk, but he's not a shut-out player.
