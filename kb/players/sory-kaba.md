## Snapshot

A veteran poacher with a no-nonsense approach to centre-forward play—he understands positioning and isn't afraid to get stuck in, making him a reliable source of physical presence and occasional goals in a lower-tier European league. His game is built on simplicity: he waits for service, finishes when he gets it, and wins fouls rather than friends. The issue is that at 30, with limited technical range and no capacity to break defensive lines through progressive dribbling or passing, he's become a fairly one-dimensional proposition—useful as a short-term fix or impact sub, but not a foundation piece for any serious tactical architecture. He'll score the odd important goal, but he won't unlock anything.
