## Snapshot

A winger with real directness and athleticism who's found Newcastle a much better fit than Manchester United's demands. He attacks space with genuine conviction, cuts onto his right foot naturally, and has developed a decent work rate out of possession. But he remains somewhat predictable in his patterns and isn't consistently effective in the final third—his expected assists rarely match his shot volume.
