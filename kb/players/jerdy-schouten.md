## Scouting Notes

A disciplined central midfielder with proper positional intelligence and the ability to shield the backline effectively—good at breaking up play, decent short-passer, and he understands pressing triggers. He's not a creator and his range of passing is limited; he won't break lines or operate in the half-spaces with any real invention. An excellent functional midfielder for a well-structured team, but he's the kind of player who needs genuine quality around him to shine—he amplifies good systems rather than creating them.
