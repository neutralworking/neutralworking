## Scouting Notes

An aggressive, direct forward who gets himself into goalscoring positions through intelligent movement and the physical strength to hold up play. His first touch can betray him in congested areas, and he's not a natural finisher despite his positioning instincts. Useful player, especially in transition, but doesn't have the technical refinement you'd want from a lead striker at a genuine contender.
