A ball-winning midfielder with excellent positional discipline and genuine awareness in the defensive phase, capable of breaking play effectively and transitioning possession smartly. His creativity is limited—he's not a playmaker—and against technically superior midfields in Europe, he can be bypassed, but domestically he remains a durable, reliable operator for Marseille's engine room.
