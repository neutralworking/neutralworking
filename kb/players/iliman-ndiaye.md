## Scouting Notes

Senegalese winger-forward with genuine athleticism and directness; he can pick a pass in transition and his vertical runs create chaos in crowded spaces. What holds him back from the 85+ tier is consistency—he'll disappear entirely in some matches, and his decision-making in the final third is patchy. On song, he's genuinely dangerous; off-colour, he's a passenger.
