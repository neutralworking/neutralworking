## Snapshot

A reliable centre-back who organises well and reads the game at a functional level, with good positioning to intercept and a tidy left foot for progression. He's the sort of player you don't notice if things go well, which is often the highest compliment for a defender. His athleticism is average for the position and he struggles against genuinely quick forwards in one-on-one situations; he needs a defensive line to shelter him.
