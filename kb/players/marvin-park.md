## Scouting Notes

A right-sided winger with reasonable dribbling ability and genuine pace on the ball—he can take a man and find space in the final third, which is the minimum requirement for his position at this level. The issue is consistency and decision-making; his final ball wavers between the inspired and the overwrought, and he hasn't yet developed the intelligence to know which moments demand directness versus which call for a cutback. For a 25-year-old in the second division, he's a legitimate starter, but the gulf between occasional flash and sustained control is still too wide for a step up.
