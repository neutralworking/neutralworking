## Snapshot

A pacy, tactically disciplined right-back who understands positioning and doesn't get dragged out of shape by intelligent movement; his work rate down the line is solid if unspectacular. The weakness is obvious: he's not a ball-progressor in the modern sense, and his inability to play out from the back or drive into midfield makes him a passenger in possession phases. Fine at a mid-table club, but you'd want someone more technically assured at an ambitious outfit.
