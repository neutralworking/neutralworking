Jovetić is known for his dribbling, technical ability, and creativity.[3] His playing style has led to comparisons with Roberto Baggio. He began his career with FK Partizan, winning the double of Serbian Superliga and Serbian Cup in 2008, then joined Fiorentina for £8 million. In five seasons at the Italian club, he scored 40 times in 134 matches, prompting a £22 million transfer to Manchester City. In his first season in England, he won the League Cup and the Premier League. Jovetić is a Montenegrin international, having previously represented his team at under-21 level. He made his senior international debut in 2007, in Montenegro's first international football match as an independent country.
