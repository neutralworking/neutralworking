## Scouting Notes

Almiron, a quintessential Extremo, thrives as a Sprinter-Dribbler with blistering pace, incredible acceleration, and endless stamina, making him a nightmare for defenders in wide channels. He can deliver incisive through balls, but his lack of guile and low intensity mean he rarely varies his approach or provides the creative spark needed to unlock stubborn defenses. Furthermore, his struggles in duels mean he's easily dispossessed if he can't outrun his marker.
