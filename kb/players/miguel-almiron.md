Almiron, a quintessential Extremo, thrives as a Sprinter-Dribbler with blistering pace, incredible acceleration, and endless stamina, making him a nightmare for defenders in wide channels. Furthermore, his struggles in duels mean he's easily dispossessed if he can't outrun his marker.
