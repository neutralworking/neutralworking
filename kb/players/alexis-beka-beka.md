## Snapshot

A box-to-box midfielder with genuine athleticism and a willingness to carry the ball into the final third, Beka Beka operates best in a side that values tempo and directness rather than intricate build-up play. He's tenacious in transition and not afraid of the physical side, but his decision-making in the half-spaces can be rushed, and he lacks the technical composure you'd want from a midfielder at a club with genuine European ambitions. At 24, he's still developing, but Caen's current trajectory means he's unlikely to improve substantially in this environment.
