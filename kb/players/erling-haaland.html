Erling Haaland is a 25-year-old Norwegian international striker for Manchester City, known for his prolific goalscoring and physical prowess. Standing at 194 cm and preferring his left foot, he embodies the "Marksman" archetype and "Prima Punta" role.

Recent Form

Erling Haaland has maintained his exceptional scoring record during the 2025/2026 season. He has scored 22 Premier League goals in 30 appearances, alongside 7 assists. Across all competitions in the 2025-26 season, Haaland has registered 46 goals and 9 assists. In the Champions League alone, he has contributed 33 goals and 7 assists in 45 games. Despite some reports of a "slow start" to 2026, he still managed an impressive 41 goals in 41 games for club and country by mid-February. Manchester City has achieved 31 wins, 9 draws, and 9 losses in the 49 fixtures Haaland has participated in this season.

Tactical Profile

Haaland is recognized as a complete center-forward, combining speed, strength, and intelligent positioning with lethal finishing. He effectively uses his substantial frame for hold-up play, involving teammates, and possesses the pace to exploit space behind defenses. His finishing ability is considered his greatest strength, characterized by quick, powerful shots with minimal backlift, and a tendency to avoid overplaying in the penalty area.

Operating primarily as a lone striker, Haaland typically stays high and close to goal, often within the penalty area, which pushes opposing center-backs deeper. He plays a crucial role in Manchester City's build-up, acting as a "third man" to connect passes from the defense to the midfield, then laying off the ball to forward-facing midfielders. His strong physical presence allows him to dominate 1v1 duels, bully defenders, and win aerial challenges, making him a significant threat from crosses. Haaland also excels at playing off the blindside of defenders and utilizes "double movements" to create space for himself in the box. His overall impact extends beyond just scoring, encompassing disruptive off-the-ball movement and creating opportunities for others, making City's attack more dynamic and unpredictable.

Career Highlights

Erling Haaland has amassed an impressive collection of team and individual accolades throughout his career. With Manchester City, he has won the Premier League in 2022/23 and 2023/24, the UEFA Champions League in 2022/23, the FA Cup in 2023, the UEFA Super Cup in 2023, the Community Shield in 2025, the FIFA Club World Cup, and the English League Cup in 2026. Prior to joining City, he won the DFB-Pokal with Borussia Dortmund in 2021.

Individually, Haaland was awarded the European Golden Shoe in 2022/23 and the UEFA Men's Player of the Year Award in the same season. He also received the Golden Boy award in 2020, was named Bundesliga Player of the Season in 2021, and has been included in the FIFA FIFPro World11 for 2021, 2022, 2023, and 2024. He holds records for the most goals in a Premier League season (36) and is the quickest player to score eight hat-tricks. Haaland is Norway's all-time top goalscorer and the fastest player to reach 50 international goals, achieving this in just 46 games. He also secured his second Premier League Golden Boot in the 2023/24 campaign.

Personality

Erling Haaland's personality is characterized by a blend of professionalism, focus, and a direct communication style. He exhibits traits associated with the ISTP personality type, indicating a tactical and precise approach, combined with high physicality and athleticism, and a comfort with risk-taking. He is described as analytical and logical, preferring to focus on the present moment to achieve immediate results.

Off the pitch, Haaland is known for his assertive and confident nature in interviews, often answering questions literally and logically, sometimes with a perceived "isn't it obvious?" demeanor. Despite his fame, he emphasizes his commitment to hard work and remaining grounded, stating he will "never change who I am". His agent has described him as a "hilarious boy" but also "hyper-professional and serious about his work". Haaland is deeply passionate about football, watching various games, and expresses appreciation for Manchester City's support in keeping him in peak physical condition. He is also noted for his persistence and discipline, constantly analyzing his matches to improve his game and understand opponents.

Condition / Medical History

Erling Haaland has experienced several injury setbacks throughout his career, though Manchester City reportedly provides significant support to manage his fitness. In May 2025, he had an ankle injury that led to a six-match absence. More recently, in March 2026, he missed two matches due to an injury picked up in training, described by Pep Guardiola as "niggles" or discomfort, though the manager downplayed its severity. Despite being substituted early in a Champions League match against Real Madrid in March 2026, Guardiola confirmed he had "no injury" and would be available for the subsequent Carabao Cup final.

Historically, Haaland missed 26 games for Borussia Dortmund between November 2020 and January 2022 due to various issues, including hamstring and hip flexor injuries. In 2021, he dealt with a hip injury from October to November and a muscle injury from September to October. In February 2026, he sustained a knee injury, and in November 2025, he was reported to have "niggles". His physical strength is a key aspect of his game, and he maintains a rigorous training regimen focusing on his core, upper, and lower body to withstand the physical demands of playing against defenders.
