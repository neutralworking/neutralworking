## Snapshot

A wide midfielder at 25 who's got reasonable technical ability and understands how to manipulate space down the flank. He can beat a man and isn't afraid to drive forward. He'll have patches where he looks dangerous, then disappear for games. He's got the attributes to improve, but he's not quite a nailed-on starter level yet.
