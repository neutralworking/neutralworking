## Snapshot

A left-sided centre-back who reads the game with a composure you don't always find in Ligue 2, sitting deep and using his frame to dominate in direct duels. His distribution is functional rather than inventive—he'll find the easy pass out, but won't probe forward into the half-spaces or carry the ball through pressure himself. The issue isn't his defending; it's his lack of pace and mobility when forced to cover ground laterally, which exposes him against quick, direct attackers, particularly when his partner is caught out of position. At 27, he's a dependable Ligue 2 regular who'd manage as a fourth-choice option at a Ligue 1 club, but he's unlikely to progress further.
