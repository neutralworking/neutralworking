## Scouting Notes

A composed, left-footed centre-back who reads the game with intelligence and distributes from deep with genuine quality—progressive passing is a genuine weapon rather than an afterthought. Strong in the air, positionally astute, and rarely caught flat-footed in transition. The limitation is mobility against genuinely quick strikers operating in behind; he's a cerebral defender who needs a pressing trigger ahead of him rather than a sweeper covering chaos.
