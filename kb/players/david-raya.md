## Recent Form

David Raya is currently enjoying a period of strong performance for Arsenal during the 2025-2026 season, where he is regarded as one of the world's best goalkeepers due to his consistency, distribution, shot-stopping, and command of the box. He has made 32 league appearances, securing 15 clean sheets and conceding 24 goals, alongside 51 saves. His 15 clean sheets place him first among Premier League goalkeepers.

Raya's individual accolades reflect his impact, having won the Premier League Golden Glove in both the 2023-24 and 2024-25 seasons. He has also earned the Premier League Save of the Month award three times, in August 2024, March 2025, and December 2025. As of early February 2026, Arsenal sits atop the Premier League after 24 matches, with Raya expressing that he feels he is reaching his peak, feeling most comfortable and confident this season. In the 2025/26 UEFA Champions League, he maintains an average of 0.73 clean sheets and 2.55 saves per match.

## Tactical Profile

David Raya operates as a proactive goalkeeper, adept at sweeping behind the defensive line and maintaining composure under pressure. His distribution skills are a key asset for Arsenal, enabling the team to draw in the opposition's press before breaking through it effectively. He is known for being brave off his line and is well-suited for teams employing a high defensive block, as he is quick to anticipate and react to balls played in behind, often choosing to reset possession rather than simply clearing.

Raya's playing style has evolved significantly, with goalkeeping coach Iñaki Caña credited for transforming him from a reactive to a proactive stopper. He excels in shot-stopping, particularly with his reflexes and ability to save close-range efforts. Furthermore, Raya possesses a potent long-range kicking game, demonstrating excellent reading of player movement and precise execution to initiate counter-attacks. Despite his height of 1.83m (6 ft), he displays strong judgment and timing in aerial duels, frequently coming off his line to claim crosses and dominate his box.

## Career Highlights

David Raya has accumulated several significant achievements throughout his career. Individually, he earned the EFL Championship Golden Glove in the 2019–20 season and has twice secured the Premier League Golden Glove, in 2023–24 and 2024–25 (shared). His performances also led to his inclusion in the PFA Team of the Year for the 2023–24 Premier League season.

On the international stage, Raya has been a part of successful Spanish national teams, winning the UEFA Nations League in 2023 and the UEFA European Championship in 2024. He made his international debut for Spain in 2022 and was included in their squad for the 2022 FIFA World Cup. At club level, he was instrumental in Brentford's promotion to the Premier League in 2021 and previously helped Blackburn Rovers achieve promotion from League One in 2018.
