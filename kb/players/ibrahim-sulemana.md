## Snapshot

A Ghana international midfielder with impressive dribbling range and the physicality to compete in midfield battles; he can carry the ball progressively and has a decent cross on him. The issue is his decision-making in the final third—he often chooses the wrong moment to release the ball or overextends a dribble—and his consistency remains a question mark. Young talent with clear ceiling, but not yet the complete midfielder.
