## Snapshot

Behrens

  

  

#powerforward

  

First headed hattrick in the Bundesliga in 25 years. 

  

The beefy attacker played his way into the Union team in the second half of the season and shone as a congenial partner for Sheraldo Becker. Behrens has less speed, but physical presence and header power. He scored six of his eight goals in the second half of the season and became the winner of the season.
