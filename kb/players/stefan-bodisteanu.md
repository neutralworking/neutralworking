## Snapshot


ClubFC Viitorul

PositionAttacking midfielder

Born1 February 2003

Gheorghe Hagi knows a No 10 when he sees one. Born in Moldova, Bodisteanu moved to Hagi’s academy when he was just 11. He now has Romanian citizenship and plays for Romania’s under-18s. A skilful offensive midfielder, with great passing and dribbling abilities, Bodisteanu made his debut in the Romanian first tier last season for Viitorul, the team Hagi owns. Despite leaving his role as manager at the end of the previous campaign to concentrate on developing the academy, Hagi believes Bodisteanu is a jewel for the future of the club. This season Bodisteanu has regularly featured in Viitorul’s league squads. **[Emanuel Rosu](https://twitter.com/Emishor)**  
  
**October 2021 update** Bodisteanu is part of the first team at Farul, the team owned and managed by Hagi.  
  
**September 2022 update** The attacker is spending the season on loan at Steaua. He is a regular for the Romanian Army’s sports club, currently in the second tier.  
  
**October 2023 update** Has impressed for Otelul Galati in recent months, after spending last season on loan at Steaua. He's expected to be recalled by Hagi at league champions Farul sooner or later.
