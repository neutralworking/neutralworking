## Snapshot

A left-back who operates with real intent in the half-spaces, comfortable carrying the ball forward and linking into midfield rather than purely hugging the touchline. His positioning is sharp and he reads the game well enough to anticipate danger before it crystallises. The vulnerability? He can be caught napping against genuinely rapid wide men, and his defensive recovery pace is good rather than exceptional.
