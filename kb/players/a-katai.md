## Scouting Notes

A. Katai is a Marksman best utilized as a Poacher, possessing strong technical skills and moderate mental attributes but limited physically and tactically. However, his effectiveness diminishes in duels, he lacks passing accuracy, and struggles with guile in congested areas. A flamboyant, direct big-game player, his value lies in delivering decisive moments rather than consistent all-round play, suiting clubs that prioritize a pure goal threat.
