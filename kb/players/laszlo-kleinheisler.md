## Scouting Notes

A deep-lying wide forward who has built a journeyman career on defensive grit and directness rather than technical brilliance; he'll track back without complaint and use his frame to shield possession out wide, but his final ball is often hurried and his dribbling is workmanlike rather than dangerous. At 31, playing in the Croatian league after spells in Hungary and lesser European spots, he's a functional, hard-working option who does his defensive duties and occasionally creates an opening through sheer determination. The gap between his commitment and his execution in the attacking third is the story of his career—he's never quite had the touch or vision to justify regular minutes at a genuinely ambitious club.
