## Scouting Notes

A football intelligence extremist — genuinely elite positioning and an almost supernatural ability to find pockets of space in crowded boxes. Exceptional finisher with either foot and impeccable game-reading for a man in his mid-thirties. The trade-off is physicality; he cannot press effectively anymore, and against younger back fours with pace, he'll be isolated as a lone striker.
