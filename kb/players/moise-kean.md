## Snapshot

An Italian striker with genuine pace, intelligence in the box, and the ability to finish from tight angles; he's dynamic in his positioning and presses aggressively from the front. His movement is what separates him — he escapes defenders vertically and horizontally with excellent timing. The inconsistency is frustrating; his confidence fluctuates wildly and he sometimes loses focus in big matches.
