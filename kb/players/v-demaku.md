His strong shielding and balance, combined with good awareness, contribute significantly to breaking up play and maintaining possession. He embodies the classic 'engine room' midfielder, thriving in high-pressing systems where his tireless work rate and physical prowess are vital for winning the ball back and sustaining pressure. At 26, Demaku is in his prime, offering a robust and reliable option for clubs requiring a high-energy midfielder capable of dominating physical battles, though his technical ceiling and inability to block effectively mean he's best suited with creative players around him.
