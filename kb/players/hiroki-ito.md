## Snapshot

A left-sided centre-back who offers versatility and can shuffle across to right-back in a pinch. He's composed on the ball and doesn't panic under pressure, but he lacks the pace and aerial dominance to be a premier defensive presence at this level. He's functional depth—useful in a five-man back line or when Bayern want to control possession, but not someone you'd trust as a regular starter in a high-intensity fixture.
