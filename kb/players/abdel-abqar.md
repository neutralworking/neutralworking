## Scouting Notes

A composed, left-footed centre-back who reads the game well and isn't afraid to step into midfield to break up play, but lacks the athleticism to recover when caught out of position against quicker forwards. He's the kind of defender who wins through intelligence rather than pace, and while his positioning is sound, he can be exploited on the turn by dynamic attackers. His distribution is tidy but unremarkable — a reliable, dependable option in a structured backline rather than a creator from the back.
