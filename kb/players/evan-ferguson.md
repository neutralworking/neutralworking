## Snapshot

Irish striker with natural goalscoring instinct and decent movement in the box, capable of reading half-chances and finishing them with composure when given space. His hold-up play is minimal, his work-rate patchy, and he hasn't yet proven he can perform consistently at Serie A level after a difficult period at Brighton. Raw talent that needs game time and patience, but the foundation is there.
