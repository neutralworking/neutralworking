## Scouting Notes

A winger with electric feet and the ability to isolate defenders in one-v-one situations through pace and directness. His off-the-ball movement is generally intelligent, and he can arrive in dangerous positions. However, there's inconsistency in his end product — he'll beat defenders and then make poor decisions or deliver underwhelming crosses. His defensive application is also a recurring issue; he doesn't always track runners or press with conviction. He's a squad player with flashes rather than a nailed-on starter.
