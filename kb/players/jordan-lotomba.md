## Snapshot

A composed right-back who reads the game with the maturity you'd expect from a Swiss international and rarely commits egregious errors in transition or set-piece situations. He's left-footed, which gives him a subtle advantage in his passing range, and he's willing to invert and support the number eight when the system demands it. However, he lacks the explosive athleticism to recover against elite wingers, and his attacking contribution is functional rather than threatening—he's the definition of a dependable modern fullback with a ceiling.
