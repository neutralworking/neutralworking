## Scouting Notes

A centre-forward who works genuinely hard off the ball and offers real pressing disruption, with decent movement in the box if service is accurate. His technical ceiling is moderate—he's not a creator or a passer, and he lacks the explosiveness to break away from genuinely elite defenders. He's a functional No. 9 for a team built to function without a dominant striker, but he'll struggle if asked to carry a team creatively.
