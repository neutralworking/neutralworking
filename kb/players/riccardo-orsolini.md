## Snapshot

A right-winger with genuine directness and the ability to beat defenders in one-on-one situations; his crossing is accurate, and he can create from the byline or cut inside convincingly. He can be caught out defensively and occasionally lacks the decision-making to execute the right choice at pace. A very good attacking player with clear weaknesses that better sides exploit ruthlessly.
