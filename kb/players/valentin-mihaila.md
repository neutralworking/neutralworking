A direct, aggressive winger who lives in the half-spaces and uses surprising upper-body strength to create separation from defenders—think of him as a player who wins his duels through physical intelligence rather than pure pace. He's comfortable on either flank and can pick a pass or drive to the byline with genuine purpose, but his final product remains frustratingly inconsistent; too often the cross or cutback lacks precision when the moment demands it. At 26, playing for a mid-table Turkish side, he's a capable rotation option at a respectable European club, but the ceiling appears to be exactly where he is now—efficient, occasionally dangerous, never quite transcendent.
