## Scouting Notes

A centre-back who compensates for moderate athleticism with positioning discipline and a willingness to use his body effectively in duels, though he's prone to getting caught in transition when Augsburg is caught high. His passing is competent rather than progressive—he'll find the fullback, but won't spray it into midfield—and against technically superior attackers he can look a touch pedestrian. He's a reliable depth option for a side fighting relegation, but hasn't shown enough on the ball or in coverage to suggest he'll evolve into anything more than a squad defender.
