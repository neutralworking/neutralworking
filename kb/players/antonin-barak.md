A crafty Czech playmaker who threads passes from deep and operates in the No. 8 role with genuine technical security; he's not explosive but his positioning and reading of space are mature. His left foot is reliable and he carries the ball nicely in the half-spaces. The problem is that he's slowed considerably and no longer has the pressing intensity or recovery speed to justify starting in a high-octane midfield.
