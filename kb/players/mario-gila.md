## Snapshot

A centre-back with impressive physical tools and genuine composure on the ball for someone still in his mid-20s, showing the ability to execute forward passes and drive out of defence with authority. His reading of the game is developing well, though he can still be caught in advanced positions without proper cover. As he matures, he has the makings of a very good Serie A defender; right now, he's an excellent young talent operating in a big-club system.
