An English fullback with good athleticism and a willingness to get forward to support attacks, though his defensive positioning can be careless and he's vulnerable to runners in behind. His recovery pace saves him regularly; he's energetic and modern in approach but lacks the discipline or tactical awareness of truly elite fullbacks. Capable starter with clear weaknesses.
