## Scouting Notes

A genuinely excellent wide forward whose left foot is a legitimate weapon—he creates space through incisive half-space runs, can beat a man with pure technique, and delivers accurate service into dangerous areas with genuine consistency. His intelligence off the ball is underrated; he times his movements into pockets and understands when to check deep versus stretch play. The wear-and-tear of 31 seasons is beginning to show in his recovery speed, and he's vulnerable to pressing sides who can isolate him defensively, but he remains a cornerstone attacking asset for Braga and would start for any European side looking for technical width.
