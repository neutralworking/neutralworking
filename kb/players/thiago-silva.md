## Scouting Notes

Thiago Silva is still going strong as he approaches his 40s and it's remarkable to see just how lost Chelsea look at the back when the Brazilian is not around.  
  
Fitness and injury issues have plagued the Brazilian in recent years but he hardly puts a foot wrong when available, even if the Blues suffer the odd calamity at the back.  
  
Silva's masterful positioning and leadership in defence has inspired his former AC Milan and PSG sides to league glory, while he finally won the Champions League with Chelsea in 2020.


CD	Determined	Defender	Titan	AC Milan	BRA	A	86.4	Silva’s technical ability mixed with strength and aerial competence has given Laurent Blanc’s side defensive security and helped them rise through the ranks as one of the finest teams in Europe.		Arguably the finest central defender in the world, Thiago Silva signed for Paris Saint-Germain in 2012 and has gone on to play 150 games for the club. His move represented the almighty financial power that PSG gathered since their takeover in 2011 with the Brazilian signing at the same time as star-striker Zlatan Ibrahimovic (who might also be in this team).
