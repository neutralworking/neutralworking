## Scouting Notes

A centre-forward whose career has been a study in the diminishing returns of a player who built his game on athleticism and directness rather than craft. He can still finish with authority when the service is service is clean, and his pressing intensity remains genuine, but he's immobile against modern, quick-footed defenders, struggles to operate in tighter spaces, and offers very little in build-up play—essentially a pure nine in an era when that's become almost anachronistic.
