## Snapshot

An industrious fullback with genuine recovery pace and decent crossing quality when given space on the wing, though he plays the game with a touch of recklessness that occasionally leaves gaps in transition. His pressing is spirited rather than coordinated, and he can be caught daydreaming in possession, making him vulnerable to press-resistant centre-backs who can turn him sideways. For a mid-table Bundesliga club he's a serviceable option, but he'd need to tighten his reading of the game significantly to function at a higher level.
