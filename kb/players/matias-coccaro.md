## Scouting Notes

A Uruguayan forward with useful movement in the box and decent technical awareness for a No. 9, capable of linking play or finishing chances that arrive cleanly. His issue is that he doesn't impose himself physically or athletically enough to create space against stubborn defenders, and his press is more committed than effective. Useful depth but not a long-term solution.
