Snapshot

A Portuguese centre-back operating in La Liga who combines decent physicality with reasonable positional awareness, allowing him to be a regular starter for Getafe, though he's not someone who will perform at a significantly higher level. He's adequate in one-on-one situations and wins a fair share of aerial contests, but his passing range is limited and he can be clumsy on the ball in transition. Duarte is a workhorse defender — effective within the structure of a defensive-minded team but bereft of the technical or athletic qualities that would elevate him beyond that remit.

Tactical Profile

As a right-footed center-back standing at 191 cm, Domingos Duarte embodies a "Fortress" and "Stopper" archetype, focusing on robust defending. His statistics for the 2025/2026 season highlight his physical approach, with 7 clean sheets in 25 appearances and a high volume of defensive engagements. He has engaged in 106 duels, winning 76 of them, and participated in 182 aerial duels, winning 62.

Duarte's role involves disrupting opposition attacks, evidenced by 12 successful tackles, 79 ball recoveries, and one blocked shot. While primarily a defensive anchor, his passing statistics show a 75.64% completion rate from approximately 27.10 passes per game, indicating his ability to distribute the ball when needed, though he is not primarily a ball-playing defender. His tendency to commit 1.05 fouls per 90 minutes and accumulate 9 yellow cards this season further underscores his combative and assertive defensive style.

Career Highlights

Domingos Duarte began his football journey in the youth academy of Sporting CP. He gained valuable senior experience through loan spells in Portugal's Primeira Liga with Belenenses and Chaves, and in Spain's Segunda División with Deportivo La Coruña. His move to Granada CF in July 2019 marked a significant step, where he went on to make over 175 appearances in La Liga across his time with both Granada and current club Getafe.

A notable achievement in his career was reaching the UEFA Europa League quarter-finals with Granada during the 2020-21 season. During his tenure at Granada, he also found the net against prominent clubs like Real Madrid and Barcelona. Duarte made his full international debut for Portugal in 2020 and has since earned three caps for his national team. He joined Getafe in July 2022, continuing his career in the Spanish top flight.

Condition / Medical History

Domingos Duarte has experienced a few minor injury setbacks in the recent past, including brief periods of absence in February 2026 and May 2025 due to unspecified injuries. More significantly, he sustained a shoulder injury that kept him out of action for an extended period from late March to mid-July 2024.

His medical history also includes previous shoulder injuries in October 2020 and from September to December 2021. Other past injuries have encompassed knee issues in April 2021 and October 2019, as well as muscle, hip, and groin problems in early 2020.
