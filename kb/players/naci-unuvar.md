## Scouting Notes


ClubAjax

PositionMidfielder

Born13 June 2003

In August 2019, a couple of months after he turned 16, Ünüvar was named in an Ajax under-19 squad that travelled to Barcelona to play the first game at the brand new Estadi Johan Cruyff at Barça’s youth complex. In front of Lionel Messi, Frenkie de Jong, Sergio Busquets and Jordi Cruyff, Ünüvar, the youngest player on the pitch, scored the only two goals of the game. Back at Ajax Hakim Ziyech personally took him under his wing before leaving for Chelsea. The versatile midfielder with Turkish roots made his first-team debut for Ajax in January, coming on in the 75th minute of a cup game and winning and converting a penalty. **[Bart Vlietstra](https://twitter.com/BVlietstra?lang=en)**  
  
**October 2021 update** Still looking to follow up on his debut, Ünüvar says he had a “not good and not bad season” at the highest level of Ajax’s youth teams, playing in the first division. Admitted he has to grow physically but the gym is “not the most fun place to be. I still don’t go there with joy but now I do it with motivation,” he told Ajax Life.  
  
**September 2022 update** Regarded as one of the most talented young players at Ajax but the arrival of players such as Davy Klaassen, Steven Berghuis and Mohammed Kudus did not help his chances of playing. Extended his contract until 2026 and joined Trabzonspor on loan.  
  
**October 2023 update** Injuries meant that Ünüvar did not get as much game time as he or Ajax would have hoped while on loan at Trabzonspor. Now with Twente on another temporary deal.
