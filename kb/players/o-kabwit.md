## Scouting Notes

O. Kabwit, a Marksman Complete Forward, possesses excellent finishing from both close and long range, combining this with surprising vision and creativity to act as a direct offensive threat. Despite these striking qualities, his limited tactical understanding means he struggles with overall pass accuracy and lacks the guile to consistently influence play outside the immediate goal zone, often losing duels due to a lighter physical frame. This player is a modern striker who offers both goal threat and some creative capacity, suited to systems that demand a versatile forward capable of moments of individual brilliance, even if his broader link-up play is underdeveloped. Clubs looking for a forward with genuine goal-scoring instincts and flashes of creativity would find Kabwit an interesting, albeit tactically unrefined, prospect; his struggles in duels and inconsistent passing suggest he's a higher-risk investment who needs specific tactical coaching and a robust midfield to truly flourish.
