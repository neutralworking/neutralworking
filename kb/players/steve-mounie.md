## Snapshot

A powerful, physical centre-forward who uses his frame to shield possession and hold play up in the final third, allowing teammates to advance around him—he's genuinely effective at this holding role and understands how to receive on the half-turn. His limitations are significant: he's not a goal scorer in any real sense (his finishing is unreliable), he lacks the movement to create space in the box, and his technical ability is limited, meaning he can't operate in more fluid, possession-based systems. At 31, he's a battering ram at this level—useful if you want a target man, but nothing approaching elite.
