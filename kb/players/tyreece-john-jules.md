## Snapshot

A rare profile—a marksman with credible long-range threat and guile alongside close-range finishing, yet tactically limited and burdened by discipline and pass-accuracy issues that undermine his utility as a complete forward. His introversion paired with competitive intensity suggests a player who operates best when given clear instructions and minimal creative responsibility, making him an ideal poacher or second-striker for a direct system rather than a focal point of a possession-dominant attack. The technical strengths in finishing are genuine, but the pass accuracy weakness means he cannot reliably participate in fluid combination play or serve as a link-up forward. Clubs pursuing a pure goalscorer—one who finishes chances and occasionally contributes long-range threat—will find value; those seeking a complete forward should look elsewhere.
