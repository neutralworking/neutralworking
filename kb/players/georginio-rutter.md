An attacking midfielder with excellent technical maturity for his age, capable of both creating chances through clever passing and driving forward with the ball at his feet in half-space areas. He uses his body effectively to shield possession and has developed a real maturity in his decision-making. The limitation is that he's not yet a difference-maker against elite defensive structures—he needs space and time to operate, and elite teams suffocate that.
