## Snapshot

A competent shot-stopper with genuine distribution ambitions—he actively seeks out the ball at feet and isn't afraid to play out from the back in a modern sense. His reflexes remain sharp enough for Serie A. The rub is that his sweeping game is sometimes overambitious for the situation, and he lacks the elite composure under genuine pressure that separates the very good from the excellent.
