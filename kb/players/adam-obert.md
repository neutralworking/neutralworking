## Scouting Notes

A centre-back who reads space well and positions himself intelligently in the deeper phases, rarely getting drawn into needless fouls. His distribution is competent without being a threat, and he manages the aerial duel with reasonable consistency. The limitation is pure athleticism—he's not a ball-carrier and lacks the recovery pace to cover mistakes, which becomes problematic against properly clinical forwards.
