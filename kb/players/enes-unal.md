## Scouting Notes

Turkish striker with genuine movement and the ability to create space for himself in the box with smart timing; he's a proper finisher when the ball arrives and isn't afraid of the physical battle. The limitations are marked — he's not a presser, he doesn't contribute much outside the box, and his all-around play lacks the intelligence of an elite forward. Useful poacher but a one-dimensional threat.
