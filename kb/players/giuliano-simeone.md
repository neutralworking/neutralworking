## Scouting Notes

A young, raw centre-forward still developing his craft at the periphery of Atlético's first team, with the athleticism and work rate you'd expect from his lineage but a very incomplete finisher. His game is largely built on effort rather than intuition; he presses aggressively and moves energetically, but positioning, timing, and composure in front of goal are all works in progress. At 23, there's time, but he's not ready to be relied upon at this level yet.
