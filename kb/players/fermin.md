Fermín's exceptional technical ability and movement make him a nightmare to defend against, as he can drop deep and create space for himself and others. However, his lack of tactical discipline and guile can lead to him taking on too much and losing possession. His strong physical presence and balance allow him to shield the ball effectively, but he can be prone to making reckless decisions. A team that emphasizes fluid, attacking football and has a strong defensive structure to compensate for Fermín's limitations could get the most out of him.
