## Scouting Notes

Attack-minded midfielder with genuine technical ability and an eye for a pass in the final third; he dribbles past pressure and can operate across the front line with invention. His inconsistency is chronic, and there are long spells where he disappears from matches entirely; his defensive work rate is also patchy. Capable of a moment of brilliance or invisibility depending on the night—you never quite know which version you're getting.
