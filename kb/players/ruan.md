## Scouting Notes

A left-back with good natural athleticism and the instinct to press high and engage defenders, offering genuine attacking intent down the flank—he wants to drive forward and create width in the final third. However, his defensive positioning is loose when isolated, he can be caught ball-watching on the back post, and he lacks the technical composure to be trusted in a team that needs to retain possession under pressure. He's a functional defensive fullback with attacking ambitions at a mid-table Süper Lig side, capable but inconsistent.
