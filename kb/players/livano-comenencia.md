## Scouting Notes


ClubPSV Eindhoven

PositionDefender/midfielder

Born3 February 2004

Captain of the Dutch under-18 team, he scored the opening goal against Italy in a friendly which the Netherlands won 5-0. Comenencia was the outstanding player on the pitch, a real leader. Had the ambition to become a professional footballer at a very young age, turning to his dad when he was four while watching football on TV saying: “Dad, I want to be on TV too!” Often compared to a certain Virgil van Dijk, who was also born in Breda, but Comenencia hopes to become a midfielder like Georginio Wijnaldum. “I want to be a kind boy who is good to other people off the pitch but on it I have to be a monster.” **[Bart Vlietstra](https://twitter.com/BVlietstra?lang=en)**  
  
**September 2022 update** In February he signed a new PSV contract until 2025, which brought tears to his parents’ eyes, but he has not been promoted to the first team yet. That may only be a question of time, however, with the head coach, Ruud van Nistelrooy, speaking highly of him.  
  
**October 2023 update** Was on the first-team bench twice during the 2022-23 season and looked set to be given more opportunities this season when Juventus swooped and paid €3m for him in August. Life in Italy could hardly have started better with Comenencia scoring 44 seconds into his first start in the Next Gen team, who play in Serie C.
