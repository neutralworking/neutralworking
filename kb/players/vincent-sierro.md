## Scouting Notes

A Swiss metronome in midfield who does the things that don't show up in highlight reels: he breaks lines with intelligent short passes, shields possession with a remarkably low centre of gravity, and rarely loses the ball in dangerous areas. He's not a creative fulcrum—doesn't progress play through the half-spaces or carry it into the final third with any real aggression—but as a defensive screen and a recycler, he's dependable in a way that suggests he's done this job for a long time and knows exactly where to position himself. The limitation is ceiling: at 30 and still in Ligue 2, he's probably a very solid squad rotation option at a mid-table Ligue 1 side, not the sort who'll make you forget what you're missing in the engine room.
