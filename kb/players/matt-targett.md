## Snapshot

Competent left-back with solid attacking instincts and reliable crossing ability when given time on the ball. His defensive positioning is generally sound, though he is not explosively quick and will struggle against pacey inside-forwards making inverted runs. Dependable option for a Championship club, comfortable in possession and useful going forward, but lacks elite athleticism or defensive reading—he is a safe, steady option rather than a transcendent left-back.
