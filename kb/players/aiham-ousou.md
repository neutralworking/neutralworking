## Snapshot

A centre-back operating in La Liga's basement tier, Ousou brings decent positional sense and a willingness to engage physically, but lacks the pace and reading of the game to be relied upon at higher levels. He's functional at this level—solid in one-on-one situations, progressive with the ball at his feet—but would struggle against quick, intelligent forwards. For a 26-year-old, he's reached his ceiling as a mid-table defensive stalwart.
