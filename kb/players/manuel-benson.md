## Snapshot

A Belgian winger with decent end-product and the ability to play either flank, Benson is capable of both crossing and cutting inside with intent. His one-v-one duels are reasonable, but he can lack that explosive step to truly separate from defenders in the final third. He's a solid rotation piece — the kind of player who'll grab a goal or assist every few games — but not a guaranteed starter in a competitive setup.
