## Scouting Notes

A right-back with genuine athleticism and the capacity to progress the ball through pressing triggers and inverted carries into the half-space. He's tidy defensively and rarely caught completely flat-footed. The limitation is his top speed—elite wingers will occasionally find a yard of space—and he can be careless with possession in dangerous areas. Solid, modern fullback without being elite.
