A high-pressing terrier with strong technical foundation and impressive interception work, yet tactical limitations and a blocking deficiency expose him as a specialized piece rather than a complete midfielder. His pass-range and marking provide build-up contributions, but limited tactical acuity means he functions best in narrow, rigid systems where his stamina and jump compensate for decision-making gaps. Suits a Ligue 1 squad seeking energy and verticality off the ball without requiring the field-reading to unlock complex passing sequences.
