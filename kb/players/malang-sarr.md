## Scouting Notes

Left-sided centre-back who can play in a back five and offers genuine technical competence on the ball—he'll carry forward and isn't afraid of possession in dangerous areas. Good physical profile and reading of zonal space, but he can be caught out by quick attackers in transition and needs a more defensive-minded partner. His inverted, progressive tendencies don't always align with protecting the backline.
