## Snapshot

A 22-year-old Spanish midfielder with impressive technical security and an instinct for positioning himself in the half-spaces to receive on the half-turn. He's graceful on the ball and capable of short-range playmaking, but there's a lack of bite to his game—he doesn't progress the ball with the urgency or verticality that elite clubs demand. His defensive contribution is neat but not assertive, which limits him to a squad role at genuine contenders.
