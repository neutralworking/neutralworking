## Snapshot

Rafa is a pure Sprinter, a Direct Winger built for the Extremo role, with jaw-dropping acceleration and pace that makes him a nightmare for defenders in open space. His superb shielding, balance, and take-ons mean he's nearly uncatchable once he gets going. Yet, his virtually non-existent intensity and alarmingly low capacity for progressive carries are significant issues, suggesting he struggles to consistently drive the team forward despite his raw speed.
