## Scouting Notes

A young keeper with the technical foundation to play out from the back—comfortable on both feet, decent range of distribution—but still operating in the second tier where the intensity of chance-creation and finishing is a rung below the elite. His shot-stopping has improved noticeably this season, though positioning against crosses remains a work in progress; he'll occasionally come for balls he shouldn't, a confidence thing at his age. At 21, he's got the physical tools and mentality to develop into something, but right now he's a prospect with genuine upside rather than a finished article—think a year or two away from being genuinely interesting to a top-five league club.
