## Scouting Notes

Nsame is the quintessential Prima Punta, a formidable target man who dominates in the air thanks to his elite jumping, physicality, and heading ability. He displays excellent composure in front of goal and from the penalty spot, but his complete lack of defensive contribution, especially in tackling and marking, means he's strictly a one-way player. You're not getting a press from him.
