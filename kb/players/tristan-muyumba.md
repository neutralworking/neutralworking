## Snapshot

Muyumba is a true Powerhouse Relayeur, unparalleled in his ability to shield the ball, coupled with superb stamina and surprising burst of pace for a central midfielder. He's excellent at ball retention and breaking lines with progressive carries, but his significant lack of discipline often leads to unnecessary fouls or poor positioning. Moreover, his low creativity and blocking limit his impact as a playmaker and as a defensive screen.
