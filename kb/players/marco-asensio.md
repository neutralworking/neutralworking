## Snapshot

A right-footed attacking midfielder with cultured technique and an underrated ability to operate in the half-spaces, shifting play with accuracy and finding pockets with subtle movement. His finishing remains inconsistent and he lacks the dynamism to beat defenders on the dribble consistently; he's at his best in possession-dominant systems where he can manipulate space rather than create it. Still a quality option, but the 2019-20 version feels like a distant memory.
