## Scouting Notes

Right-sided attacking midfielder with a genuinely tidy left foot and the ability to operate in tight spaces; he's not a winger in the traditional sense, preferring to cut inside and create from the half-space rather than whip crosses. The limitation is pace — he's not beating defenders with explosive acceleration — so his game is predicated on technical excellence and positioning. A valuable player for a side that wants control and precision.
