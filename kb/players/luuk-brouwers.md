## Snapshot

A methodista who organizes play from deep with genuine range and press-resistant first touch, Brouwers compensates for moderate tactical sophistication through strong stamina and intuitive movement off the ball. His 27-year tenure at Heerenveen suggests rootedness rather than ambition, which tracks with his structured temperament but raises questions about whether he has peaked in a middling league. The blocking weakness is a genuine liability for a holding midfielder—he'll get turned too easily in transition—and his guile deficit means he cannot unlock congested defenses laterally. Best suited to a high-pressing, direct system where his engine does the heavy lifting and his passing range initiates attacks rather than orchestrates them.
