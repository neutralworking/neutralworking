## Snapshot

A veteran presence in the Spanish second tier, Campaña remains a tidy operator in central midfield with reliable short-range distribution and decent positional sense. He's the type of 32-year-old who knows his limits and manages the tempo effectively — you'll see him in the defensive pocket, breaking up play with a well-timed tackle or interception rather than trying to dictate from deep. The problem is he lacks the press resistance and dynamic carry ability you'd want at this stage; he's functional rather than influential, and at Alcorcón he's filling a squad role rather than being the engine that drives the team. Fine for Segunda División, but he's drifted into the range where he's a journeyman settling into a lower league rather than someone with upward trajectory.
