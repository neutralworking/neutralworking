Left-back with decent athleticism and a reasonable left foot, capable of carrying the ball into the half-space and offering a third-man option in build-up play. His positioning is inconsistent—he can be caught ball-watching on the back post, and his recovery pace isn't quite quick enough to cover for aggressive forward movement. Functional at La Liga's mid-table level, but not someone you'd trust against elite wide forwards.
