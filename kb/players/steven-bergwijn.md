## Scouting Notes

A winger whose ability to find space in the half-space and execute a sharp cut-back remains genuinely valuable, though he's never been a player who beats defenders with sustained pace. His left foot is cultured, and he understands positioning well enough to drift into pockets where he can be dangerous. The limitation is real: against modern fullbacks with genuine athleticism, he can be isolated, and his tendency to look for the backward pass means he doesn't consistently drive teams forward.
