Player Research Brief: Jeffinho

Recent Form

Jeffinho is currently on loan at Chinese Super League club Liaoning Tieren from Botafogo, where he has been active in the 2026 season. In the current 2026 Super League campaign, he has registered one goal and has not yet provided an assist across 418 minutes of play in five matches, maintaining an average FotMob rating of 7.5 with two yellow cards. His recent performances in March and April 2026 include a goal in a 2-1 home win against Beijing Guoan, earning an 8.1 FotMob rating, and an 88-minute appearance with a 7.1 rating in a 1-1 draw against Qingdao West Coast.

In the 2025 season, while with Botafogo, Jeffinho made 26 appearances, scoring two goals and contributing four assists. Prior to that, during his loan spell at Botafogo from Lyon in 2024, he featured in 19 matches, finding the net five times and assisting twice.

Tactical Profile

Jeffinho, primarily a left winger, is noted for his strong dribbling ability and capacity to deliver key passes, aligning with his "Magician" archetype. His playing style involves a preference for short passes and frequent dribbling, often playing the ball off the ground. He also demonstrates a strong defensive contribution, indicating a willingness to track back and engage opponents.

While adept at beating defenders and making runs, his weaknesses are identified as holding onto the ball excessively and crossing. As a right-footed player operating predominantly on the left flank, he is well-suited to cutting inside, creating opportunities for shots or intricate link-up play.

Career Highlights

Jeffinho began his professional career at Resende, making his first-team debut in June 2020 and scoring his first goal in a Campeonato Carioca match. After a loan spell at Gama in 2021, he joined Botafogo on loan in April 2022, with the move becoming permanent in August of the same year. His performances caught the attention of European clubs, leading to a €10 million transfer to Ligue 1 side Lyon in January 2023.

During his time at Lyon, Jeffinho scored his first goal in the Coupe de France in February 2023 and his first Ligue 1 goal in June 2023. He returned to Botafogo on loan in January 2024, a move that was made permanent for €5.3 million in December 2024. With Botafogo, he achieved significant success, winning both the Copa Libertadores and the Série A title in 2024. As of 2026, he is on loan to Liaoning Tieren.
