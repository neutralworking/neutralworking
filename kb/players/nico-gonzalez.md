## Snapshot

A technically secure, left-footed central midfielder who controls the ball with elegance and moves it with precision, thriving in Atlético's possession-based phases. His positioning is intelligent, and he's excellent at finding space in the half-spaces to receive and turn, but he's not a presser with particular intensity, and his defensive output is functional rather than commanding. He's a conductor, not a ball-winner.
