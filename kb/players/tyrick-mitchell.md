## Snapshot

A diligent left-back who understands his defensive responsibilities and rarely gets caught in a vacuum, but his attacking contribution remains modest and his ball progression is functional rather than creative. Mitchell has the athleticism to cover ground and the positioning sense to stay tight, yet he lacks the technical range to consistently unlock play in the half-spaces or drive forward with authority. In a defensive-minded Palace setup, he's a reliable executor, but don't expect him to be the type of fullback who initiates attacks from deep.
