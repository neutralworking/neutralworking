## Snapshot

At 35, he's become a positional striker rather than an athlete, relying on intelligent movement and an understanding of centre-back positioning that most forwards take years to develop. His link play is genuinely useful, and he still finds pockets in the box through clever timing. But he's no longer a threat in behind, his pressing lacks the intensity it once possessed, and he's become a luxury rather than a necessity.
