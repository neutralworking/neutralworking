## Scouting Notes

A competent shot-stopper with solid reflexes and reasonable positioning, though his distribution is fairly elementary—lots of long clearances, limited ball progression. He's adequate in the air and commands his box reasonably well, but there's no real sophistication to his game; he's a traditional shot-stopper rather than a modern sweeper-keeper. Reliable depth option who's solid in a back-five system but wouldn't inspire confidence at a club with aspirations to play high.
