He's adequate in the air and commands his box reasonably well, but there's no real sophistication to his game; he's a traditional shot-stopper rather than a modern sweeper-keeper. Reliable depth option who's solid in a back-five system but wouldn't inspire confidence at a club with aspirations to play high.
