## Snapshot

Physical, direct forward who thrives on chaos and physicality; excellent in transition when given space to run, and genuinely difficult to dispossess in a crowd. His technical range is narrow: he can't construct play from a withdrawn position, and his decision-making in the box is sometimes hasty. Ideally suited to counterattacking systems where he's fed and released rather than asked to orchestrate.
