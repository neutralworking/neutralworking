## Snapshot

A heartbeat midfielder at 30 with strong close-range technique and genuine blocking discipline, Rodrigão excels in the trenches—his dueling prowess and press-resistance anchor defensive stability. Weak mid-range vision and absence of take-ons confine him to a reactive, compact role; he cannot drive tempo or exploit space in transition, making him valuable only in low-possession systems. Serbian football has likely maximized his utility; European interest should be cautious unless a club specifically needs a defensive anchor for a back-five structure with minimal transition demands.
