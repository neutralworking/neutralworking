## Scouting Notes

A centre-back who compensates for diminishing athleticism with positional astuteness and an almost forensic understanding of when to drop into a defensive line or step up into midfield; he's the kind of defender who wins you matches through reading, not recovery pace. The problem, bluntly, is that he can't afford a mistake anymore—his legs won't cover the ground if he's caught out—and he's vulnerable to any attacker with genuine pace and a willingness to run in behind. Still the fulcrum of Bilbao's defence, but you're managing decline rather than peak performance now.
