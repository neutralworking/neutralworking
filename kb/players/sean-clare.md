## Snapshot

A left-back in the lower reaches of the Championship, Clare is a dependable, physical presence who understands his job—stay compact, win the first ball, keep shape. He's got decent pace and a willing engine, but he's not a progressive force; his passing is functional rather than creative, and he won't hurt you going forward with any frequency. At 29, he's a squad rotation option, the kind of defender who'll have a solid 7/10 outing on a Tuesday night but won't move the needle in either direction. His weakness is exactly what you'd expect: limited technical security means he can look uncomfortable when pressed high, and his crossing is rarely a weapon.
