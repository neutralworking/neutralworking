Italian centre-back with excellent positional discipline and an underrated ability to progress the ball through passing rather than carrying; his reading of space is exemplary and his leadership is visible. His acceleration is moderate by modern standards and he can be exposed by genuinely explosive attackers, but his consistency and tactical intelligence make him essential to Roma's structure. A proper Serie A mainstay and occasionally excellent.
