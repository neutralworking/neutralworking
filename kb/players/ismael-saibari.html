Ismael Saibari is a 25-year-old Moroccan central midfielder for PSV Eindhoven, known for his right-footed play and his "Magician" archetype, fulfilling a playmaker role. Standing at 185 cm, he has established himself as a key figure for both club and country.

Recent Form

Ismael Saibari is currently enjoying a highly productive 2025/2026 season with PSV Eindhoven. Across all competitions, he has registered 22 goals and 9 assists in 47 appearances, playing over 3300 minutes. In the Eredivisie alone, he has scored 15 goals and provided 8 assists in 26 matches, earning an average FotMob rating of 7.94. His strong performances include a notable match on April 4, 2026, where he scored two goals and assisted one in a 4-3 victory against FC Utrecht, achieving a 9.4 FotMob rating.

Saibari's consistent output extends to other competitions, with 3 goals in 7 UEFA Champions League appearances and 1 goal and 1 assist in 2 KNVB Beker matches during the 2025/2026 season. This follows an excellent 2024/2025 season where he became a regular starter, contributing 15 goals and 13 assists across all competitions and helping PSV secure the Eredivisie title. He also achieved his first career hat-trick on October 26, 2025, in a 3-2 away win over Feyenoord.

Tactical Profile

Saibari's tactical versatility is a significant asset, allowing him to operate primarily as an attacking midfielder but also effectively on the wings, as a central midfielder, and even as a central striker. His playing style is characterized by exceptional dribbling ability, vision, and composure, enabling him to navigate tight defenses and pick out teammates in dangerous positions. He possesses a strong finishing ability, including long-range shots, and demonstrates excellent concentration during play.

Beyond his offensive prowess, Saibari contributes significantly to the defensive phase. He is described as a focused and aggressive player with a high work rate, showcasing good positioning, effective reading of the game, and a strong reaction to losing possession by closing down spaces. His physical attributes combine power and nimble movement, making him difficult to dispossess, and he exhibits tactical intelligence, adapting well to various roles and systems. He excels at breaking lines and driving play forward with intensity and speed, often engaging in layoffs and short passes while also demonstrating a good range for long passes and crosses.

Career Highlights

Ismael Saibari's career has been marked by significant achievements at both club and international levels. He joined PSV Eindhoven in 2020, initially playing for Jong PSV before earning a promotion to the first team where he has become a key player. He scored his first goal for PSV in August 2023 against Vitesse and notably netted twice in a Champions League play-off against Rangers that same month, securing a spot in the group stage and earning Man of the Match honors. He further scored his first Champions League group stage goal in November 2023 against Sevilla.

Saibari has been instrumental in PSV's domestic success, contributing to three consecutive Eredivisie titles in the 2023-24, 2024-25, and 2025-26 seasons. He has also lifted the KNVB Beker in 2022-23 and the Dutch Super Cup three times in 2023, 2024, and 2026. On the international stage, Saibari was part of the Morocco U23 team that won the 2023 U-23 Africa Cup of Nations, which also qualified them for the 2024 Summer Olympics. He has earned 27 senior caps for the Morocco national team, scoring 7 goals, and was a winner of the Africa Cup in 2025. His contract with PSV was extended until 2028, reflecting his growing importance to the club.

Personality

Ismael Saibari's journey reflects a resilient and adaptable personality, driven by an unwavering passion for football. He has demonstrated a strong mental fortitude, consistently bouncing back from setbacks throughout his career. Notably, during his youth academy days at Anderlecht, he was initially misjudged and deemed "too fat" to progress, a challenge he overcame to reach his current professional standing.

Off the pitch, Saibari is recognized for his evolving fashion sense. He was nominated for the 2023 Fashion Player Awards and describes his style as "street, yet classic," embodying a blend of relaxed confidence and timeless detail. His multicultural background, having been born in Spain, raised in Belgium, and representing Morocco, has provided him with a unique cultural perspective that influences not only his intelligent style of play but also his lifestyle and public image.

Condition / Medical History

Ismael Saibari has experienced several injuries throughout his career, though he has consistently demonstrated resilience in returning to play. In 2022, he sustained a hamstring injury that ruled him out until after the winter break. The 2024 season saw him deal with muscle injuries. In 2025, he suffered an ankle injury. More recently, in early 2026, he had minor periods of "Rest" and "Injury". Additionally, he missed Round 7 of a competition in 2026 due to disciplinary points. His ability to overcome these physical challenges, alongside childhood health issues, is a testament to his strong character.
