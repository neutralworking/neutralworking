## Snapshot

A young ball-carrier with genuine press-resistance and the kind of physical profile that suggests a modern number six—strong in transitions, willing to make progressive carries forward rather than the sterile sideways pass. At 21 with limited senior football at this level, though, he's still learning positional responsibility and the timing of defensive actions; he can be loose in possession under pressure and occasionally overwilling to dribble when a simple pass suffices. The tools are there, but this remains a prospect at the threshold of senior consistency rather than a finished product.
