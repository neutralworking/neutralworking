## Snapshot

Engels is a press-resistant midfielder with genuinely lovely left-foot distribution and the spatial awareness to play progressive passes through midfield that others miss—he's comfortable receiving under pressure and turning quickly. The concern is physicality; at 22 he's still lean, and against high-octane Number 10s or aggressive ball-winners, he can get knocked off balance or isolated. He's a very good young talent in the Scottish Premiership, not yet a continental midfielder, but entirely on schedule.
