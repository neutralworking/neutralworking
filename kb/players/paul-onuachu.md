## Snapshot

A bruising, hold-up number nine who wins the physical battle and offers an outlet for long balls into advanced areas. His aerial dominance is real, and he understands the mechanics of occupying defenders, but he's never been a clinical finisher and his touch in tight spaces remains a liability. At 31, playing in Turkey, he's a useful battering ram for a mid-table side or a cup-run centerpiece, but he lacks the refinement or scoring instinct for sustained pressure on genuine contenders.
