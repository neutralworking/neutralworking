## Scouting Notes

A reliable, aerially dominant centre-back who has done solid work in the Cypriot top flight and Europa League qualification rounds, but his reading of the game in transition remains pedestrian and his distribution is functional rather than progressive. At 33, he's a no-nonsense, old-school defender—good in the air, decent at blocking—but lacks the mobility to play a high defensive line or the technical security to build from the back in a possession-heavy system. Omonia's defensive anchor, but not a player who'll elevate a team's setup or cover ground at pace.
