## Snapshot

Greek left-back with genuine pace and an aggressive approach to both defending and attacking, capable of getting into threatening positions on the flank and delivering crosses. His positional discipline can be suspect, his passing range is functional rather than special, and he can be caught flat-footed by direct runners. Decent option in a high-energy pressing system, but streaky enough that he's typically a backup.
