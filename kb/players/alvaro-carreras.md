## Tactical Profile
Carreras is recognized as a modern, attacking wing-back with significant offensive involvement, having been converted from a forward in his youth career. His tactical versatility allows him to operate as a left-back, wing-back, or even an inverted wing-back, often tucking into midfield to aid ball progression. He excels in progressive carries, progressive passes, and passes into the final third, ranking highly among European fullbacks in these metrics. Carreras demonstrates strong close ball control, vision, acceleration, and dribbling ability, enabling him to drive the ball forward and create opportunities. While primarily attack-minded, he possesses strong one-on-one defensive capabilities, good game reading, and effective tackling, and can even adapt to a left center-back role in a back five.

## Career Highlights
Álvaro Carreras began his youth career at Racing Ferrol and Deportivo La Coruña before joining Real Madrid's U13 squad in 2017, and later Manchester United in 2020. During his time at Manchester United, he was named Denzil Haroun Reserve Player of the Year in 2021–22. He gained significant senior experience through loan spells, including 39 appearances for Preston North End, where he earned the Young Player of the Year award in 2022–23, and stints at Granada and Benfica. Benfica made his loan permanent in 2024 for €6 million, and he went on to win the Primeira Liga Defender of the Month in March 2025 and was named in the Primera Liga Team of the Year for 2024–25, also securing the Portuguese League Cup with the club. On July 14, 2025, Carreras returned to Real Madrid for a reported €50 million, signing a six-year deal, making his debut on August 19, 2025, and scoring his first goal on November 1, 2025. He has also represented Spain at the U19 and U21 youth international levels.
