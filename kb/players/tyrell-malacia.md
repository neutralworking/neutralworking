## Scouting Notes

A tough, disciplined left-back whose pressing and positional solidity make him valuable as a squad player, Malacia works hard on his near-side coverage and isn't afraid to engage full-backs physically. The problem is a lack of genuine quality in possession—his left foot is serviceable rather than confident, and he rarely creates anything meaningful from deep, limiting his utility in build-up play. He's the sort of dependable, unglamorous defender who fills a gap without filling it brilliantly; useful against a chaotic 10 Hag system, less so when United need to control games through the back.
