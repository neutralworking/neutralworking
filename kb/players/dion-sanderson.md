## Snapshot

A centre-back who reads the game with genuine intelligence and rarely panics in possession; he has the composure to play out from the back and the positional sense to cut out passes before they develop. His aerial dominance is reliable, though he's not the quickest at recovering against sharp, direct movements. A sensible, control-oriented defender who suits a side that wants to dictate play.
