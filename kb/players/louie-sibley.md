## Scouting Notes

An attacking midfielder with decent footwork in tight spaces and a willingness to roam across the pitch and seek possession. He dribbles at a reasonable level but lacks the decision-making or final-ball quality to be a genuine creator; he'll nutmeg a defender then put the pass in row Z. Functional attacking asset for a side that can tolerate his inefficiency, but he's not a player you build around.
