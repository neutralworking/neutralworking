## Snapshot

Physically imposing defender with excellent aerial dominance and the ability to read forward passes before they're played, though his lateral mobility is declining and he can be beat by direct runners using explosive first-step acceleration. A stalwart performer in the center of defense who remains one of Dortmund's most reliable ball-winners, but increasingly a player managing rather than controlling his frame.
