## Snapshot

A genuinely quick, aggressive left-winger who runs in behind with purpose and has the technical foundation to be something in a couple of years, but at 18 and still learning his trade, he's raw in both decision-making and consistency. His final ball is underdeveloped—he'll occasionally find a teammate with an incisive pass, but more often he overcomplicates or plays it sideways—and his ability to play in tight spaces rather than just running is still a work in progress. Worth the investment in a development plan, but not yet a finished article.
