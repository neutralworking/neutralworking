## Snapshot

Australian attacking midfielder with decent technical foundation and the kind of movement that suggests he understands space — makes intelligent runs into the half-spaces and isn't afraid of the half-turn. The issue is consistency in execution; he can drift invisible for stretches, and his final ball lacks the incisiveness you'd want from someone in his position at this level. Works hard off the ball and moves the play forward, but he's a touch-and-move operator rather than a creator, which limits his ceiling at anything above Eerste Divisie level.
