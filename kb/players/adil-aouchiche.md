## Scouting Notes

Left-footed eighth-man with decent ball retention and a tidy short passing range, but lacks the dynamism or press resistance you'd want from a midfielder stepping up a division. Operates best in a structured, possession-dominant setup where he can recycle play in the half-spaces; ask him to break lines or carry the ball through a congested middle and the limitations become apparent. Has the tools for a solid Ligue 2 operator, not much beyond.
