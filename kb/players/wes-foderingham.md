## Snapshot

A journeyman shot-stopper who's been around the block without ever quite nailing down a sustained run at the elite level. Decent shot-stopper with quick reactions and decent distribution for a goalkeeper of his generation, but he's 35 now and at Aris Limassol—a respectable Cypriot outfit—he's essentially in the twilight, managing games rather than commanding them. Won't embarrass himself on the ball, but he's never been the sort of goalkeeper who sweeps aggressively or commands his box with authority; you're getting a traditional, reactive presence between the sticks.
