Recent Form

Jules Koundé's 2025-2026 season at Barcelona has seen him make 26 appearances in La Liga, contributing one goal and three assists as of early April 2026. He has also featured in the Champions League during this period. However, recent reports from April 2026 indicate growing concern within Barcelona regarding Koundé's form, citing inconsistencies and a dip in performance, particularly during the Champions League quarter-final first leg against Atletico Madrid where he struggled defensively. This match also marked his first start in over a month following a hamstring injury.

Despite recent concerns, Koundé concluded the 2024-2025 season strongly, scoring the winning goal in the Copa del Rey final against Real Madrid and reaching 100 appearances for Barcelona. He was a pivotal player under Hansi Flick, making 53 appearances across all competitions in the 2024-2025 season, with four goals and eight assists, demonstrating his significant attacking contributions and durability. His ability to play a high volume of minutes at a high level was a notable aspect of his performance, with him playing 104 consecutive matches for club and country between November 2023 and April 2025.

Under Hansi Flick's tactical vision, Koundé has embraced an expanded offensive role, particularly as a right-back, contributing significantly to Barcelona's attacking play. His three assists in a Champions League match against Red Star Belgrade in November 2024 highlighted his improved offensive prowess and comfort in balancing defensive duties with attacking support. He has also shown an eye for a pass, with close to 20 forward passes per 90 minutes and high precision, aligning with Barcelona's possession-based style.

Tactical Profile

Jules Koundé is recognized for his exceptional versatility, capable of playing both as a central defender and, predominantly for Barcelona, as a right-back. His playing style is characterized by speed, quick interceptions, and comfort on the ball, making him difficult to beat in one-on-one situations. He is adept at defending high up the pitch, relying on his recovery pace to track back effectively.

Beyond his defensive solidity, Koundé contributes significantly to offensive phases. He possesses strong progressive passing abilities and has developed his crossing accuracy, even though he originally played as a center-back. His tactical intelligence allows him to make sound decisions on when to join the attack and when to maintain defensive shape, a crucial attribute in Barcelona's system. Under Hansi Flick, he has been given more freedom to push forward, showcasing ball-carrying acumen and an ability to identify attacking opportunities.

Koundé's defensive strengths include excellent anticipation, a calm demeanor, and physical prowess in duels despite his height. He is known for his ability to read the game, adapt his movements to attackers, and rarely concedes fouls. His capacity to perform effectively in a high-risk, high-pressure system as a full-back, while maintaining strong defensive metrics, underscores his adaptability and skill.

Career Highlights

Jules Koundé has accumulated several significant honors throughout his career. With Barcelona, he has been instrumental in winning multiple titles, including the Spanish Super Cup in 2022-2023, 2024-2025, and 2025-2026, and the La Liga title in 2022-2023 and 2024-2025. Notably, he scored the winning goal in the 2025 Copa del Rey final for Barcelona, securing another major trophy for the club.

Prior to joining Barcelona, Koundé achieved success with Sevilla, winning the UEFA Europa League in 2020. On the international stage, he has been a key member of the French national team, winning the UEFA Nations League in 2021 and being a FIFA World Cup runner-up in 2022. He also participated in UEFA Euro 2020 and Euro 2024, starting all six matches at right-back in the latter and being named man of the match in a victory over Belgium.

Individually, Koundé has been recognized for his consistent performances, earning a place in the UEFA Europa League Squad of the Season in 2019-2020 and the La Liga Team of the Season in 2021-2022, 2022-2023, and 2024-2025. He reached 100 appearances for FC Barcelona during the 2024-2025 season.

Personality

Jules Koundé is described as an introverted and quiet individual, a thinker who carefully considers his words before speaking to avoid giving a negative impression. He maintains a calm and focused demeanor both on and off the field, suggesting a preference for privacy and introspection.

Despite his reserved nature, Koundé possesses strong leadership qualities that were evident from a young age; a former coach at Bordeaux noted his remarkable leadership skills at 19, describing him as having the mentality of a veteran. He sees himself as a key player and one of the leaders in the Barcelona locker room, emphasizing his reliability. Koundé is dedicated to his profession, taking it very seriously and maintaining a rigorous routine for self-care and training, which he believes contributes to his consistent availability. He has also shown maturity in his public statements, for instance, praising rival Kylian Mbappé's natural leadership for the French national team.
