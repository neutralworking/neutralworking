## Snapshot

Tino Kadewere is a physical striker with a strong jumping ability and decent pace, allowing him to be a threat in the box, particularly from crosses and set pieces. However, his lack of awareness and tactical acumen often lead to him being in the wrong position, and his poor interceptions make him a non-factor in terms of pressing and winning the ball back. As a poacher, he can be effective, but his limitations in terms of build-up play and defensive contribution are clear.
