A left-back with decent positional awareness and the ability to stay compact defensively, Abdelli is the sort of workmanlike fullback who rarely embarrasses himself. His attacking output is non-existent—he doesn't overlap, he doesn't drift into the half-space, he just stands—and his pace is unremarkable. He's acceptable rotation depth for a club without serious European ambitions.
