Dean Huijsen is a 21-year-old Spanish centre-back for Real Madrid, known for his imposing 197 cm frame and ability to play with either foot. He is regarded as a "Conductor" and "Distributor" within his internal archetype and role, reflecting his comfort on the ball and capacity to initiate play from the back.

Recent Form

Dean Huijsen has been an active presence for Real Madrid during the 2025/2026 season, participating in 35 matches across all competitions, accumulating 2 goals and 2 assists. He has also registered 9 yellow cards and 1 red card during this period. In LaLiga specifically, he has made 23 appearances, scoring 2 goals and providing 2 assists, while receiving 6 yellow cards and 1 red card. His performance metrics for the 2025/2026 LaLiga season include an average FotMob rating of 7.24, with high scores in assists, overall rating, and goals compared to other centre-backs in the league.

His recent match involvement in March and April 2026 includes appearances against top-tier opponents such as Bayern München, Mallorca, Atlético Madrid, and Manchester City, as well as scoring a goal against Elche. Huijsen has also expressed pride in his return to the national team in March 2026, indicating an improvement in his form after a period he acknowledged as below his expected level. Prior to joining Real Madrid, his performances for AFC Bournemouth in the 2024/2025 Premier League season earned him a nomination for the PFA Young Player of the Year award, where he notably achieved the highest percentage of successful defensive duels (74.29%) among Bournemouth players with over 50 duels.

Tactical Profile

Huijsen operates as an aggressive, front-footed central defender who is comfortable playing on either side of the defence. He actively seeks to defend in front of the back line, often stepping into midfield to engage in duels or intercept passes, disrupting opponents' first touches and forcing them to receive with their back to goal. His tactical understanding allows him to read the game effectively and make crucial interceptions, demonstrating agility despite his significant height.

With the ball, Huijsen displays exceptional composure and is capable of initiating attacks from the back with precise passing, utilizing both feet effectively. He is known for his strong press resistance and versatile passing range, able to thread passes into midfield or deliver long balls to release wingers. His aerial dominance is a key asset, making him a threat in both defensive and offensive set-piece situations. While adaptable to various defensive formations, some tactical analyses suggest areas for improvement, particularly in one-on-one situations and physical duels against quick, agile attackers, where he can sometimes be overzealous.

Career Highlights

Born in Amsterdam but raised in Spain, Dean Huijsen began his youth career with Málaga CF before joining Juventus in May 2021. He made his senior professional debut for Juventus Next Gen in January 2023, notably scoring two goals in a Coppa Italia Serie C semi-final. His Serie A debut with the Juventus first team followed in October 2023.

In January 2024, Huijsen was loaned to AS Roma, where he scored two goals in 13 appearances. He then moved to AFC Bournemouth in July 2024 on a six-year deal, making his Premier League debut in August of that year. During his time at Bournemouth, he became the club's youngest-ever Premier League goalscorer in December 2024 and was nominated for the PFA Young Player of the Year award for his impressive 2024/2025 season performances. Having received Spanish nationality in 2024, Huijsen was called up to the Spain U21 squad and subsequently made his debut for the senior Spain national team in March 2025. In May 2025, Real Madrid activated his £50 million release clause, securing his transfer and signing him to a contract until July 2030. He made his Real Madrid debut at the 2025 FIFA Club World Cup and his La Liga debut in August 2025.

Personality

Dean Huijsen is described as a calm, professional, and determined individual who exhibits significant personality for his young age. Raphael Varane, a former Real Madrid defender, noted that Huijsen's ability to perform at a high level at such a young age speaks to his strong character and personality. Manolo Sanchís, another Real Madrid legend, also commented on his "phenomenal personality" and quick integration into the dressing room.

However, Huijsen faced controversy in February 2026 when he reposted an image on Instagram containing derogatory comments mocking the eye shape of Asian people, which led to widespread backlash and allegations of racism. He subsequently deleted the post.
