## Scouting Notes

An English winger with genuine technical ability and an eye for a dangerous pass, Edwards can operate in the half-spaces and create for others with a delicate touch. His movement is intelligent and he understands how to find pockets of space. However, he's not the most direct finisher himself, and his defensive work rate can be patchy — lovely in possession, but asking him to scrap in a high press is a stretch.
