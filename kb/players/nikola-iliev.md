## Scouting Notes


ClubInter

PositionAttacking midfielder

Born06 June 2004

A tremendously gifted player with great technique, he made his Bulgarian top-flight debut for Botev Plovdiv aged just 15. Having also scored 15 goals in 11 games for Botev’s under-17 side, he drew Ajax’s attention and the Dutch giants were close to signing him at the start of 2020. Iliev decided to wait as there were reports that Manchester United were interested too but when Inter came calling last summer he decided to move abroad. "I've always dreamed of playing for a big European club and this dream is now a reality," he said after the deal was confirmed. The _Nerazzurri_ reportedly paid €500,000 for the youngster, whose dribbling skills and playmaking abilities are truly impressive. **[Metodi Shumanov](https://twitter.com/shumanskoo)**  
  
**September 2022 update** Iliev scored the title-winning goal that won Inter U19’s the Primavera trophy and there have already been talks over a potential contract extension with the Nerazzurri. He has also just been called up to Bulgaria’s senior team for the Nations League games against Gibraltar and North Macedonia.  
  
**October 2023 update** Great progress for the attacking midfielder who made his debut for Bulgaria in 2022 and already has eight caps for the national team. At club level he has joined Bulgarian top-flight side CSKA 1948 on loan from Inter’s Primavera hoping to make a triumphant return to the Nerazzurri next summer.
