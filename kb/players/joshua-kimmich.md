## Scouting Notes

An elite defensive midfielder who combines intelligent positioning, pressing discipline, and the ability to dictate tempo through possession retention. His range of passing—both short and progressively long—is excellent, and he rarely turns the ball over in dangerous areas. At 31, his explosiveness in transition has declined marginally, and he's not the box-to-box athlete he was at 26, but he remains indispensable to Bayern's control.

Joshua Kimmich 

  

  

Joshua Kimmich tried to take the lead but often wanted too much. He was the central build-up player in Bayern's game, as central defense lacked strong players. The 28-year-old took responsibility as a penalty taker and was involved in important goals. In terms of football, he is indisputably one of the best sixes in Germany. In the national team, however, he did not live up to the increasing responsibility and is one of the faces of the DFB crisis.
