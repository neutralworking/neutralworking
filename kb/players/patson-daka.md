## Scouting Notes

Striker with genuinely sharp finishing instincts and the willingness to work in transition; he'll attack space intelligently and his ball retention in the box is better than his frame suggests. His inconsistency—both in positioning and in taking chances—remains a concern, and he doesn't offer the hold-up play required at the highest level. A rotation or Europa League-level striker, not a long-term primary option.
