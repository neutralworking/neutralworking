## Scouting Notes

A centre-forward whose profile is aerial dominance and physical presence in the box—he's a genuine threat at set pieces and can bully defenders in confined spaces. The issue is that his range of play outside that sphere is limited; he doesn't build play effectively, his technical execution under pressure is inconsistent, and he doesn't add much in a pressing scheme. As a backup option for a team that can build crosses or set pieces around him, he offers value; as a primary number nine, he's a limitation.
