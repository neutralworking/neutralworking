## Snapshot

A centre-back with decent positional sense and the physical tools to handle aerial duels, though his reading of the game remains inconsistent and he can be drawn out of position by clever movement in the half-spaces. He's a functional defender at this level—Qatar's standard—but lacks the composure on the ball to progress play with any real authority, which limits his utility in a possession-dominant side. At 29, he's a reliable squad option rather than a cornerstone.
