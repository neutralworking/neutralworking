## Snapshot

Aydın is a Sprinter-Dribbler, relying on exceptional pace, acceleration, and balance, making him a dynamic Flanker capable of decisive movement and a good first touch. As a traditional Flanker, he represents a direct wide threat, suitable for systems that emphasize quick transitions and vertical play, utilizing his speed to stretch defenses. While best suited as an Inventor creating from the right wing, his technical ability is only moderate, and he notably lacks guile in tight spaces, struggling with discipline despite strong mental and physical pillars. For a club seeking a quick, direct winger, Aydın, at 25, offers potential, but his limited tactical and technical attributes mean he needs a system built around his strengths.
