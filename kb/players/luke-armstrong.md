## Snapshot

Striker with reasonable movement in and around the penalty area and the ability to finish half-chances from close range, though he lacks the explosive first step or pace to create separation from defenders himself. Relies heavily on teammates creating space or pulling defenders away; in a team with limited possession he can look isolated. Better suited to a structured, possession-based system.
