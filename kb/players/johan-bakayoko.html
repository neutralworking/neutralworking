Recent Form

Johan Bakayoko's debut season with RB Leipzig in 2025/2026 has seen him make 17 appearances in the Bundesliga, contributing two goals but no assists across 751 minutes of play. His average FotMob rating for the Bundesliga season stands at 6.7, and he has received one yellow card. This performance has been noted as a "surprisingly weak debut season" for the Belgian winger.

Bakayoko has also participated in the DFB Pokal during the 2025/2026 season, where he holds a rating of 7.1. However, his season has been interrupted by several injury setbacks. He sustained a calf injury from December 2025 to January 2026, a muscle injury in November-December 2025, and another calf injury in October 2025.

Tactical Profile

Johan Bakayoko operates primarily as a right-sided inverted winger, known for his dynamic and active approach to the game. He frequently cuts inside onto his preferred left foot to create scoring opportunities, a trait that has drawn comparisons to Arjen Robben. Beyond staying wide, Bakayoko is adept at combining in midfield, dropping deep to receive the ball, and stretching play on the flanks.

His playing style is characterized by explosiveness, verticality, and exceptional dribbling ability, often utilizing sudden changes of pace and quick combinations to bypass defenders. Bakayoko attempts nearly 10 dribbles per game with a 52% success rate, positioning him among the most frequent and effective dribblers in Europe's top leagues. He is also strong in offensive duels, completing 41% due to his agility and rapid acceleration. Furthermore, he contributes defensively with an intense pressing game. While possessing good passing ability and vision for crosses, he can sometimes be prone to losing possession and has shown areas for improvement in decision-making and long-range passing accuracy.

Career Highlights

Bakayoko joined RB Leipzig in July 2025 from PSV Eindhoven for a transfer fee between €18 million and €22 million. During his tenure at PSV, he was instrumental in securing significant silverware, including two Eredivisie titles in 2023/24 and 2024/25, two KNVB Cups in 2021/22 and 2022/23, and two Johan Cruyff Shields in 2022 and 2023. His most prolific campaign came in 2023/24, where he registered 12 goals and 9 assists in 33 league games, accumulating 14 goals across all competitions. His impressive performances earned him the Eredivisie Talent of the Year award in 2023/24.

He made his senior debut for PSV in February 2022 and scored his first Eredivisie goal during the 2022-23 season. Internationally, Bakayoko debuted for the Belgium national team in March 2023 and netted his first international goal in June 2023. Notably, he was coached by legendary striker Ruud van Nistelrooy during his time with Jong PSV, where he recorded 17 goals and 12 assists in his final season with the youth team.

Condition / Medical History

Johan Bakayoko has experienced several injuries during the 2025/2026 season with RB Leipzig. These include a calf injury that kept him sidelined from December 2025 to January 2026, a muscle injury in November-December 2025, and another calf injury in October 2025. Prior to his move to Leipzig, he also dealt with an illness in February 2024 and an unspecified injury during the 2024/2025 season.
