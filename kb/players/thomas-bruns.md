Bruns is a composed, technically assured metodista—the metronome archetype—with excellent vision, shielding, and stamina that anchors build-up sequences and dictates tempo through intelligent positioning rather than dynamism. His one-club longevity and strong mentality signal reliability, yet limited tactical thinking and weak dueling ability mean he's at his best in possession-rich, structurally tight systems where he can orchestrate rather than contest ground transitions.
