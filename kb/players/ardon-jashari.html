Ardon Jashari: Midfield Dynamo at AC Milan

Ardon Jashari, the 23-year-old Swiss midfielder, joined AC Milan on August 6, 2025, for a reported €36 million, marking a significant transfer in Belgian football history. Known for his versatility and tactical intelligence, Jashari has quickly integrated into the Serie A side after a stellar season in Belgium. His current market value is estimated between €30 million and €41.3 million.

Recent Form

Jashari's 2025/2026 season at AC Milan has seen him make 10 Serie A appearances, contributing one assist in 685 minutes of play. A notable performance came on February 19, 2026, where he was named MVP in AC Milan's 1-1 draw against Como. In that match, he provided a decisive assist for Rafael Leão's equalizer, showcasing his vision, control, and ability to impact both offensive and defensive phases, completing 60 passes and winning two tackles. Despite an early season injury setback, Jashari has demonstrated his value to the squad, with an average FotMob rating of 6.78 for the current Serie A season.

Tactical Profile

Jashari is recognized as a complete and versatile midfielder, adept at fulfilling roles from defensive midfield to an advanced playmaker. His playing style has drawn comparisons to Sergio Busquets due to his calmness on the ball, positional discipline, and deep tactical understanding. He is also likened to Granit Xhaka for his ball distribution, recovery skills, and being left-footed. Jashari excels in both build-up play and ball-winning, possessing excellent passing range, vision, and construction ability, making him particularly effective in possession-dominant systems.

His tactical contributions extend to leading the press, organizing defensive shapes, and actively closing down opponents to force errors. Jashari is proficient in tackling and interceptions, demonstrating a sharp reading of the game. Despite not being the tallest, he effectively shields the ball and can break the press using quick feet and dribbling. He consistently ranks high for progressive passes and long balls, showcasing his ability to find central depth and switch play effectively.

Career Highlights

Before his move to AC Milan, Jashari enjoyed a highly successful season with Club Brugge in 2024-2025, where he made 35 appearances, scoring three goals and providing six assists. His exceptional performances earned him both the Player of the Year and Young Player of the Year awards at the Pro League Awards in 2025, as well as the prestigious Belgian Golden Shoe. During his time at Club Brugge, he also helped the team win the Belgian Cup (2024/2025) and reach the UEFA Champions League Round of 16. Earlier in his career, Jashari won the Swiss Cup with FC Luzern in the 2020/2021 season and was named the Swiss Super League Player of the Month in September 2022. He made his debut for the Switzerland national team in September 2022.

Personality

Ardon Jashari is described as a calm and mature individual, particularly for his age. He has openly expressed that joining AC Milan was a childhood dream, a sentiment he shared upon his arrival at the club. Jashari is eager to learn from experienced teammates like Luka Modrić, acknowledging the privilege of playing alongside such accomplished figures. He identifies as Swiss and also speaks Albanian. While aiming to be a leader on the pitch, he describes himself as soft-spoken off it, enjoying conversations with teammates and expressing excitement for the Champions League.

Condition / Medical History

Jashari's transition to AC Milan was impacted by an early injury during the 2025/2026 season. On August 28, 2025, he sustained a fractured fibula in training, an injury that sidelined him for approximately two to three months. He also experienced a calf injury from late August to early November 2025. Following his return, he had a minor injury from January 19-23, 2026. Throughout his career, he has also faced some disciplinary suspensions due to yellow cards.
