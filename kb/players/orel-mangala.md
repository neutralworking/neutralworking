## Snapshot

Energetic defensive midfielder who wins possession through aggression and positioning rather than technical interception work. Recycling play isn't his strength and he lacks the composure to orchestrate play from deep, tending to hit safe backwards passes when pressed. Effective as a pure destroyer in a compact system, but limited if you need your deepest midfielder to contribute to build-up phases.
