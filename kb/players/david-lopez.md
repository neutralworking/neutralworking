## Snapshot

Central defender with strong reading of the game and good leadership qualities; he positions himself intelligently to intercept and is comfortable in possession for building from the back. He's not particularly quick laterally and can be exposed by clever runs or balls into the half-space behind him. A steady, no-nonsense defender who organizes and doesn't make mistakes, but he's not stopping elite forwards through athleticism.
