## Scouting Notes

A box-to-box midfielder with legitimate work ethic and the physique to mix it in transition; he covers ground well and isn't shy about a challenge, giving Clermont some grit in the middle third. His passing is pragmatic—side-to-side stuff mostly, nothing creative—and he lacks the technical security to progress the ball through tight spaces or retain possession when the opposition press high. He's a squad player at a Ligue 1 club, useful for bodies and energy, but lacks the craft or vision to drive Clermont's play forward when it matters.
