## Snapshot

A poacher with legitimate technical range and aerial dominance, yet whose mental limitations and modest physical endurance raise serious questions about consistency and resilience under sustained pressure. His tactical intelligence—particularly his take-on and close-range finishing—marks him as a sharp finisher comfortable in the box, but his vision and carrying ability are poor, meaning he cannot function as a complete forward demanding service from deep. At 24 and in his peak, Strelec has a defined window to convert his technical gifts into goals at a higher level; Middlesbrough's investment suggests faith in his poaching prowess, but the absence of durability and mental robustness means he's best deployed as a focal point given clear service rather than expected to carry creative burden. Clubs seeking an efficient penalty-box operator with aerial threat will find value; those needing a complete forward or selfless team contributor should consider alternatives.
