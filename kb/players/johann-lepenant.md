## Snapshot

A technically fluent young midfielder with excellent press resistance and the ability to dribble out of congested spaces; his range of passing is impressive and he shows maturity in recognising when to slow play. Defensively, he can be overrun in transition and sometimes lacks the intensity needed against physical opponents. A genuine prospect who should be playing European football within two years if his trajectory continues.
