## Snapshot

Tim Ream functions as a classic Cover Zagueiro, demonstrating strong tactical awareness and technical ability in his interceptions, clearances, and ability to carry the ball out of defense. He excels in reading the game and covering space, making him ideal for a system that relies on a deeper defensive line, but his limited mental strength and lack of aggression mean he struggles with high-intensity pressing and direct blocking. A Director of Football would target him for a possession-based side needing a composed ball-playing center-back, but his vulnerabilities in aggressive defensive schemes and physical confrontations need careful consideration.
