## Scouting Notes

A compact midfielder whose game revolves around winning the ball and disrupting opposition tempo through aggressive pressing and intelligent positioning. His tackling and blocking work mark him as a genuine destroyer archetype, though his technical range—particularly long-range passing and ball-striking—is merely functional rather than a platform for playmaking. The discipline issues are a real concern for a 32-year-old entering the twilight of his career; his hot-headedness will eventually cost a team in crucial moments, and his moderate physical profile means he cannot sustain the high-press intensity forever. Best suited to a club needing a short-term battler in central midfield, ideally one where his competitive fire can be channeled into a structured system that doesn't demand progressive range or creative sophistication.
