## Snapshot

ClubFluminense

PositionMidfielder

Born23 April 2003

Metinho was born in Congo but his family moved to Brazil when he was only one year old. It was a difficult period for his parents but they grew stronger as the days and weeks passed by. In his new country, Abemly Meto Silu became Metinho, a nickname in keeping with his new life and new country. He is working hard and is planning a great future at Fluminense. His main inspiration is Paul Pogba, not only for the Frenchman’s football, but because of his appearance too. Some supporters even call him “Pogbinha”. Last year, he obtained Brazilian citizenship and is free to target his main dream: playing for the Seleção. **[Thiago Rabelo](https://twitter.com/thiagorabelo1?lang=en)**  
[Watch Metinho in action](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=R1MFuYwD0gk)  
  
**October 2021 update** Was one of the best players for Brazil’s under-17s last year. His excellent performances caught the attention of Manchester City and they paid €5m to sign him from Fluminense. In July, he was loaned to the French club Troyes to gain experience in European football.  
  
**September 2022 update** A shoulder injury kept him out until February and even after that he did not make any first-team minutes for Troyes. Is spending this season on loan at Lommel in the Belgian second tier.  
  
**October 2023 update** Made 27 appearances in all competitions for Lommel last season and has earned a new loan move, this time to Sparta Rotterdam in the Dutch top flight. Will be interesting to see how he gets on.
