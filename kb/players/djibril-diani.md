## Scouting Notes

A winger with decent explosive power and the ability to create from the byline, Diani is the kind of functional wide player who contributes in a narrow sense—he'll whip in a cross, occasionally beat a man, and show up for the defensive side of things when required. His issue is banality: there's nothing in his game that suggests he could operate at a higher level, and his decision-making when he gets into good positions is often lateral rather than penetrative. He's a reliable squad player, which is precisely his ceiling.
