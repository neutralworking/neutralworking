## Scouting Notes


ClubBechem United FC

PositionStriker

Born23 September 2003

Prince Kwabena Adu got off to a flying start at the beginning of last season, catching everyone by surprise by netting eight league goals in 12 matches. A clever and mobile striker with explosive rapid acceleration and nifty dribbling, Adu will not stay for long in the Ghanaian Premier League. He has already been called up to represent the Black Satellites and has spoken on the record of interest from Scandanavian clubs. **[Maher Mezahi](https://twitter.com/mezahimaher)**  
[Watch Prince Kwabena Adu in action](https://twitter.com/OtiAdjei/status/1228962464450142208)  
  
**October 2021 update** Adu was called up for the Black Stars late last year but has suffered with injuries since, necessitating surgery and hampering his progress.  
  
**September 2022 update** Despite rumours of a move to Danish club Randers FC, Adu is still at Bechem United where he enjoys regular playing time.  
  
**October 2023 update** Finally made the move to Europe earlier this year and scored eight goals in 16 appearances in Belarus for Isloch. Adu then completed a deadline day transfer to Ukrainian club Kryvbas, but has yet to feature for them at the time of writing.
