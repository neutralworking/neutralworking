## Scouting Notes

A genuinely intelligent left winger who's spent enough time in the Celta system to know exactly where the half-spaces open up and when to drift infield for the underlap. His best work comes when receiving in transition with space to run at; he's tidy enough on the ball and his decision-making is rarely panicked. The problem is the engine—at 31, he lacks the relentless pressing intensity a modern La Liga club demands, and he's not quick enough to consistently beat defenders on the outside anymore, which limits his effectiveness against compact defenses.
