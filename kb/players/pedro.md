## Scouting Notes

A versatile attacking midfielder or right winger with excellent technical ability and the capacity to find space through intelligent movement, even as he approaches 28 years old. His pressing is reliable, and he's effective both in narrow spaces and on the flank. The trade-off is that he's not quite explosive enough to dribble past multiple defenders, meaning his best work comes in possession-dominant phases where he can execute one or two touches and find a teammate.
