Edon Zhegrova, the 27-year-old Kosovan wide forward, currently plays for Juventus, having joined the Italian club on September 1, 2025. Standing at approximately 180-181 cm tall, Zhegrova is known for his left-footed play and operates primarily as a right winger or attacking midfielder.

Recent Form

Zhegrova's 2025-2026 season at Juventus has seen limited playing time and production. He has made 13 appearances in Serie A, one in the UEFA Champions League, and two in the Coppa Italia. Across these Serie A appearances, he has yet to register a goal or an assist, accumulating 289-290 minutes on the pitch. His average FotMob rating for the season stands at 6.41.

In recent Serie A matches, Zhegrova has frequently been an unused substitute or played minimal minutes, such as 19 minutes on March 31, 2026, and 2 minutes on March 21, 2026. Despite taking 14 shots in his 13 Serie A matches, with half of them on target, he has not converted any into goals. Public reporting suggests a "disappointing campaign" and struggles for form, leading to speculation about a potential summer exit, although Zhegrova himself is reportedly determined to fight for his place at the club.

Tactical Profile

Edon Zhegrova is characterized as a direct, agile, left-footed winger who frequently cuts in from the right flank. His primary strengths include exceptional dribbling, creativity, powerful ball-striking, acceleration, and agility. He is particularly effective in one-on-one situations and is adept at progressive carries, moving the ball forward into dangerous areas.

Zhegrova possesses a high volume of successful take-ons and contributes significantly to shot-creating actions. He is known for his ability to deliver quality crosses, often cutting inside and dinking the ball to the back post, which was a notable source of goals for his previous club, Lille. He also demonstrates a strong preference for shooting from distance, utilizing his long-shot ability. While his offensive attributes are highly praised, his weaknesses include aerial ability and defensive contribution.

Career Highlights

Edon Zhegrova's career has seen him play for several European clubs before his move to Juventus. He joined Juventus on September 1, 2025, from Lille, in a transfer valued at approximately €15 million. During his time at Lille, he scored his first UEFA Champions League goal on October 23, 2024, in an away victory against Atlético Madrid.

Early in his career, Zhegrova was recognized as "Hope of the Year" in Kosovo. He has also achieved team success, winning the Swiss Cup with FC Basel in the 2018/19 season and the Jupiler Pro League with Genk in 2019. Individually, he was named Footballer of the Year in Kosovo in 2024. Prior to his Juventus transfer, he was Lille's most prolific dribbler in Ligue 1 from the 2021/22 season, completing 181 dribbles.

Personality

Edon Zhegrova is often described as a player who approaches the game with natural ease and a desire to enjoy himself on the pitch. He has earned a reputation as a "mercurial talent" and a "walking highlight reel" due to his exciting and unpredictable style of play. Over time, he has shown maturity, evolving from a player who might have focused solely on individual brilliance to one who is more integrated into team dynamics.

In interviews, Zhegrova has revealed that his hidden talent is music, a passion he shares with his sister. His spontaneity and ability to maintain his unique personality are noted, even within the structured environment of a club like Juventus. His parents are known to be very supportive, frequently visiting him. Despite a challenging start at Juventus, Zhegrova has expressed a strong determination to fight for his place in the squad.

Condition / Medical History

Edon Zhegrova has experienced several injuries throughout his career. He notably missed the latter half of the 2024-25 season due to a hip adductor injury sustained on December 14, 2024, returning to action in August 2025. His medical history also includes a virus (2025), a groin injury (2024), periods of physical discomfort or lack of fitness (2023, 2025), and various muscle injuries (2022, 2023). Additionally, he has dealt with knee (2021), meniscus (2021), rib (2022), ankle (2020), and thigh (2023) injuries.
