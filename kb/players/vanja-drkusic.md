## Scouting Notes

A robust, physical centre-back who wins his aerial duels and isn't afraid of the physical contest, the kind of defender who'll slide in front of you without hesitation. He reads the game reasonably well for a player operating at this level and has the legs to recover if positioning slips. However, he lacks the technical ease on the ball that modern football demands—his distribution under pressure is pedestrian, he can't consistently play out from the back, and against technically superior attackers he can be drawn out of position or caught in transition.
