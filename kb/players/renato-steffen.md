## Snapshot

A Swiss winger entering his mid-thirties whose best days—a spell of genuine dynamism at Wolfsburg—are firmly in the rear-view mirror. He retains the ability to find space in the half-spaces and can still deliver a dangerous cross when the opportunity presents itself, but his pace has eroded to the point where he's no longer a consistent threat on the dribble, and his decision-making in the final third remains maddeningly inconsistent. In a Swiss Super League context, he's a functional starter capable of contributing to a mid-table push; anywhere more ambitious, he'd be a squad depth piece at best. His game has become predictable—defenders know he'll slow down before committing to a move—and there's little left in the tank to suggest otherwise.
