## Snapshot

A young German keeper still finding his feet in professional football, Seimen shows the essential foundations — decent footwork for a modern distributor, reasonable command of his box, the kind of composure you'd expect from a German academy product. The issue is that at 20, he's still a prospect rather than a performer; he doesn't have the presence or decision-making consistency of a ready-made starter, and his positioning can be loose against direct play. He'll likely need another season or two of regular football to establish whether he's genuinely Bundesliga material or destined for a lower tier.
