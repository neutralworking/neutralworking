## Snapshot

Swiss winger-forward with genuine electric pace on the burst and a low centre of gravity that allows him to evade pressure with quick footwork. His directness is a weapon, but his final product is inconsistent and his crossing is often wayward—he has the athleticism to trouble any defender, but lacks the composure and spatial awareness to consistently convert that advantage.
