## Snapshot

Box-to-box midfielder with a strong physical presence and reasonable ball-carrying ability; he's useful in transition and can arrive at the back post to finish chances. He's not a natural ball-progressor and can be careless under pressure, losing possession in dangerous areas. Solid squad option, particularly in matches where you want physicality and directness over sustained possession.
