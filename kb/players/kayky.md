## Snapshot

A left winger with genuine pace and directness—he'll take a man on in the half-space and has the leg speed to create separation, which is rare at Fluminense's level. The issue is consistency and decision-making: he can go missing in games, his final ball is unreliable, and he doesn't yet have the positional intelligence to operate as a true left-eight in a more structured system. He's still developing as a finisher and lacks the physicality to dominate lower-tier Brazilian defenders consistently, but there's a genuinely interesting athlete here if the mentality hardens.
