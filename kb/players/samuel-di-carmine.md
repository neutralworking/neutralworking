## Snapshot

Samuel Di Carmine is a seasoned striker with a physical presence, boasting impressive acceleration, balance, and shielding abilities, allowing him to hold up the ball and bring others into play, but his technical shortcomings and lack of intensity mean he often struggles to create chances for himself, and his tactical awareness is a concern, as he can be slow to react to changing situations on the pitch. His playing style is more suited to a target man role, where he can use his physicality to win headers and flick-ons, rather than a mobile, progressive striker. Despite his experience, Di Carmine's limitations mean he is not a striker who can carry a team on his own.
