## Snapshot

Ivorian centre-back with genuine pace and an aggressive approach to defending, comfortable stepping into midfield to press and capable of covering ground quickly to recover errors. His passing range is moderate and he occasionally misjudges distance when making blocks, but his intensity and athleticism make him a proper top-six defender in Italy. A cornerstone of any Roma backline that aspires to European football.
