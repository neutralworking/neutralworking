Evan Ndicka, a 26-year-old Ivorian center-back for AS Roma, has established himself as a formidable presence in defense, known for his left-footed play and imposing 192 cm height. He operates with an internal archetype of a "Conductor" and a "Sweeper" role.

## Recent Form
Evan Ndicka has demonstrated exceptional consistency and a surprising offensive flair in recent seasons. During the 2024-25 Serie A season, he achieved a remarkable feat by becoming the first Roma outfield player since 2004 to play every minute of all 38 league matches without substitution, showcasing his durability and importance to the squad. This ironman performance anchored Roma's defense and contributed to their securing a Europa League spot.

In the current 2025-26 season, Ndicka has continued his strong performances, notably adding significant offensive contributions. As of March 2026, he has scored four goals in his last five matches across all competitions for AS Roma, including strikes against Cremonese, Juventus, and Bologna in the Europa League. This unexpected "striker's instinct," particularly from set pieces, has made him an additional attacking asset for his team, bringing his total to five goals in 35 games for the season.

## Tactical Profile
Ndicka is a versatile defender, primarily operating as a center-back but also capable of playing as a left-back. He is recognized for his aggressive style, effectively pressing high and quickly winning back possession. His tactical intelligence allows him to marshal teammates and direct the defense, acting as a crucial "glue" that instills confidence within the team, a quality he notably displayed while wearing the captain's armband in a Europa League match.

Beyond his defensive solidity, which includes strong aerial ability and good recovery pace, Ndicka is comfortable and technically sound with the ball at his feet. He exhibits good vision and passing ability, contributing to build-up play from the back. His recent development of a goal-scoring touch, particularly from set-pieces, highlights his growing influence across both defensive and offensive phases of play.

## Career Highlights
Evan Ndicka began his professional career with Auxerre before joining Eintracht Frankfurt in 2018, where he quickly became a key player. His performances earned him the Bundesliga Rookie of the Month award in February 2019. A significant achievement during his time in Germany was winning the 2021–22 UEFA Europa League with Eintracht Frankfurt.

He signed with AS Roma on a five-year contract in June 2023. Ndicka made his international debut for Côte d’Ivoire on September 9, 2023, after switching his allegiance from France. He played a pivotal role in his national team's success, featuring in every minute of Côte d’Ivoire's victorious 2023 Africa Cup of Nations campaign. His first goal for Roma came in the UEFA Europa League on November 28, 2024, in an away draw against Tottenham Hotspur.

## Condition / Medical History
Ndicka has experienced a few medical incidents throughout his career, though he generally maintains strong physical resilience. On April 14, 2024, he collapsed during a Serie A match against Udinese due to chest pain, but was conscious when taken off the field and was discharged from the hospital after a few days to continue his recovery.

His injury history also includes various minor setbacks such as muscle injuries, a knee injury, an ankle injury, and periods of illness. Despite these, he has shown remarkable durability, exemplified by his ironman season in 2024-25 where he played every minute of all Serie A matches.
