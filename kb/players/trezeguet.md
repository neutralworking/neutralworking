## Snapshot

A right winger operating in a narrow, inverted role where he cuts onto his left foot and looks for shooting opportunities or late-arriving cutback passes rather than attempting to beat his man consistently on the line. He's got a reasonable left-foot strike and understands half-space positioning well enough to be a rotation option at a mid-table Egyptian outfit. What he isn't is a dynamic threat in 1v1 situations or someone who stretches defences horizontally; his best work comes when the space is already compressed and he can make a quick technical decision, which limits his impact against well-organized defending.
