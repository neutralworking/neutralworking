## Snapshot

A left-back with genuine attacking intent who uses his pace well in transition and isn't afraid to invert into midfield spaces on the ball. He's quick enough to recover defensively and has the athleticism to play high. The limitation is consistency and concentration—his defending is streaky, he can be caught out of position by one-touch combinations, and he doesn't have the spatial intelligence to anticipate danger before it arrives.
