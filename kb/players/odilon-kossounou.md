## Scouting Notes

A centre-back defined by aggressive ball-playing and willingness to step into midfield to break lines, which is excellent until it isn't—his positional discipline can be suspect, and he occasionally leaves his back line exposed through overeagerness. He's strong in one-on-one situations and reads the game well, making him an asset in a high-pressing system. His limitations surface against composed, technical attacking play that punishes his occasional recklessness.
