## Snapshot

A left-back who remains a genuinely competent operator despite being into his early thirties. Digne's reading of the game is excellent, he's a threat on the overlap, and he delivers a consistent cross. The physical decline is evident when asked to recover against genuine pace, and younger options will eventually supersede him, but he's not finished as a high-level football player.
