## Scouting Notes

Athletic attacking midfielder with sharp vertical passing and the capacity to manipulate space in the half-spaces through intelligent positioning. Tends to operate in bursts rather than sustaining tempo, and his press resistance isn't elite—gets hurried when teams close him quickly. Useful rotational option at a competitive Ligue 1 side but lacks the consistency or range to anchor a midfield.

Karabec is the most talented player at the Sparta Prague youth academy and, probably, in the Czech Republic. He started playing at Bohemians Prague but moved to Sparta at the age of 15. Like many of the most talented players, he always played with older age groups. Karabec has excellent technique, very good vision and always seems to make the right decisions. After winning many man-of-the-tournament awards at youth level he was promoted to senior football at the age of 16. At first he played for Sparta’s reserves but made his first-team debut still aged 16. This summer he received an invitation to the national team for the first time, although he remained on the bench for the game against Scotland. In the Czech league he has, at the time of writing, played eight matches and scored one goal. If he keeps improving, he will be a player for a top European club. **[Pavel Hartmann](https://twitter.com/PavelHartman1)**  
  
**October 2021 update** Is part of the first team squad but has been a bit unfortunate in that he is playing in the same position as the veteran Borek Dockal, who is first choice at the moment. He is still improving but has thus far only played three games in the league this season.  
  
**September 2022 update** Has, remarkably, played 94 senior games for Sparta at the age of 19 but he is still not a regular starter. He will want to change that during the 2022-23 season.  
  
**October 2023 update** Still mostly used as a substitute at Sparta Prague and that is a bit problematic as he appears to be better when he starts. The quality is clearly there, as he showed at the Under-21 Euros, where he was one of the Czech Republic's best players.
