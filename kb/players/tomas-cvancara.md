## Snapshot

A physical, direct centre-forward who operates as a focal point for Antalyaspor's attack, relying on strength and aerial dominance rather than technical subtlety. He's effective in the channels and capable of holding play up, but his first touch can be heavy and he lacks the movement intelligence or spatial awareness to consistently find space in congested boxes—he's a battering ram, not a surgeon. At 25 in the Turkish top flight, he's a functional starter with some Premier League loan experience in his past, though nothing suggests he'll develop into a truly elite number nine.
