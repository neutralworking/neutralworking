## Snapshot

An England international right-winger whose explosive first step and inverted running style make him genuinely difficult to contain in the half-spaces — he'll dart infield from the flank, drag defenders into dangerous positions, and either create or finish himself. His crossing from the byline is functional rather than elite, and he can be caught defensively when his gambits don't come off, but the attacking threat is real and consistent at this level. For Fiorentina, he's a very good squad rotator and European-level talent, though he's never quite breached the ceiling of a truly world-class winger because his decision-making under pressure remains slightly erratic.
