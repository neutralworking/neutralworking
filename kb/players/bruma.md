## Scouting Notes

A tidy, intelligent winger with excellent pressing intensity and a willingness to drift into half-spaces to receive and turn. His finishing remains frustratingly imprecise—he creates chances far more consistently than he converts them. Works hard defensively and understands positional play, but lacks the explosive acceleration or directness needed to be a consistent threat at the highest level.
