This "false nine" aspect of his game is a key tactical element for Bayern Munich, allowing him to distribute long passes and switch play effectively.

His tactical intelligence and spatial awareness enable him to make decisive contributions whether scoring or assisting. Kane possesses excellent finishing ability with both feet and is a significant aerial threat, frequently scoring headed goals.
