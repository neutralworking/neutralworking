## Snapshot

A physically aggressive 25-year-old sweeper with strong tackling and interception instinct but a notable weakness in range and short-distance control that constrains his technical reliability in possession; his aggression and jumping compensate for moderate mental resilience, making him a physical force rather than a calm, composed distributor. His newcomer career status and Porto placement suggest development ambition, yet his volleys and close-range deficiency indicate he requires a structured tactical system to minimize creative burden. Fit for clubs valuing reactive, athletic sweeping play where limited technical range in buildup isn't a liability.
