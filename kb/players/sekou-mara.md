## Scouting Notes

A French centre-forward with proper intensity in the pressing game and enough technical quality to hold play up briefly, though he's not a classic number nine in the traditional sense. His movement in the box can be laboured and he doesn't generate the sort of clinical finishing you'd want at the sharp end; instead, he's more valuable as a disruptor who makes defences uncomfortable. At 25, he's settled into his ceiling as a functional lead man.
