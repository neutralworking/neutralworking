## Snapshot

As an Extremo, Fassnacht is an aerial threat from wide positions, combining impressive jumping with a powerful physique and quick acceleration to get past his marker. He's adept at beating a man with take-ons, yet his almost non-existent creativity and guile severely limit his ability to unlock defenses with a clever pass or intelligent movement in the final third. He's a bulldozer, not a craftsman.
