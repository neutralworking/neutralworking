## Snapshot

A centre-back operating at a realistic League Two level, offering physicality and a willingness to be first to the ball, but without the composure in possession or reading of space that marks out better defenders. He's a commitment player—solid headers, decent in the air—but his passing range is limited and he can be schooled by strikers who move him laterally. Honest player, ceiling is clear.
