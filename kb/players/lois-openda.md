## Scouting Notes

Mobile centre-forward with explosive acceleration and genuine finishing quality in transitions; can press effectively and work the channels to create space. His aerial ability is limited, and his hold-up play remains elementary compared to more physical number-nines, but his movement is intelligent and his decision-making increasingly assured. A modern forward who thrives in high-tempo systems but can struggle when football becomes methodical.
