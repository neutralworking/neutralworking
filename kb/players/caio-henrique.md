## Snapshot

Athletic left-back with excellent recovery pace and a genuine threat on the overlap, comfortable in transitions and capable of affecting play high up the pitch. His crossing is serviceable, though not his primary strength, and his weakness is positional discipline in a back four—he can be caught too high or too narrow. Reliable rather than inspiring, the kind of defender who makes matches easier without dominating them.
