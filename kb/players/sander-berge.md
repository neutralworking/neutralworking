## Snapshot

A rangy defensive midfielder whose primary contribution is regaining possession with intelligent positioning and distribution in transition, capable of playing vertical passes that bypass defensive lines with surprising subtlety. His ability to dominate possession phases is limited, his short-passing range under intense pressing can be exploitative, and his athleticism has diminished slightly with age. In a side that values tempo and directness, he's genuinely valuable.
