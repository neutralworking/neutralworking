## Snapshot

A press-resistant, ball-progressing midfielder with excellent press-evasion mechanics and an ability to escape pressure through intelligent positioning and quick short passes. He's most effective in a side with structure around him rather than as a lone creative voice, and while he rarely loses possession, his long-range passing range is limited and he doesn't contribute enough defensively for a top-six team. As a Bundesliga regular for a competitive mid-table outfit, he's perfectly fit, but he lacks the elite tempo or creation to function at a higher level.
