Boubacar Kamara: Player Research Brief

Recent Form

Boubacar Kamara's 2025-2026 season with Aston Villa was significantly impacted by injury, though he demonstrated strong performance prior to being sidelined. Before his season-ending knee injury in January 2026, Kamara had made 18 appearances in the Premier League, contributing 1 goal and 3 assists across 1,418 minutes of play. He maintained an average FotMob rating of 7.25 and accumulated 5 yellow cards during this period. Aston Villa's record with Kamara in the Premier League this season was impressive, with 13 wins, 3 draws, and only 2 losses.

His contributions were noted as crucial to Aston Villa's success, with his presence allowing teammate Douglas Luiz greater freedom and providing control to the team's midfield. Kamara's return to the first team in October 2024, following an ACL injury in February 2024, saw him gradually regain his form, including scoring his first Premier League goal for Aston Villa in May 2025. He also signed a new contract with Aston Villa in July 2025, extending until 2030, underscoring his importance to the club's long-term plans.

Tactical Profile

Boubacar Kamara operates primarily as a defensive midfielder, a role in which he excels due to his awareness, positioning, and ability to win duels. He is noted for his strong defensive capabilities, including high numbers of interceptions and successful ground duels, making him an effective ball-winner. Kamara's tactical intelligence allows him to scan his surroundings constantly, anticipate danger, and effectively break up opposition play. He is also capable of dropping into a center-back position, a role he played during his youth career.

In possession, Kamara functions as a deep-lying playmaker, comfortable in tight spaces and adept at distributing the ball to teammates. While his passing is often focused on connecting and controlling tempo, he possesses a good passing range and vision, capable of executing long passes and switches of play. His ability to recycle possession and shield the defense is a key aspect of his game, making him a vital component in Unai Emery's system at Aston Villa.

Career Highlights

Kamara progressed through the Olympique de Marseille academy, joining at the age of five and eventually captaining the U19 team. He made his professional debut for Marseille in December 2016 and went on to play 170 times for his boyhood club, featuring in various midfield and defensive roles. During his time at Marseille, he participated in the 2017-2018 Europa League campaign, reaching the final. He also contributed to Marseille's second-place finish in Ligue 1 in the 2019-2020 and 2021-2022 seasons.

Kamara has represented France at multiple youth levels, including the U17, U18, U19, U20, and U21 teams, participating in tournaments such as the 2019 FIFA U-20 World Cup and the 2021 UEFA European Under-21 Championship. He earned his first call-up to the senior France national team in May 2022 and made his debut in June 2022, securing his international future with France. He has accumulated 5 caps for the senior national team. In May 2022, he joined Aston Villa on a five-year contract, and in July 2025, he signed a new contract with the club until 2030.

Condition / Medical History

Boubacar Kamara has experienced significant injury setbacks during his career, particularly involving his knee. Most recently, he suffered a serious knee injury on January 10, 2026, during an FA Cup match against Tottenham Hotspur, which required surgery in Lyon and ruled him out for the remainder of the 2025-2026 season. This injury was to the same knee that had been previously affected.

Prior to this, Kamara sustained an anterior cruciate ligament (ACL) injury in February 2024, which sidelined him for eight months. He returned to football on October 5, 2024, playing for Aston Villa's U21 team, and made his full return to the first team on October 22, 2024. His injury history also includes various muscle, thigh, and ankle issues throughout his career. The recurring nature of his knee injuries has raised concerns about his long-term durability.
