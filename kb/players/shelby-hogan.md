## Snapshot

An athletic, reactive goalkeeper with genuine presence in the box and the kind of frame that commands respect on crosses and set pieces. She's most comfortable in a fairly direct, organized system where shot volume is manageable and service into her box is predictable; less comfortable with the modern demand to be an outfield sweeper or progression passer. She's a solid reserve-level keeper who can hold her own in the NWSL, but lacks the all-around game-intelligence and distribution range to be truly competitive for a starting berth at a title-contending side.
