## Snapshot

A right-back with genuine athleticism and the ability to contribute in the final third, but his concentration can lapse defensively and he's vulnerable to sustained pressure down his flank. Johnson has Premier League experience and understands the demands, yet his inconsistency in one-on-one situations limits his ceiling. He's a serviceable squad fullback for a team in transition.
