## Snapshot

Serbian goalkeeper with excellent distribution and comfort playing out from the back, capable of executing progressive passes and functioning as a quasi-midfielder in pressing systems. His shot-stopping can be erratic on occasion and he's occasionally vulnerable to long-range efforts, but his technical foundation is genuine and he's athletic enough for modern demands. A very good top-flight goalkeeper, if not elite.
