## Snapshot

A right-winger with exceptional technical ability and the kind of innate finishing quality that marks genuinely elite talent; his left-foot strike is effortless and his ability to manipulate space in the box is rare for his age. His weakness is consistency—he's still capable of completely anonymous performances—and defensively he offers almost nothing, but when he's switched on, he's genuinely dangerous.
