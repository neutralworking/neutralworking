## Scouting Notes

A left-back with decent athleticism and a willingness to engage in the press, though his positioning in transition can be unreliable—he's often caught square when Metz lose the ball in midfield. His crossing is functional rather than dangerous; he'll beat a man down the flank but the final delivery rarely troubles the keeper. At 29, he's a serviceable Ligue 1 rotation option without the technical range or reading of space to handle elite wingers consistently.
