## Scouting Notes

A straightforward, no-frills centre-back who reads the game adequately and wins his share of headers, but carries the technical limitations that plague many English defenders stepping up from the Championship. He's competent in possession within 10 yards but uncomfortable with progressive carries or switches, making him a squad rotation piece rather than a long-term solution. Reliable when asked to defend; uninspired when asked to build.
