## Snapshot

M. Mountes is a Terrier archetype, best utilized as a Tuttocampista, blending strong technical attributes with moderate tactical, mental, and physical pillars. He excels in disrupting play with high interceptions, awareness, and duels, combined with surprising guile and take-ons, contributing effectively to both defensive and transitional phases. However, his effectiveness is limited by a lack of vision, poor blocking, and an inability to impact the game from long range. A flamboyant high-press player, he would be a valuable asset for a system that thrives on winning the ball back quickly and launching counter-attacks, but not for a team requiring deep-lying playmaking.
