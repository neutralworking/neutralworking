## Snapshot

Matthew Platt is a quintessential Powerhouse and Traditional CB, whose exceptional physicality and jumping prowess make him dominant in aerial duels and a robust presence at the heart of the defence. He combines this with solid stamina and aggression, allowing him to sustain high-intensity defending. However, his significant lack of close-range agility, poor movement off the ball, and rudimentary technical ability severely restrict his capacity to operate effectively against mobile attackers or contribute to progressive build-up play, pigeonholing him into a very specific Vorstopper role.
