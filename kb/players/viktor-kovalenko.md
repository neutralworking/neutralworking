## Scouting Notes

A Ukrainian midfielder of considerable technical sophistication who operates in the left-half space with genuine dribbling quality and an ability to manipulate defenders through close control. His passing range is excellent and his willingness to take players on gives any team a different profile in midfield. The drawback is his inconsistency in the pressing game—he's a far more effective player when allowed time to operate, and against suffocating defensive structures he can become isolated and ponderous, offering more aesthetics than actual penetration.
