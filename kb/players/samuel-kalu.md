## Scouting Notes

A winger with genuine pace and directness who can stretch defences vertically, but his decision-making in the final third remains frustratingly inconsistent. He operates best in transition, using his acceleration to create space in wide areas, though he lacks the technical refinement to consistently beat defenders with dribbling alone. At 28, still a squad-rotation option rather than a guaranteed starter at this level, he offers functional wide-play depth but won't trouble top-tier defenders with poor touch or timing on crosses.
