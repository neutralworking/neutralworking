## Snapshot

A goalkeeper with good shot-stopping reflexes and a solid physical presence for the position, capable of commanding her area effectively. Van Eijk's limitation is her distribution and comfort with the ball at her feet—in the modern Dutch football environment where the keeper is almost a playmaker from the back, her restricted range of passing and decision-making under pressure marks her out as a more traditional stopper than the progressive presence Ajax demand. She's a serviceable option for domestic football but will find herself constrained at the highest European level.
