## Snapshot

A maestro in autumn, still possessed of that cultured left foot and the spatial awareness that made him a Champions League staple—but at 38, the legs have gone gossamer. He'll find the killer pass in midfield, understands press triggers and half-space positioning as well as anyone in Saudi football, but can't press anymore and will get exposed in transitions by any team with genuine pace. Useful as a metronome in a low-tempo league, but you're paying for the name and the intelligence, not the athleticism.
