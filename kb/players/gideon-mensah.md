## Scouting Notes

A left-back with attractive physical attributes and genuine pace, capable of both shutting down wide attackers and driving forward with intent. His positional awareness can be suspect, however, and he occasionally over-commits, leaving pockets of space behind him. A useful squad player for a mid-table side, but not the sort of player who'll elevate a defence.
