## Scouting Notes

A right-back operating at Ligue 2 level with decent athleticism and positional discipline, though his reading of the game in transition is occasionally naive. He's functional in a defensive shape and can carry the ball forward into the half-space, but he lacks the explosive first step to consistently shut down quick wingers and his crossing is more hopeful than clinical. At 31, he's a dependable rotation option at this level—steady rather than inspiring.
