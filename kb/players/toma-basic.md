## Scouting Notes

A reliable central midfielder with the kind of technical security and positional discipline that keeps a midfield functioning—good passer, capable of breaking lines when required, understands his role in a system. He's the glue player rather than the architect; useful in transitions and tidy on the ball in tight spaces. The limitation is straightforward: he lacks the dynamism or ball-carrying capacity that transforms a midfield from competent to commanding, and he's not someone who'll consistently drive you up the pitch or win you a match with an unexpected moment of brilliance. At 29, he's a solid squad option at a good club, exactly what he is.
