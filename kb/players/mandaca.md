## Scouting Notes

A physically imposing wide midfielder with genuine long-range and set-piece capability, but his tactical awareness lags behind his technical gifts, creating friction in transition and off-the-ball positioning. His composure and take-on prowess mark him as a useful outlet player on the tornante mold—someone who thrives in direct, lower-complexity attacking sequences. Clubs seeking a physical, ball-carrying winger in high-turnover systems will find value, though his lack of vision and occasional lapses in discipline suggest he cannot be trusted as a primary creative engine.
