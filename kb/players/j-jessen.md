## Snapshot

A 22-year-old anchor who combines strong foundational defensive tools—tackling, awareness, interceptions, dueling—with surprising technical competence and take-on ability for a defensive midfielder, suggesting a ball-progressing anchor rather than a pure destroyer. His blocking weakness is notable; he reads play and intercepts cleanly but struggles in direct physical scrambles, which may limit his fit in chaos-heavy pressing systems that demand contest-winning in tight spaces. His high-press trait and structured personality indicate coachability, and for a club seeking a transition-capable anchor with modern passing range, Jessen offers a promising profile; the guile and close-range limitations suggest he is not a creative engine, however.
