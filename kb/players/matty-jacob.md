## Scouting Notes

English fullback showing decent recovery pace and positioning discipline down the flank, capable of genuine defensive contribution without the attacking range modern full-backs typically need. His crosses lack precision and his progressive carrying is minimal, rendering him an asset mainly in transition defence rather than build-up play. He fits the mould of a functional wing-back in a back five more naturally than a modern inverted fullback.
