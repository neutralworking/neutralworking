## Snapshot

A competent MLS-level goalkeeper with solid fundamentals and decent distribution—nothing flashy, nothing broken. Sirois has the tools to be a reliable starter in this league: his positioning is generally sound, his reflexes adequate, and he can play out from the back without embarrassing himself. The ceiling is a steady journeyman; he'll never be the kind of goalkeeper who steals you three points on a Saturday, and his decision-making under pressure—particularly in dealing with cutbacks and crosses—remains a work in progress.
