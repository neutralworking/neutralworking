## Snapshot

An intelligent, left-footed Argentine forward with exceptional movement in the box and a keen reading of where the next pass is going—he functions almost as a second playmaker when Austin are building. His touch is soft, his positioning instinctive, and he's capable of decisive finishes in congested areas. The limitation is straightforward: he lacks genuine elite athleticism or explosive pace, so he'll always need the midfield to do legwork ahead of him, and he can look pedestrian in open space.
