## Snapshot

An inverted attacking midfielder or right winger who operates with genuine technical flair and an appetite for the ball in tight spaces. Buendia's run of form has been patchy, but when he's on, he's a genuine creative threat with an eye for the through-ball and the ability to unlock defences through dribbling. The criticism is straightforward: he can be lightweight in the press and sometimes plays as if the defensive obligation is optional.
