## Snapshot

R. Messali is a Terrier archetype operating as a Holding Midfielder, a Tuttocampista who demonstrates strong technical ability through his excellent awareness, interceptions, and guile in duels, coupled with composure. While his high-press trait points to proactive engagement, his limited mental pillar is underscored by being hot-headed, and his shooting from both close and long range, along with vision, are notable weaknesses. He's a modern interpretation of the deep-lying disruptor, a pivot suited for pressing systems that require a ball-winning presence in central areas, but his lack of creative passing vision means he's not a primary distributor. Toulouse benefits from his defensive solidity, though clubs acquiring him would need to account for his occasional disciplinary lapses and offensive limitations.
