## Scouting Notes

A Colombian centre-back with genuine physical presence and aerial authority, though his reading of the game can be erratic and he sometimes overcommits defensively. His pace is adequate rather than exceptional, which can expose him against properly mobile forwards making inverted runs. He's a useful asset who brings intensity but needs stability around him.
