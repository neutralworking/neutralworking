## Snapshot

Dutch right-back with encouraging technical security and willingness to carry the ball into the half-space, displaying composure in possession and a decent range of short and medium passes. His athleticism is solid if unspectacular, and he can struggle against truly explosive wingers; his defensive positioning can also be slack in transition. Young enough to develop further, but currently a very good depth option rather than a long-term cornerstone.
