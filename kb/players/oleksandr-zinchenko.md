## Scouting Notes

A left-back who genuinely wants to play as a midfielder and has the technical range to justify that ambition—his passing range and progressivity are genuine assets in transitions. However, he's occasionally naïve defensively and can be exposed against direct, pacey opponents if he's caught ball-watching or overcommitting. For Forest, he's a luxury defender; you need the architecture around him to work, or he becomes a liability.
