## Scouting Notes

R. Fofana is a Marksman archetype, a Complete Striker who shines as a Poacher due to his strong technical and tactical pillars, particularly excelling in close and long-range finishing, guile, and aerial duels with good discipline. Despite these attacking strengths, his mental pillar is limited, and his creativity, vision, and mid-range shooting are notable weaknesses, positioning him as a primarily direct, goal-focused forward. He represents the classical "number 9" tradition, thriving in systems that get the ball into dangerous areas quickly, allowing him to focus on clinical finishing rather than extensive link-up play or creative build-up. Lens benefits from his direct goal threat, and clubs seeking a reliable, disciplined finisher who prioritizes scoring over intricate playmaking would find value, though his lack of broader attacking creativity might require a supportive creative midfield.
