## Snapshot

Silky progressive passer with excellent close control and the ability to operate in narrow spaces, but his decision-making under pressure can be reckless and he lacks the defensive nous required for a top-six midfield role. He's a gifted technician still learning when to keep rather than play, best suited to a more structured role than Dortmund's current demands.
