## Snapshot

A Swiss wide forward of considerable technical quality who operates with intelligence in the half-spaces and drives through-balls with precision. Reuteler's press is tireless and her close-control dribbling in the final third is genuinely sophisticated, but she can be frustratingly predictable when space is congested, tending to retreat rather than commit a defender with an incisive final touch. She's a very good squad player who occasionally demonstrates borderline excellent form.
