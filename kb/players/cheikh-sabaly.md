## Snapshot

Centre-back with adequate reading of the game and a willingness to step into midfield to intercept. His physical recovery pace is moderate, and he can be exploited by quick, technical forwards making runs in behind; his passing range is conservative and rarely influences play in the attacking third. Competent Ligue 1 defender for a lower-table side, but without the athleticism or technical ambition for a top-six club.
