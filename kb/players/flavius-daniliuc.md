Left-sided centre-back with the technical range to play out from the back and enough athleticism to recover when things go sideways. He's progressive in possession and comfortable in a back three, but his positional reading in transition can be loose—he'll occasionally buy a dummy or lean the wrong way when a striker times a run well. Fine for a Swiss Super League title contender, but you'd be nervous about his concentration in a genuinely high-pressing European environment.
