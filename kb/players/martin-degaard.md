## Scouting Notes

Arsenal's talisman and arguably the Premier League's most complete number ten — he's extraordinarily progressive with the ball, capable of elite dribbling or the pass that opens a defence entirely, and his intelligence off the ball is genuinely exceptional. He's both the playmaker and occasional goalscorer depending on what the phase demands. The only limitation is that he can occasionally be dispossessed in crowded areas and his goal output doesn't always match his underlying quality, but he's a legitimate world-class operator.
