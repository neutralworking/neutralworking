## Snapshot

A workmanlike central midfielder operating in the Spanish fourth tier, Luna offers positional discipline and a willingness to cover ground, but lacks the technical range or press resistance to suggest top-flight readiness. He's functional as a shield in front of a back four and moves the ball simply to either flank, yet struggles to progress play through the half-spaces or execute the kinds of forward passes that distinguish players operating at higher levels. At 26 with limited evidence of evolution, he's a reliable depth option for a mid-table Segunda División outfit—no more than that.
