## Snapshot

Quick winger who can create moments of genuine danger in the final third, with lovely footwork in confined spaces. His ability to shift the ball and accelerate away from defenders remains a legitimate threat. Defensively, he's a passenger; his positioning is poor and he won't track runners, which makes him a luxury option only in sides comfortable controlling possession.
