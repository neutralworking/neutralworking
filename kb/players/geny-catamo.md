## Career Highlights

Catamo began his football journey in Mozambique, training at CD Maxaquene and the Black Bulls youth academy before moving to Portugal in 2018 to play for Amora FC. His performances there attracted the attention of Sporting CP, which he joined in 2019, signing his first professional contract in September 2020 after a period in the club's youth academy. He made his senior team debut for Sporting CP on December 29, 2021.

Following loan spells at Vitória Guimarães in 2022 and Marítimo from 2022 to 2023, Catamo returned to Sporting CP and signed a new five-year contract on December 22, 2023, which includes a €60 million release clause. He has been a part of several title-winning campaigns with Sporting CP, including two Primeira Liga titles (2023/2024, 2024/2025), one Taça de Portugal (2024/2025), and the League Cup (2021/2022). Internationally, Catamo debuted for Mozambique on September 10, 2019, scoring in his first senior appearance, and has since accumulated 43 caps with 14 goals as of January 5, 2026.
