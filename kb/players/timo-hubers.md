## Scouting Notes

A composed, left-sided centre-back with excellent reading of the game and the ability to play progressive passes into midfield, making him one of Köln's more valuable defensive outlets in possession. He's rarely flustered and positions himself intelligently, but he lacks the physical dominance or explosive athleticism to be a genuine top-four calibre defender, and he can be caught out by direct runners if overcommitted to playmaking. He's a solid, intelligent Bundesliga stalwart who elevates a mid-table side through positioning rather than dominance.
