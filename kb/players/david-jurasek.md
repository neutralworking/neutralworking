## Snapshot

A left winger with real elasticity to his game—he can play inverted or on the touchline, and he's got the dribbling range and directness to hurt teams in the half-spaces. His delivery is improving and he does the defensive work without it feeling like a chore, which counts for something, but he's not yet consistent enough in his decision-making to be trusted as a starter at a top-six side. A genuine prospect who needs to marry his talent with discipline over a sustained run.
