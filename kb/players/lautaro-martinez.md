## Snapshot

A complete centre-forward at his peak, capable of both leading the line physically and operating deep to link play, with genuine athleticism and a keen finishing instinct. He's press-resistant, capable of turning in tight spaces, and has developed into a proper nine rather than an inverted option. His weakness is that he can be isolated if Inter lose midfield control, and his output will naturally fluctuate depending on service—he's world-class but not generational.
