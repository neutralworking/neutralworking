## Snapshot

A functional centre-back who has become Heidenheim's reliable defensive anchor in their unlikely Bundesliga survival campaign. Keller reads the game with intelligence—positioning over athleticism—and uses his body well in duels, though his lack of genuine pace means he'll be exposed against the league's quickest forwards if caught out of position. His distribution from the back is competent rather than transformative; he'll play the simple pass and rarely attempts the ambitious progressive carry that modern top-six clubs demand.
