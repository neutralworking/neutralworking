A versatile midfielder-cum-defender with excellent reading of the game and positional discipline that allows him to screen effectively without fouling excessively. He's left-footed, comfortable on the ball, and can play in multiple roles without complaint. At 32, though, his legs have lost the snap—he can't press with conviction anymore and relies entirely on anticipation, which is a diminishing asset.
