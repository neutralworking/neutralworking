Career Highlights

Éverton Ribeiro boasts an illustrious career marked by numerous domestic and continental titles. He is a two-time Copa Libertadores winner with CR Flamengo (2019, 2022) and a four-time Brazilian Champion, winning twice with Cruzeiro (2013, 2014) and twice with Flamengo (2019, 2020). His trophy cabinet also includes the Recopa Sudamericana (2020), Copa do Brasil (2022), and two Supercopa do Brasil titles (2020, 2021) with Flamengo.

Individually, Ribeiro was recognized as the Player of the Year in the Campeonato Brasileiro Série A for two consecutive seasons (2012/13, 2013/14). On the international stage, he has represented Brazil at the Copa América in 2015 and 2021, and was part of the squad for the 2022 FIFA World Cup. More recently, he has added the Copa do Nordeste (2025) and two Campeonato Baiano titles (2025, 2026) to his list of achievements with Bahia.
