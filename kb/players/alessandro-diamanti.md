## Snapshot

"Nicknamed ""Alino""", Diamanti is a dynamic, creative, and technically gifted left-footed playmaker, who usually plays as an attacking midfielder; he has also been deployed as a winger or as a supporting striker on occasion, due to his ability to both create chances for team-mates, and score goals with his accurate striking ability from outside the area. He is predominantly known for his vision, Press Resistant, crossing, control, and his accuracy on penalties, as well as his curling set-pieces.	

At club level, he has previously played for Prato, Empoli, Fucecchio, Florentia, AlbinoLeffe, Livorno, West Ham United, Brescia, Bologna, Fiorentina and Watford.

At international level, he represented Italy at Euro 2012, winning a runners-up medal, and at the 2013 Confederations Cup, winning a bronze medal."
