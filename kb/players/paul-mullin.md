## Snapshot

Striker who's built a tidy career as a lower-league goalscorer through intelligent positioning and a knack for being in the right place; his movement in tight areas is genuinely clever, and he converts the chances Morecambe creates. He lacks genuine pace to trouble a high defensive line, and his hold-up play is ordinary, but as a poacher at this level, he delivers. A proper League Two finisher.
