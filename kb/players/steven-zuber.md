## Scouting Notes

A left winger operating in the Greek Super League whose best days are firmly in the rear-view mirror. Zuber remains a functional, intelligent mover in wide areas with reasonable ball retention and an understanding of positioning, but at 34 he lacks the explosiveness and one-v-one penetration that made him useful for Basel and Wolfsburg. His left foot is still competent for cutbacks and diagonal passes into the half-space, yet he'll struggle to consistently beat defenders at this level and offers minimal defensive intensity—a squad rotation option masquerading as a starter, living on European pedigree rather than present contribution.
