A left winger operating in the Greek Super League whose best days are firmly in the rear-view mirror. His left foot is still competent for cutbacks and diagonal passes into the half-space, yet he'll struggle to consistently beat defenders at this level and offers minimal defensive intensity—a squad rotation option masquerading as a starter, living on European pedigree rather than present contribution.
