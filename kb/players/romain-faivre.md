## Snapshot

A technically capable left-sided midfielder who has never quite nailed down a consistent role at the highest level, despite genuine ability on the ball. He operates best in pockets of space rather than in transition, showing competence with his first touch and a willingness to receive in congested areas, but his lack of explosive acceleration means he often requires three or four touches to create separation from defenders. His left foot is his stronger weapon—he can drift inside from the flank and pick a progressive pass—yet his defensive workrate remains fitful and his body language can suggest resignation when the press is on. At 27, this feels like his level: a useful squad option at a Ligue 1 club, occasionally influential when the game opens up, but lacking the pace, physicality, or off-ball intelligence to be a reliable starter in Europe's elite competition.
