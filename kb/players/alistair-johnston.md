## Snapshot

A genuinely modern fullback who can play inverted into the half-space and progress the ball through short, purposeful carries—Johnston's intelligence off the ball is his real asset, anticipating danger before it arrives. His weakness is explosive athleticism; he won't recover from positional mistakes with a 40-yard sprint, which matters when Celtic face elite left-sided threats in Europe. He's a good, thinking player at a top-six club, not a transcendent one.
