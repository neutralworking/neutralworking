Seasoned centre-forward in his late twenties who knows how to hold the ball up and bring teammates into play through his physical presence. Limited in terms of pace and explosive movement, so he's almost entirely dependent on service and must be perfectly set up to score. Works hard but is ultimately a depth option, someone who'll get you 8-10 goals a season if used correctly.
