## Snapshot

English midfielder with legitimate technical ability in tight spaces and an eye for a vertical pass through the lines. He's a press-resistant option in transition and moves the ball with purpose rather than panic. The real question isn't what he can do on the ball—it's whether he has the positional discipline and reading to shield a back four at this level, and frankly, the evidence at Hull suggests he doesn't quite yet. Could develop into a solid Championship regular, but he's a ways from being a Premier League corner-stone.
