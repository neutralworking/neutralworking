## Scouting Notes

Craque

Incredible talent. Wants the ball in tough times. Ronaldo. Messi. Neymar. Beckham. Not captains but leaders by example.

Figo and Ronaldo are very similar in terms of career and talent/style. Before that you have Paulo Futre.

They need to feel valued for what they give.

They care what the fans think. They love the fans and the recognition, the records, the titles, the accolades, everything.

Real Madrid in its current guise owes partially to a lie told by Futre to Perez about Figo being interested in the transfer if he could get 10m commission.

Perez increased his commission offer to 6m and the transfer was in motion.

Sums up modern football quite nicely.

Perez just wants to win. Not at any other club. Just at Real Madrid. He has been a socio/member since birth. It is his destiny.

His signings and the transfer policy provide an insight into the 'Win at All Costs' recruitment mentality. He wants to make maximum money, throw money at winning and do it until nobody else every wins again.  

Definitely look into this.  

I want that weird old kit with Figo on it.
