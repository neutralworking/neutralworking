## Snapshot

A right-sided forward in his early thirties with intelligent movement in the half-spaces and a genuine goal-threat from open play, still showing the technical quality that made him a reliable Europe-standard player. However, he's operating in a diminished physical state—neither quick enough to run in behind nor explosive enough to create separation in tight spaces anymore. He'll find pockets and finish chances in the Jordan Premier League, but he's a cautionary tale of how quickly elite athleticism becomes mandatory once the mind alone isn't sufficient.
