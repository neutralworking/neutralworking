## Snapshot

A dependable centre-back who reads the game well and positions himself intelligently in the Championship's lower reaches, but lacks the athleticism and ball-playing range to function in a genuinely progressive side. His strength is in organization and communication—he's a leader in the dressing room—but he's increasingly reliant on positioning rather than pace, which exposes him when faced with direct, quick attacking play. At 32, he's a steady hand for a mid-table outfit, nothing more.
