## Snapshot

Robust defender comfortable on either flank, with decent positioning and a stubborn physical approach to defending. His passing range is notably limited—most attempts are sideways or backwards—and he will never be asked to initiate play from deep. Serviceable in a back three or five where his role is simply to win contests and be difficult, but entirely out of his depth in possession-dominant setups or against high-pressing teams.
