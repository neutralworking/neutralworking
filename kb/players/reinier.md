## Scouting Notes

Brazilian attacking midfielder with lovely technical quality and genuine creativity from the half-spaces or as an inverted winger. Intelligent movement off the ball and decent progressive passing, but he's not consistently decisive in the final third and can go missing in duller stretches of games. Top-half performance in Serie A but not elite.
