## Career Highlights

Gabriel Magalhães began his professional career at Avaí in 2016 before moving to Lille in 2017. During his time in France, he had loan spells with Troyes and Dinamo Zagreb, where he notably won a league and cup double in the 2017-18 season. He joined Arsenal in September 2020 for £27 million, making an immediate impact by scoring on his debut in a 3-0 victory against Fulham.

His early performances at Arsenal earned him multiple Player of the Month awards in late 2020. Gabriel signed a new long-term contract with Arsenal in October 2022, and again in June 2025, securing his future with the club. He made his senior international debut for Brazil in 2023 and scored his first international goal during the 2026 FIFA World Cup qualification campaign. He was also part of Brazil's squad for the 2024 Copa America in the USA. To date, Gabriel has scored 20 Premier League goals for Arsenal and won the English FA Community Shield with the club in the 2023-24 season.
