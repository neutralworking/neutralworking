## Scouting Notes

A left-winger who combines genuine pace with the ability to cut inside and operate in the half-space, where he's more dangerous than on the touchline. His crossing is adequate rather than exceptional, and defensively he can be exposed against aggressive full-backs, but his willingness to drive forward and his close control make him a useful attacking option. A fine complementary player for a side that builds around him being managed carefully defensively.
