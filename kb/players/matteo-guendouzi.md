## Snapshot

A ball-carrying midfielder with genuine press resistance and the ability to progress play through dribbling or incisive short passes. His intensity off the ball is excellent, and he's improved his decision-making considerably since his early career; he can operate box-to-box and isn't solely reliant on having a shield in front of him. The limitation is his occasional loss of composure when closed down under pressure, but he's a very good player for Lazio's system.
