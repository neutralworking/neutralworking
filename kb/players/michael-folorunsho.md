A box-to-box midfielder with admirable intensity and a willingness to cover ground defensively, providing genuine work-rate and press resistance that steadies a side. His passing is generally smart without being creative, and he can drive the ball forward when space allows, though he's not a playmaker. Exactly the kind of reliable, unspectacular midfielder who enables more talented players to function.
