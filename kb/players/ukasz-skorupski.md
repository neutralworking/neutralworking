## Snapshot

A reliable, composed goalkeeper who makes himself big and rarely concedes soft goals; his decision-making on when to leave his line is generally sound, and he commands his area with decent presence. He is not exceptional with the ball at his feet—his distribution, while competent, does not enable progressive play—and his shot-stopping, while good, is occasionally vulnerable to accurate shooting at range. A very good, understated presence.
