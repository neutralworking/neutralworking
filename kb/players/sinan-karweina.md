A serviceable depth centre-forward who operates with decent work rate but lacks the technical range or positional instinct to be a reliable starter at Bundesliga standard. He's physical enough and can hold the ball up adequately, but he doesn't progress play effectively through progressive carries or creation, and his movement off the ball is predictable. For a promotion-chasing side he's a useful squad option; as a primary number nine, he's limited.
