Recent Form

Jan Paul van Hecke has been a crucial component for Brighton & Hove Albion during the 2025/2026 Premier League season, featuring in 34 appearances across all competitions, including 29 league matches. He has contributed offensively with three goals and two assists, while also being part of eight clean sheets for his side. His performance in March 2026 earned him the highest monthly player rating of the season, indicating strong recent form.

Throughout the current season, Van Hecke has demonstrated his influence in Brighton's build-up play, averaging 13.44 final third passes attempted per 90 minutes, with 8.93 completed, ranking him fourth and eighth respectively among Premier League central defenders. Defensively, he averages 2.93 aerial duels won per 90, alongside 3.23 headed clearances and 5.26 total clearances per 90 minutes. His consistent performances have led to speculation about his future, with his contract nearing its final year and top clubs reportedly showing interest.

Tactical Profile

Jan Paul van Hecke operates as a proactive, front-foot central defender, known for his aggressive style and comfort on the ball, aligning with his internal archetype as a "Conductor". He excels in Brighton's system, which often involves building from the back with both central defenders and the goalkeeper, supported by a double pivot. His elite on-ball qualities include a wide passing range, enabling him to break lines, fire passes into pockets, and switch play effectively, making it difficult for opponents to anticipate his actions. He is adept at baiting pressure and playing through to create advantages, a key strategy for ball-playing teams.

Defensively, Van Hecke's internal role as a "Sweeper" is evident in his ability to read the game and position himself to intercept passes and thwart attacks. He is solid in his own box, scanning space, tracking runners, and timing his tackles and blocks well. Standing at 189 cm, he is strong in aerial duels, reading the flight of the ball to win 50/50 challenges both inside and outside the box. Despite not being the fastest in wide areas, his game reading and positioning allow him to cover full-backs and make recovery challenges. He is composed under pressure, willing to receive the ball and make intelligent decisions, whether playing a simple pass or dribbling out of defense.

Career Highlights

Van Hecke began his senior career with NAC Breda, making his professional debut in August 2019. He joined Brighton & Hove Albion in September 2020 and subsequently had loan spells with Heerenveen in the Eredivisie and Blackburn Rovers in the Championship. During his time at Blackburn Rovers in the 2021-22 season, he was named the club's Player of the Season, becoming the first loanee to achieve this accolade.

His breakthrough at Brighton saw him make his Premier League debut in August 2022 and his first league start in January 2023. A notable performance included keeping Erling Haaland goalless in a 1-1 draw against Manchester City in May 2023, which helped Brighton secure a spot in the 2023-24 UEFA Europa League. In the 2024/2025 season, Van Hecke's strong performances were recognized as he won both the Brighton Player of the Season and Players' Player of the Season awards. He made his 100th appearance for Brighton in a 2-1 home win against Newcastle United in October 2025. On the international stage, he has represented the Netherlands at U21 level and earned his first senior cap in September 2024, accumulating seven senior caps by October 2025.
