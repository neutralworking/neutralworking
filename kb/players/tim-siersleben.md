## Snapshot

A willing, hard-working centre-back who represents functional Bundesliga depth rather than long-term cornerstone material. Siersleben is aerially decent and committed in the challenge, but he's neither quick enough to cover space dynamically nor comfortable enough on the ball to initiate attacks from the back. In a defensive-minded setup where he's asked to be steady rather than spectacular, he'll do the job; ask him to step into the half-space or carry the ball forward and you'll quickly find his limitations.
