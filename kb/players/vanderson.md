## Snapshot

Right-back with electric pace and athleticism, dangerous in transitions both offensively and defensively, capable of affecting play across the width. His positioning and tactical discipline remain works in progress and he can be caught too high or too narrow, but his raw recovery pace and ability to burst forward compensate. A genuinely exciting attacking fullback.
