## Snapshot

A robust right-back with surprising attacking ambition for a player of his vintage; he's positionally sound and rarely gets turned inside-out on the flank. His pace has diminished noticeably, and he struggles when asked to cover ground laterally against fleet-footed wingers. A reliable, unglamorous presence in a structured formation, ideal for a team that doesn't ask their fullbacks to be primary creators.
