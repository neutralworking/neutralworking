## Snapshot

A centre-back who reads the game reasonably well and isn't afraid to step into midfield to break up play, fitting the modern profile of a ball-playing defender. However, his physical limitations—neither particularly quick nor imposing aerially—mean he can be exposed against direct, athletic strikers, and he sometimes overthinks his positioning when pressed. A reliable Ligue 1 rotation option, but the kind of player whose mistakes tend to compound because he lacks the recovery pace to compensate.
