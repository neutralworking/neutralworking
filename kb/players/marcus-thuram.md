Marcus Thuram is a physical specimen as a Complete Forward, using his jumping ability and pace to terrorize defenders, but his tactical limitations can lead to him being a bystander in build-up phases. His lineage can be traced back to traditional French strikers who thrive off direct play. A club that focuses on quick transitions and counter-attacks would benefit from Thuram's skills, but his inability to press and block effectively may hinder teams that rely on intense pressing.
