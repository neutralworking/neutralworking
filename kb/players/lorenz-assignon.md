## Scouting Notes

A right-back with excellent defensive positioning and the athleticism to recover quickly if caught out of shape; he's comfortable in one-v-one situations and doesn't shy from the physical battle. Going forward, he's competent but uninspired—his crossing is accurate rather than creative, and he rarely beats a winger on the outside. A very solid defender, but not a wing-back or someone creating from deep.
