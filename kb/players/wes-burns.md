## Snapshot

A right-winger who genuinely wants to impact games and will run himself ragged trying to make things happen, but his decision-making in the final third is poor and his crossing is wayward more often than not. Burns has pace and willingness, which are admirable, but he lacks the technical security to operate at Premier League level consistently. He's a useful reserve option for a struggling side, someone who can change the energy of a game through sheer effort rather than craft.
