## Snapshot

Argentine winger with excellent technical security on the ball and a preference for inverted positions where he can curl shots with his right foot or execute quick combinations; his dribbling is genuinely fluid and his press-resistance strong. He's slight in stature and can be overwhelmed in transitions by robust full-backs, and his crossing is pedestrian since he rarely operates on the flank proper. An inverted winger first and foremost, not a traditional wide player.
