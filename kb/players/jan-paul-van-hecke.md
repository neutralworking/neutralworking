## Career Highlights

Van Hecke began his senior career with NAC Breda, making his professional debut in August 2019. He joined Brighton & Hove Albion in September 2020 and subsequently had loan spells with Heerenveen in the Eredivisie and Blackburn Rovers in the Championship. During his time at Blackburn Rovers in the 2021-22 season, he was named the club's Player of the Season, becoming the first loanee to achieve this accolade.

His breakthrough at Brighton saw him make his Premier League debut in August 2022 and his first league start in January 2023. In the 2024/2025 season, Van Hecke's strong performances were recognized as he won both the Brighton Player of the Season and Players' Player of the Season awards. He made his 100th appearance for Brighton in a 2-1 home win against Newcastle United in October 2025.
