## Snapshot

Ramzi Solan is a raw, athletic centre-forward who excels at making progressive carries down the half-spaces, utilizing his exceptional acceleration and pace to leave defenders in his wake. However, his lack of guile and creativity in the final third often leads to predictable, one-dimensional attacking play, and his woeful discipline is a significant concern. Despite his ability to shield the ball effectively, Solan's overall game is hindered by his impulsive decision-making and tendency to neglect his defensive responsibilities.
