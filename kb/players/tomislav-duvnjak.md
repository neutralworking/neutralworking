## Scouting Notes


ClubDinamo Zagreb

PositionMidfielder

Born5 Febuary 2003

“It takes a lot of blood, sweat and tears to beome a pro. I am ready to do anything for football,” Duvnjak once told Jutarnji list. “I play football because I like it and not to make millions, drive expensive cars or be with the most beautiful women.” This diminutive (1.72 m) but very mature and strong-charactered boy has captained the Dinamo and Croatia youth teams, is very good on the ball and copes well with pressing, constantly making the right decisions at the right time. He’s a No 6, a defensive/central midfielder who tends to play his way out of trouble and assume a pivotal role in the buildup. Currently with Dinamo’s reserves in the second division, he has already attracted attention of several Bundesliga clubs, as well as Juventus and others. **[Aleksandar Holiga](https://twitter.com/AlexHoliga)**  
  
**October 2021 update** With Dinamo II in the Croatian second tier and is not being considered for the first team squad at the moment.  
  
**September 2022 update** Made a promising start of the season on loan at the league’s also-rans Istra but now a serious injury will keep him off the pitch for months.  
  
**October 2023 update** Injuries have hampered his development and he was unable to establish himself with Istra, so he was sent on loan to Sesvete in the second division. He still has the right attitude though, ready to fight his way back through hard work.
