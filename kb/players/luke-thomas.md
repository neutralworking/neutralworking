## Scouting Notes

Left-back with reasonable attacking thrust and the pace to recover defensively; he understands modern full-back principles and can drive down the line with purpose. His positioning against inverted wingers can be loose and he's occasionally caught out on the turn by quicker opponents. He's a dependable rotation left-back who would benefit from consistency but lacks the technical range to be a creators from deep.
