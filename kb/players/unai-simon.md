## Snapshot

A modern goalkeeper with crisp handling and the footwork to act as a legitimate outlet in Bilbao's build-up; he sweeps intelligently and distributes with enough precision that his presence genuinely shapes how the team constructs attacks. The trade-off is that he can be exposed against aerial bombardment and occasionally errs on the side of bravado when claiming crosses in crowded boxes—sometimes you want him smaller, less convinced he's a sweeper. Still, he's comfortably a top-tier La Liga keeper and has performed well enough in European competition to warrant a 'very good' bracket.
