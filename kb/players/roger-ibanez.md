## Scouting Notes

A physically imposing center-back with excellent recovery pace and genuine leadership presence, though his positioning is sometimes reactive rather than proactive and he can be susceptible to technical forwards who move him laterally. Strong in the air and committed in the challenge, but his distribution under press is a weakness—he'll boot it long rather than play out of it, which limits Al-Ahli's ability to build from the back.
