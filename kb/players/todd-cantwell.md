## Scouting Notes

A slick, press-resistant central midfielder with neat feet and the ability to progress the ball in short, intelligent sequences, ideally suited to teams that want to control possession through the middle. He's naturally cautious with his passes—rarely risky, rarely expansive—and while that keeps turnovers down, it also means he doesn't break lines or create chances with the frequency a higher-level club demands. He's the kind of midfielder you're comfortable with on the ball but also the kind you won't build a title challenge around, decent depth rather than a cornerstone piece.
