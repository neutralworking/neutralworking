## Snapshot

Willian is a dynamic wide forward who excels at making progressive carries into the half-spaces, often beating his marker with quick changes of direction. He offers a unique ability to operate in tight areas, using his low centre of gravity to evade tackles and create space for himself or others. However, his tendency to overplay in the final third can sometimes lead to turnovers, and he can be guilty of neglecting his defensive responsibilities when the opposition has possession.
