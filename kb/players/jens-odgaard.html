Snapshot

A forward who operates with genuine work rate and can function across the frontline as either a nine or a ten; he presses intelligently and his movement to create space for others is underrated. A useful, selfless operator rather than a talismanic striker.

Jens Odgaard is a 27-year-old Danish center forward who plays for Bologna, known internally as an "Assassin" and "Prima Punta." While his preferred foot is listed as right, some public reports indicate he is left-footed. He stands at 188 cm.

Recent Form

In the 2025/2026 Serie A season, Jens Odgaard has scored 5 goals and provided 1 assist across 23 matches, accumulating 1,249 minutes on the pitch, with an average FotMob rating of 6.77. Other reports indicate 7 goals and 2 assists in 35 matches played, totaling 1863 minutes for the 2025/2026 season. He registered a goal in Bologna's 1-0 victory against Pisa on March 2, 2026, earning a FotMob rating of 7.3. Earlier in the season, around late 2025, he scored in a 3-2 win against Verona and a 1-1 draw against Lazio, contributing significantly with three goal contributions in three consecutive starts around October 2025. His usage has been inconsistent in 2026, and he returned from a knee contusion in late October 2025, where his impact was noted as less significant in a 0-0 draw against Torino.

Tactical Profile

Odgaard is primarily deployed as a center forward, but he also features as an attacking midfielder (center or right). His playing style is characterized by a strong long shot, and he tends to cut inside, play short passes, and pose a counter-attack threat. He is also noted for liking to dribble and frequently drawing fouls. While he excels in offensive aspects, his passing and ability to hold onto the ball are considered weaknesses. His FC 26 ratings highlight his shooting ability with 76 for positioning, 80 for shot power, and 76 for long shots, alongside a physicality rating of 76, including 83 for jumping and 79 for stamina.

Career Highlights

Jens Odgaard has accumulated several titles throughout his career, including the Coppa Italia in the 2024/2025 season with Bologna. During his youth career with Inter U19, he secured the Super Cup Primavera, Campionato Primavera 1, and the Youth Viareggio Cup, all in the 2017/2018 season. He also won the 1. Division with Lyngby in the 2015/2016 season. Odgaard made his senior debut for Lyngby at the age of 16, becoming the youngest player to appear in the Danish Superliga for the club at 17 years and 115 days. His career has seen him compete in various top European leagues, including Serie A, Coppa Italia, Europa League, Champions League, and the Eredivisie.

Condition / Medical History

Jens Odgaard has experienced several injuries throughout his career. In late 2025, he sustained a knee injury, returning to play in late October, and also dealt with a mild rectus femoris strain (thigh problem) that sidelined him for two to three weeks around the same period. Other recent issues include a groin injury in May 2025 and a knee injury in October 2025. In March 2025, an unusual mark on his neck during a match led to public speculation, which Odgaard clarified was due to medicine he was using, not a serious injury.
