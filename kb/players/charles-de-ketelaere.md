During his time there, he won the Belgian Jupiler Pro League three consecutive seasons and secured two Belgian Supercups. His individual accolades include being named Belgian Promising Talent of the Year in 2020 and Belgium's Best Young Player of the Year in 2021. Notably, he scored a 93rd-minute game-winning goal against Zenit St. Petersburg in the 2020-21 UEFA Champions League group stage.

In 2022, he transferred to AC Milan in a move that represented one of the club's top transfer fees. Following a loan spell, Atalanta made his move permanent on June 15, 2024. His first full season with Atalanta (2023-2024) was highly productive, as he recorded 14 goals and 11 assists across all competitions. On the international stage, De Ketelaere debuted for the Belgian senior squad in November 2020 and scored his first international goal against Italy in October 2021.
