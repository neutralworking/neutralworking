## Scouting Notes

A combative, ball-winning midfielder who thrives in transition and pressing triggers but lacks the technical range to progress play consistently through the thirds. His engine is genuine and his positioning in the middle third is intelligent, but he's a functional rather than creative operator—useful in a scrap, limited in build-up. At Luton's level he's a dependable starter, though you'd hesitate to ask him to dictate tempo against a superior side.
