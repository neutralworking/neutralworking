## Scouting Notes

A territorial defender with good reading of the game and the physicality to enforce his will in the box, but his pace is genuinely suspect and he's not the type to play a high defensive line comfortably. He'll be sound in a back three or in a deeper, more conservative setup where he can sit and react, but he can get exposed if PSV plays a pressing game against genuinely sharp attackers. Reliable enough as a squad piece at this age, but no longer a starter for a title challenger.
