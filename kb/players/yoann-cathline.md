## Snapshot

A left-sided attacker with genuine intensity in the press and an appetite for direct, aggressive running that can unbalance lower-division defences. Cathline moves well with the ball at pace and understands how to create separation with timing rather than touch, though his final product remains inconsistent—he'll beat a man but the cross is often panicked or under-weighted. The problem is straightforward: he's a functional pace winger in the Ligue 2/Eredivisie ecosystem, lacking the positional intelligence, spatial awareness, or composure in transition that would make him reliable at a serious level. He's still developing as a footballer, not yet a finished article.
