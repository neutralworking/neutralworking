Tactical Profile

Hugo Larsson operates primarily as a central midfielder but is also adept in a defensive midfield role, showcasing versatility in his play. He is recognized for his ability to progress the ball, win possession, and effectively transition from defense to attack. His playing style is characterized by strong ball-carrying capabilities and a relentless work rate across the full 90 minutes, making him an archetypal box-to-box midfielder.

Larsson is a technically gifted and tactically intelligent player, noted for his composure and control in high-pressure situations. His passing accuracy is high, with an 88% success rate in the Bundesliga, and he possesses a wide passing range, capable of delivering incisive through balls and switching play with ease. Defensively, he averages 1.2 tackles and 1.3 interceptions per 90 minutes, utilizing his 187 cm frame and mobility to make crucial interventions. While strong in ball progression and distribution, areas for refinement include aerial duels and consistent excellence in ground duels.

Career Highlights

Larsson began his senior career at Malmö FF, making his debut on February 20, 2022. During his time there, he won the Svenska Cupen in the 2021/2022 season and was recognized as the Allsvenskan Young Player of the Year in 2022. He gained experience in European competitions, participating in UEFA Champions League and Europa League qualifiers and group stages with Malmö.

In May 2023, Larsson signed with Eintracht Frankfurt for a record fee for a Swedish club, officially joining in July 2023. He quickly established himself, scoring his first Bundesliga goal on October 8, 2023, and notably netting against Bayern Munich in a 5-1 victory on December 9, 2023. As of April 11, 2026, he has made 84 domestic league appearances for Eintracht Frankfurt, scoring 6 goals. Larsson also made his full international debut for Sweden on January 9, 2023, and has since accumulated 12 caps for the national team.
