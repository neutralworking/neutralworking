## Career Highlights

Dreyer's career is marked by several significant achievements across different leagues. He was the top goal scorer in the 2017-18 Danish 1st Division with 18 goals, which helped Esbjerg fB achieve promotion to the Superliga. After a move to Rubin Kazan, he made an immediate impact by scoring a hat-trick on his debut in September 2021, a first in the league's history.

He has secured two major titles with FC Midtjylland: the Danish Superliga in the 2019/2020 season and the DBU Pokalen (Danish Cup) in 2021/2022. His individual accolades include being named Danish Superliga Player of the Month in February 2020 and Anderlecht Player of the Season for 2023–24. On the international stage, Dreyer was part of Denmark's squad for UEFA Euro 2024 and made his senior national team debut in November 2021. His move to San Diego FC in 2025 saw him become the MLS Newcomer of the Year, an MLS All-Star, and part of the MLS Best XI, contributing to San Diego FC setting new MLS records for points and wins by an expansion team.
