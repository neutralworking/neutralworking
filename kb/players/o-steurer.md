## Scouting Notes

A methodical connector built for the unglamorous work of breaking play and recycling possession in structured systems. Steurer's reading of space and positional discipline give him genuine control in the middle, though his limited range—both in vision and long-range execution—constrains his ability to progress play beyond simple lateral switches. At 31, he remains a valuable utility player for sides needing a technical floor in midfield, but his archetype has a natural ceiling: he cannot unlock defenses or dictate tempo, only defend it. Best deployed in a possession-heavy system where his composure and blocking work becomes a platform for more imaginative teammates.
