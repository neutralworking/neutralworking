## Scouting Notes

Young striker with decent athleticism and the positioning sense you'd want at his stage, but he's still developing his all-around game—hold-up play is functional rather than refined, and his touch in crowded areas isn't yet reliable. He's had fleeting chances at Villa and looked like a prospect, not a finished product. The pathway forward is there, but he needs consistent playing time somewhere to mature, and right now he's a prospect, not a contributor.
