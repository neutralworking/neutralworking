## Scouting Notes

A left-back recruited from Lorient, Kapuadi has the athleticism and recovery pace to play at Serie A tempo, but his positioning remains loose and his decision-making in the defensive third inconsistent. He's most comfortable when asked to simply get forward and use his frame—he's a capable crosser from deep and doesn't shirk the physical side—but Inter's defensive structure demands more sophistication than he currently offers, and against elite wide forwards he can be caught upfield or sloppy on the turn. He's a squad depth option with European pedigree, not a long-term solution to a problem Inter needed solving.
