## Snapshot

A centre-forward of unusual intelligence and movement intelligence, capable of both traditional 9 work and dropping deep to link play through the half-spaces. His off-ball running is exceptional; he creates angles and drags defenders around. The limitation is brutal finishing—he misses sitters regularly and doesn't have the goal-per-chance ratio you'd demand of a elite centre-forward. Still, he's a thorough, complete striker that any team benefits from having.
