## Snapshot

A left-footed centre-back with excellent ball-playing ability and the composure to invite press and play out under it; he's graceful on the ball and reads space well ahead of the defence. However, his athletic profile is below elite—not particularly dominant aerially, and can be caught out by explosive forward movement. A clever defender in a possession-dominant system, but vulnerable against vertical, physical attacks.
