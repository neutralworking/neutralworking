A box-to-box midfielder whose physical presence and ball-carrying ability give him utility, but he operates in fits and starts rather than with consistency. He can drive forward and break lines, yet his passing is often route-one and he doesn't have the positional discipline or spatial awareness to shield a backline or dictate tempo. His concentration lapses in pressing sequences betray a lack of tactical nous — he's a useful player to have in the squad when energy is required, not when craft is.
