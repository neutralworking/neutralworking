## Scouting Notes

A midfielder with genuine ball-carrying ability and the capacity to progress play through the half-spaces with both passing and dribbling, providing Clermont with a useful out-ball when building from deep. He's reliable enough to be a weekly starter in a Ligue 1 mid-table side, and his intelligence helps him avoid giving the ball away cheaply in dangerous areas. However, he lacks the defensive intensity or positioning to anchor a midfield alone, and he's not quick enough to make him a weapon in transition—he's best suited as part of a balanced two or three.
