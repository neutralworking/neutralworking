Veteran midfielder who understands positioning and can recycle possession in a structured system, but his legs have clearly gone and he's no longer capable of covering the ground required in modern Ligue 1. He'll shield the back four competently on a good day and isn't a liability, yet his inability to progress the ball with any real tempo or press effectively at 38 means he's essentially a game-management tool now. Useful squad player for dead rubber matches or when Angers need to sit deep and absorb pressure, nothing more.
