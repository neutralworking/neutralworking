Tactical Profile

Ernest Poku, operating as a Seconda Punta, is characterized by his electric pace, low center of gravity, and exceptional dribbling ability, which allows him to bypass defenders effectively. His playing style is often likened to Karim Adeyemi due to these attributes, and he is recognized as a versatile attacker capable of playing across all three forward positions. This versatility, combined with his technical prowess and explosive style, makes him a dynamic threat in the final third.

Poku possesses an exceptional finishing ability, demonstrating clinical precision inside the box with both feet, making him a constant goal threat. His ambipedal nature further enhances his unpredictability, challenging defenders regardless of which foot he uses. Beyond scoring, he is an unselfish playmaker with keen spatial awareness, adept at creating space for himself and setting up teammates, thereby adding depth to his team's attacking strategies.

Career Highlights

Ernest Poku joined Bayer Leverkusen in 2025, marking a significant step in his professional career after previously playing for AZ Alkmaar and Jong AZ Alkmaar from 2021 to 2025. A notable achievement in his youth career was leading AZ to victory in the UEFA Youth League during the 2022/23 season, where he scored eight goals, including two in the final match.

His debut in the Eredivisie came at the young age of 17, and he scored his first Eredivisie goal on April 28, 2024. On the international stage, Poku has represented the Netherlands at various youth levels, including the U21 squad, for whom he has scored 5 goals in 14 appearances. He particularly impressed at the 2025 U21 European Championships, scoring twice and registering the fastest sprint speed of the tournament at 22 mph (35.3 km/h).
