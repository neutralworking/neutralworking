Recent Form

Joey Veerman is enjoying a highly productive 2025-2026 season with PSV, demonstrating exceptional form in the Eredivisie and contributing significantly to the team's push for a 27th league title. In 27 Eredivisie matches, he has registered 8 goals and 14 assists, accumulating 2,223 minutes of play with an impressive average FotMob rating of 8.09. His performance highlights his strong offensive output, ranking highly in assists, overall rating, and goals among central midfielders in the league.

Veerman's creative prowess has been particularly evident, as he has created 93 chances and 27 big chances in the Eredivisie during the current season. He was recognized as the Eredivisie Player of the Month for December 2025 and January 2026, making him the outright record holder with four such awards in the history of the monthly selection. In January 2026 alone, he provided four assists, created 22 chances (12 from open play), delivered the most passes into the final third (63), and completed the most crosses (44), maintaining an 85% pass accuracy. His recent match performances include an assist and an 8.4 FotMob rating in PSV's 4-3 victory against FC Utrecht on April 4, 2026.

Tactical Profile

Joey Veerman operates primarily as a central midfielder, often fulfilling the role of a playmaker or deep-lying playmaker who orchestrates the game, dictates tempo, and connects defense with attack. His exceptional vision, technical skill, and game intelligence are key strengths, enabling him to receive the ball from deep positions, turn, and distribute passes effectively across the pitch. He is also capable of playing as a more advanced central midfielder, or 'number 8', where he supports forwards and poses a direct goal threat.

His tactical versatility allows him to adapt to different midfield roles, whether in a single or double pivot, depending on the team's setup and personnel. Veerman is particularly effective when occupying the left half-space, utilizing his right foot to change the direction of play, deliver diagonal passes, and create opportunities behind defensive lines. While his defensive contributions have shown improvement, including better positioning and involvement in pressing and tracking back, they are not considered his strongest attribute. Earlier analyses from 2020 noted weaknesses in dueling strength and susceptibility to being dribbled past, and some 2025 reports suggested occasional complacency or slowness in high-paced European games. However, more recent assessments in April 2026 indicate no outstanding weaknesses in his profile.

Career Highlights

Joey Veerman has accumulated a significant number of achievements throughout his career, particularly since joining PSV. He has been instrumental in PSV securing three consecutive Eredivisie titles in 2023–24, 2024–25, and 2025–26. Additionally, he has won the KNVB Cup twice (2021–22, 2022–23) and the Johan Cruyff Shield three times (2022, 2023, 2025) with PSV.

On the international stage, Veerman made his senior debut for the Netherlands on June 18, 2023, and scored his first international goal on March 26, 2024. He was part of the Netherlands squad for UEFA Euro 2024, where the team earned a bronze medal. Before his move to PSV in January 2022, Veerman began his professional career at FC Volendam in 2016 and later transferred to SC Heerenveen in 2019, where he established himself as a midfielder with a keen passing ability. He has since played over a hundred matches for PSV.
