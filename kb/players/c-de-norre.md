A Terrier Holding Midfielder functioning as a Tuttocampista, this player showcases strong stamina, shielding, and an excellent first touch, surprisingly coupled with an ability to unleash long-range efforts and execute take-ons. He embodies the modern, energetic central midfielder who can cover ground and contribute to a high-press, offering both defensive work rate and an unexpected offensive spark from deep. However, his discipline issues and lack of guile are notable weaknesses. He represents a useful acquisition for a competitive squad needing a high-energy central midfielder, particularly for a club that can effectively manage his disciplinary record.
