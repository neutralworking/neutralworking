He embodies the modern, energetic central midfielder who can cover ground and contribute to a high-press, offering both defensive work rate and an unexpected offensive spark from deep. He represents a useful acquisition for a competitive squad needing a high-energy central midfielder, particularly for a club that can effectively manage his disciplinary record.
