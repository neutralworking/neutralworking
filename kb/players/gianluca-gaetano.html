Gianluca Gaetano is a 25-year-old Italian central midfielder currently playing for Cagliari. Known for his right-footed play, he stands at 183 cm and is internally archetyped as an "Architect" and "Playmaker."

Tactical Profile

Gaetano is a technically gifted midfielder, initially trained as an attacking midfielder, but has since evolved into a playmaker or pure central midfielder. His playing style is characterized by excellent vision, positioning, dribbling, and shooting ability, capable of striking effectively with both feet and scoring from outside the box, including free kicks. He is noted for playing the ball off the ground frequently and is strong in taking set-pieces.

At Cagliari, he operates as part of the midfield core, often in a 3-5-2 formation, contributing to midfield control and counter-attacking strategies. He sees his role as a creative midfielder whose primary job is to facilitate team play, rather than solely focusing on scoring. His current role at Cagliari has allowed him more involvement with the ball, leading to increased enjoyment and consistency in his performances.

Career Highlights

Gianluca Gaetano joined Napoli's youth academy in 2011 and became the youngest player in the club's history to sign a professional contract at 16. He made his professional debut for Napoli in a Coppa Italia match in January 2019 and his UEFA Champions League debut in December 2019. During his youth career with Napoli's Primavera team, he amassed an impressive 43 goals and 25 assists.

He had significant loan spells at Cremonese, where he played 86 games, scoring 17 goals and providing 11 assists, contributing to their promotion to Serie A in the 2021/2022 season. During his time there, he was recognized in the Serie B Team of the Year for 2021-22 and named Serie B Player of the Month in April 2022. Gaetano was also part of the Napoli squad that won the Serie A title in the 2022/2023 season, where he scored his first Serie A goal in a 3-1 victory against Inter. His initial loan to Cagliari in early 2024 saw him score 4 goals and provide 1 assist in 11 games, proving crucial in the team's efforts to secure permanence in the top division.

Personality

Gaetano expresses a deep affection for Cagliari, the team, and its atmosphere, stating that the city has "changed his life". He maintains a strong bond with his teammates and frequently thanks the fans for their unwavering support, considering them the "12th man". He has openly discussed his childhood dream of playing for Napoli and his emotional journey in football. Gaetano is inspired by Marek Hamšík and prioritizes making the team play well over individual goal tallies.
