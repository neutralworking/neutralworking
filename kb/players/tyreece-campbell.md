## Snapshot

Tyreece Campbell is a raw, pacey winger who excels at beating his man down the flank with his blistering acceleration and balance, allowing him to deliver crosses into the box with precision. Campbell's playing style is that of a traditional, direct winger, but he needs to work on his awareness and positioning to become a more well-rounded player.
