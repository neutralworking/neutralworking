## Snapshot

A robust, aerially commanding centre-back with the positional awareness to read danger early and step into midfield to break up play. His issue is mobility—he's not nimble enough for high lines or repeated turnovers, and against sharp pressing he can look leaden, which limits his utility in modern Bundesliga football.
