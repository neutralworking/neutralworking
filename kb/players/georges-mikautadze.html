Georges Mikautadze, a 25-year-old Georgian international forward, currently plays for La Liga club Villarreal. He transferred to Villarreal from Lyon in August 2025 for €31 million, signing a six-year contract. Standing at 175 cm, Mikautadze is right-footed, though he demonstrates proficiency with both feet.

Recent Form

Georges Mikautadze has shown strong performance in the 2025/2026 season for Villarreal. In La Liga, he has recorded 9 goals and 5 assists across 26 matches, accumulating 1,734 minutes of play, and holds an average FotMob rating of 7.04. Across all competitions for the current season, he has contributed 15 goals and 6 assists in 44 matches, playing 2,930 minutes.

He started 2026 in excellent form, contributing two goals and one assist in January during Villarreal's victories against Elche and Deportivo Alavés. In March 2026, Mikautadze continued his impactful displays by scoring one goal and providing an assist in a 3-1 win over Real Sociedad. His shooting accuracy in La Liga for the season stands at 48% on target from 52 shots, with an expected goals (xG) value of 8.67, indicating his clinical finishing.

Tactical Profile

Mikautadze operates primarily as a center-forward, but his versatile skillset allows him to function effectively as a shadow striker or poacher. He is noted for his exceptional off-ball movement, particularly his central runs in behind defensive lines, and maintains impressive composure in one-on-one situations. His dribbling ability is a crucial aspect of his attacking play, often linking up effectively between the lines and employing double movements to disorient defenders and create space. He can also drift wide into the inside channel to dribble, utilizing stop-start techniques to manipulate opponents.

Technically gifted, Mikautadze possesses a smooth first touch and an unconventional way of controlling the ball, which can unsettle opposing defenses. He is adept at playing with his back to goal, facilitating link-up play with midfielders. While some sources note his defensive contribution as a weakness, others highlight his intelligent pressing, ability to read passes, screen central spaces, and apply pressure on backward passes. He is also effective in tight spaces and can finish with either foot from various angles, adding variety to offensive sequences.

Career Highlights

Georges Mikautadze has achieved significant milestones throughout his career. In the 2022-23 season, he was the top scorer and named the best player in Ligue 2 with Metz, playing a pivotal role in their promotion to Ligue 1. He continued his impressive form on the international stage, sharing the top scorer award at Euro 2024 with three goals.

His international career for Georgia also includes a remarkable four-goal performance against Thailand in October 2023. In recognition of his contributions, he was awarded the Order of Honor by the President of Georgia in 2024. Earlier in his career, during a loan spell at Seraing in 2020, he scored 15 goals in just nine matches, becoming a joint top scorer in the Belgian First Division B and helping the club achieve promotion. Following a €16 million transfer to Ajax in August 2023, he returned to Metz on loan in January 2024, where he scored 13 goals in 20 Ligue 1 appearances. He then moved to Lyon in July 2024 for €18.5 million before his subsequent transfer to Villarreal.

Personality

Georges Mikautadze is characterized by a quick-paced, bold, and competitive attitude, often described as fun-loving. He possesses a strong personality, demonstrating aggressiveness, determination, and hunger on the field. He is known to be direct and holds strong opinions, with little patience for ambiguity. Mikautadze has shown adaptability, quickly integrating into his new team at Villarreal and understanding the coach's tactical demands. He notably stated that he "sacrificed himself to save the club I love" regarding his departure from Lyon due to the club's financial difficulties, highlighting his deep connection and loyalty. While generally disciplined, he is still maturing and occasionally exhibits traits like a momentary loss of attention or impulsivity.

Condition / Medical History

Mikautadze generally maintains a clean medical record with only minor, short-term absences. His injury history includes a muscle injury in November 2025, which sidelined him for 11 days, and a brief period of physical discomfort in October 2025 lasting two days. He also experienced an illness in October 2024 and October 2023, each resulting in a few days of absence. In May 2024, he received a red card, leading to a suspension.
