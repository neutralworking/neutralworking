## Scouting Notes

A tenacious box-to-box midfielder with good pressing instincts and acceptable short-range distribution, Carey covers ground efficiently and isn't afraid of a tackle. However, he lacks the progressive vision or ability to receive the ball in tight spaces that separates a genuinely useful midfielder from a squad player; he's hardworking to the point of one-note, and opponents can work around his limitations.
