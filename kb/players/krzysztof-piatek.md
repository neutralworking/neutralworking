## Snapshot

A poacher who thrives in the box with sharp movement and a reliable left foot, still capable of 6-8 goals in a league season if service is clean and consistent. His problem has always been involvement outside the box—he doesn't press hard enough, doesn't link play, and against a high press he becomes a passenger waiting for a through ball that doesn't come. Fine as a squad option in a lower-tier league, but he's no longer the guy you build a system around.
