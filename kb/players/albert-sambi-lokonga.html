Snapshot

A press-resistant midfielder with genuine technical quality in tight spaces and a willingness to receive the ball under pressure that's increasingly rare at this level. His passing range is better than most at Hamburg, and he can dictate tempo from deep without fuss. The problem is he's a bit of a luxury player—his defensive positioning is loose, he'll get caught napping on the press too often, and against sides that sprint at him with intent he looks vulnerable; you're essentially asking your defenders to compensate for his tactical sloppiness.
