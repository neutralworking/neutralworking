Snapshot

A relayeur built on physical attributes—stamina, jumping, balance—and crossing threat rather than midfield control, offering value as a dynamic interior wing-back or inverted fullback. Discipline and tactical inconsistency betray his mental profile; while he carries the ball and launches set pieces effectively, he neither shields nor distributes with the consistency demanded of a true metronome. Best deployed in systems where his crossing and aerial presence offset his lack of positional anchoring, ideally alongside a more structured midfield partner.

Player Research Brief: Guilherme

Recent Form

Guilherme's tenure at Konyaspor concluded on March 26, 2026, when his contract was mutually terminated after he was excluded from the squad. Prior to his departure, in the partial 2025/2026 season, he made 22 appearances in the Super Lig, providing one assist. In the 2024/2025 season, he featured in 34 Super Lig matches, registering 8 assists, and also played in 3 Turkish Cup games. His performance in the 2023/2024 season was notable, with 5 goals and 7 assists across 37 Super Lig appearances, alongside 2 Turkish Cup appearances.

Tactical Profile

Guilherme, a 36-year-old Brazilian, primarily operates as a left-back or left-sided defender, though he can also play as a left midfielder or left winger. Standing at 179-180 cm, he is known for his left-footed play. His offensive contributions are a key aspect of his game, with strengths identified in crossing, taking set-pieces, and providing key passes. His high assist numbers during his time at Konyaspor underscore his role in creating scoring opportunities from wide areas.

Career Highlights

Guilherme joined Konyaspor in January 2020 and became a significant figure for the club, playing 226 official matches, scoring 13 goals, and providing 38 assists, setting a club record for assists. His career before Konyaspor included stints with several clubs across Europe, such as Jagiellonia, Termalica Nieciecza, and FCSB. During his time in Romania with FCSB and Petrolul, he won multiple domestic trophies, including one Liga 1 title and two Cupa Romaniei titles. He also gained experience in European competitions, making appearances in the Europa League with various clubs.

Condition / Medical History

Guilherme has experienced a few injuries throughout his career. In November 2023, he sustained a thigh injury that kept him out until late that month. Earlier, in January 2021, he had a muscle injury. There was also a reported injury in April 2025.

Personality

Guilherme's deep connection to Konyaspor and the city of Konya was evident in his emotional farewell message upon his departure in March 2026. He described Konyaspor as more than just a club, highlighting significant personal milestones such as getting married and having his son born in Konya. He expressed a strong desire to remain with the team, indicating that the decision for his departure was made by the club management rather than his own will. His dedication to the club was further symbolized by tattoos of the city's number 42 and the stadium's coordinates.
