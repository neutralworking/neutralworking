## Scouting Notes

A veteran poacher who still understands the geometry of the box—he knows where the space opens and he's willing to make the unglamorous runs that create half-chances. His movement is intelligent rather than explosive, relying on timing and positioning over athleticism. The problem is straightforward: at 32, his first touch has lost the sharpness it once had, he's not quick enough to separate from modern defenders in transition, and he'll never carry the ball or link play with any real conviction. Serviceable for Cluj as a functional target man, but not the kind of player who changes matches at this level anymore.
