## Scouting Notes

Marco Burch is a powerhouse of a defender, with impressive physical attributes and stamina that allows him to cover a lot of ground, making him well-suited to the vorstopper role where he can utilize his progressive carries to start attacks from the back. However, his lack of discipline and tendency to struggle in duels is a major concern, often leaving him exposed to quick interplay and half-spaces. His poor blocking ability also means he can be caught out by clever movement and runs behind the defense.
