## Snapshot

A dependable right-back who has carved out a solid career at Legia, offering reasonable pace and positional discipline in the Ekstraklasa rhythm. He's a reliable crosser with decent technical ability on the ball—comfortable in transitions and capable of progressing play down the flank—but lacks the explosive athleticism or defensive aggression to operate consistently at a higher level. At 30, he's exactly what a mid-table European side needs: a functional, low-drama presence who won't lose you games through individual mistakes, though he won't win you many either. His limitations in one-on-one defending against quicker wingers mean he's destined for this tier of competition rather than stepping up to genuine European contention.
