## Snapshot

Stefano Vecchia is a quintessential sprinter-striker, leveraging his blistering acceleration and pace to leave defenders in the dust, but his one-dimensional approach often sees him struggle to create for others, and his lack of tactical awareness can lead to him being isolated from the rest of the team. His physicality allows him to hold his own in half-spaces, but he's not one to drop deep and link play. Vecchia's inability to contribute defensively, with poor tackling and marking, means he's a liability when his team is out of possession.
