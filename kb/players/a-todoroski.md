## Snapshot

A. Todoroski, an Outlet Trequartista, brings explosive acceleration, pace, and stamina combined with aggression and good shielding to drive play centrally. While technically strong overall, his effectiveness is hampered by persistent discipline issues, poor volley technique, and struggles with close-range finishing, indicating a player who thrives in transit but lacks refinement in the final third. This profile leans towards a dynamic, forward-running Trequartista who emphasizes breaking lines and carrying the ball, rather than the more traditional creative passer, fitting systems that value directness and verticality in their attacking midfield. Todoroski's strong physical and technical base makes him an interesting option for clubs seeking a high-energy, central attacking presence, particularly those that can accommodate his moderate tactical understanding. However, his discipline and finishing weaknesses present significant risks, suggesting he'd best suit a development-focused club willing to invest in refining his end product and managing his temperament.
