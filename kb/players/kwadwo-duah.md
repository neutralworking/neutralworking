## Snapshot

Duah operates as a Poacher, combining solid physicality, acceleration, and jumping to make him a threat in the box and in transition. While he covers ground well, his extremely low creativity and poor discipline mean he rarely contributes to build-up play and can be a hothead at critical moments. Don't expect any fancy flicks or killer passes, just goals (hopefully).
