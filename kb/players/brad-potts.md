## Snapshot

Tireless midfielder who embodies the old-school Championship grind: strong in the tackle, reliable in possession, and almost impossible to dispossess in midfield. His limitation is serious, though—he's not progressive, doesn't have the range to shift play, and his verticality is nonexistent. Exactly the type of player who becomes invaluable at a mid-table club, invisible at a good one.
