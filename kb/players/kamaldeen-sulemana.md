## Snapshot

A right winger with explosive acceleration and dribbling ability, operating on the opposite flank from Lookman. Sulemana has genuine ability to take people on and create chaos in the final third, and he's a willing presser. However, his final product is inconsistent — he'll beat three defenders and then misplace a pass or strike a poor shot — and he's not a significant creator from open play.
