A tidy, intelligent box-to-box operator whose primary currency is pressing and lateral ball progression rather than incision or dribbling range. The limitation is clear: he lacks the vertical passing range or progressive carrying ability to unlock defences in the final third, and his physicality has diminished with age, making him vulnerable to more dynamic midfield operators in open play.
