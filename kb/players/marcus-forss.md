## Snapshot

Intelligent centre-forward who understands spacing and creates room for teammates through intelligent movement and positioning. His finishing is respectable without being clinical, and he works hard defensively, making him a rounded contributor. However, he lacks genuine pace or explosiveness; quicker centre-backs will have time to close him down, and he will struggle to isolate defenders or create meaningful separation in congested boxes. A solid Championship option without the attributes to be a real difference-maker.
