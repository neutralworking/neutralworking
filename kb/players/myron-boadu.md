## Scouting Notes

A number nine with genuine athleticism and the ability to hold up play while bringing others into the game, decent movement in the box, and he's capable of operating effectively as either a focal point or a secondary striker. His issue is he doesn't have the elite finishing touch or the explosive gear to drag himself into 87+ territory; he's a very good support forward who needs the game structured around him rather than a pure goalscorer. Useful in the right system, but not a difference-maker on his own.
