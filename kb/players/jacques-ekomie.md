A young midfielder operating with moderate technical security but lacking the vision or athleticism to stand out at Ligue 1 level. Ekomié's best attribute is his willingness to receive the ball and move it on, but he contributes little defensively and doesn't progress play through pressing or clever positioning. He's a youth development piece, perhaps useful for a relegation-battling side needing bodies.
