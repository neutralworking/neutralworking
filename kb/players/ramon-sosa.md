## Snapshot

A compact, intelligent left winger who operates brilliantly in half-spaces and understands how to create separation through subtle positioning rather than explosiveness. His left foot has quality, and his pressing is genuinely energetic, but he lacks the explosive pace that makes elite wingers dangerous in 1v1 situations when they're in transition. He's a functional, hardworking option who fits a specific system rather than a transformative talent.
