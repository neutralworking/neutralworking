A virtuoso complete forward whose acceleration, balance, and pace create genuine one-on-one threat through spontaneous directness—his stamina allows him to press and recover. Duel and interception weaknesses mean he's not a target or defensive contributor; he's a pure outlet requiring service structure. Tactical limitations suggest he'll thrive in counter-oriented systems but struggle in possession-heavy setups. Fits best as a direct forward for clubs needing explosive goal threat in transitions, though his guile deficits mean he'll plateau without elite playmakers feeding him.
