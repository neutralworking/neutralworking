## Snapshot

A Swedish central midfielder with legitimate technical security and the ability to carry the ball through midfield with purpose, operating with press resistance and showing genuine composure under suffocation. He's tidy in transition and understands spatial positioning well, though he lacks the dynamism or aggressive pressing to truly dominate lighter opponents and his range of passing can be conservative. A reliable, intelligent midfielder who'll hold his own in a well-structured system without ever demanding the ball in the biggest moments.
