## Scouting Notes

A functional target man who thrives on the shoulder of defenders and has the physical presence to win aerial duels consistently. His movement in the box is intelligent rather than explosive, and he's capable of holding play up with reasonable technical security. The limitation is pace—he won't separate from centre-backs in transition, and against organized defences pressing high, his lack of mobility becomes a genuine problem.
