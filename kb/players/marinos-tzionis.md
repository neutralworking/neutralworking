A left-sided operator with decent technical foundation and reasonable press resistance, Tzionis has spent his career in the Romanian second tier and Cyprus's top flight — respectable domestic football but nothing more. He can carry the ball in transitions and isn't afraid to take a man on, but his final product is inconsistent and his positioning defensive is loose; he's a squad depth player masquerading as a starter, the sort of winger who looks functional for a mid-table Romanian side but would be exposed in any genuinely competitive environment. At 24, he's likely at his ceiling.
