## Snapshot

A left-back with exceptional athleticism and the ability to cover enormous ground either defending or driving forward; he's quick enough to recover from positional mistakes and explosive enough to create overloads in transition. His passing range is good rather than exceptional, and he occasionally lacks the defensive discipline to read deeper patterns of play. A very good athlete at a big club, but not quite elite due to average reading of the game.
