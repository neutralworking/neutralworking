Christian Pulisic is an American professional soccer player known for his dribbling abilities, explosive speed, and finishing. He plays as a winger or attacking midfielder [^1^]. Pulisic is versatile and capable of playing on both the right wing and the left wing, as well as in central positions such as striker or 'false nine' [^3^]. His playing style has varied under different managers, but he is known for his speed, dribbling ability, and technical skills [^6^]. 

Todd Boehly has now poured more than £1bn into Chelsea, but it wasn’t just their big spending that caught the eye this summer. Signed for £57.6m from Dortmund in 2019, Pulisic was sold for a maximum €22m (£18.9m) – this figure feels at least £10m short.
