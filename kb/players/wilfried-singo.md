## Scouting Notes

A right-back with exceptional athletic capacity—quick, explosive, and genuinely dangerous in transition both as a ball carrier and as a defensive presser who can recover ground rapidly. He's entering his physical prime and improving on the ball with each season. The weakness is his decision-making when faced with complex scenarios; he's reactive rather than proactive defensively, and his crossing is serviceable at best.
