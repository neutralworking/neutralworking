Douglas Luiz is a 27-year-old Brazilian central midfielder who currently plays for Aston Villa on loan from Juventus. He is right-footed and stands at 175 cm tall, known for his role as a playmaker.

Recent Form

Douglas Luiz has been active in the 2025-2026 season, initially spending time on loan at Nottingham Forest before rejoining Aston Villa in January 2026. For Aston Villa in the Premier League this season, he has made 17 appearances, scoring one goal and providing no assists. Prior to his return to Villa, he featured in 8 Premier League matches for Nottingham Forest, with no goals or assists recorded during that period. His overall performance for Aston Villa in the 2025-2026 Premier League season has earned him an average FotMob rating of 6.88 across 823 minutes played.

Tactical Profile

Douglas Luiz operates primarily as a central midfielder, demonstrating versatility across various midfield roles, including a deep-lying playmaker, holding midfielder, advanced playmaker, and box-to-box midfielder. He is recognized for his technical quality, defensive awareness, athleticism, and attacking flair. Luiz excels in breaking up opposition play through good defensive positioning and vision, often sitting on the right of a double pivot in midfield. His offensive contributions include driving forward with the ball, delivering accurate long passes, and utilizing his powerful long shot, with a notable ability to score directly from corners.

Career Highlights

Douglas Luiz has achieved significant milestones in his career, including winning a gold medal with the Brazil U23 team at the 2020 Tokyo Summer Olympics. He was also part of the Brazil squad that finished as runners-up in the 2021 Copa América. At club level, while with Aston Villa, he was recognized as both the Supporter's Player of the Season and Player's Player of the Season for the 2022-23 campaign. His career has seen him play for Vasco da Gama, Manchester City (with loan spells at Girona), Aston Villa, Juventus, and Nottingham Forest.

Personality

Douglas Luiz is known for his joyful approach to the game, often expressing enthusiasm on the pitch. He has cited Brazilian legend Ronaldinho as an idol, admiring his "alegria" (joy or happiness) and skill. An anecdote from his time at Aston Villa highlights his passionate nature, where he once injured his back celebrating a goal by diving into the crowd, a detail he initially kept from his manager.

Condition / Medical History

Douglas Luiz has experienced a notable history of injuries, particularly in recent seasons. In 2025, he sustained multiple injuries including hamstring, thigh, and muscle issues, which led to periods of absence from play. During his time at Juventus in late 2024, he suffered a "mysterious injury" that was later identified as intramuscular edema, a condition prone to relapses and requiring careful management. This recurring issue contributed to an uninspiring start to his season at Juventus and necessitated a cautious approach to his return to play.
