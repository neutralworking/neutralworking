## Snapshot

A target man who thrives on chaos and second balls but isn't a footballer in the modern sense. He'll win headers and create space through sheer physical presence, which has value at this level, but he can't play in transition or link play with any subtlety. Functional as a Plan B; a liability if you need him to be your primary striker.
