However, for all his boundless energy and surprising acceleration, his glaring deficiencies in mental, tactical, and technical areas severely limit his on-ball decision-making and overall impact in possession. He's a tireless engine off the ball, but his inability to consistently contribute with intelligent passing or creative runs means he's more of a disruptive force than a constructive presence.
