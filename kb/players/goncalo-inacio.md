## Career Highlights

Gonçalo Inácio made his first-team debut for Sporting CP on October 4, 2020, and scored his first goal for the club on March 20, 2021. He has been a key figure in Sporting CP's recent successes, winning three Primeira Liga titles in the 2020–21, 2023–24, and 2024–25 seasons. His trophy cabinet also includes the Taça de Portugal in 2024–25, two Taça da Liga titles (2020–21, 2021–22), and the Supertaça Cândido de Oliveira in 2021.

Individually, Inácio has received significant recognition, being named to the Primeira Liga Team of the Year for both 2024 and 2025. He has also been honored as the Primeira Liga Defender of the Month multiple times, in December 2021, December 2023, and January 2024, and was the SJPF Young Player of the Month in November 2021. On the international stage, Inácio debuted for the Portugal national team on March 23, 2023, and notably scored his first two international goals on September 11, 2023, during a 9-0 victory against Luxembourg. He was part of the Portugal squad that won the UEFA Nations League A in the 2024–25 season. He reached his 200th competitive appearance on February 23, 2025.
