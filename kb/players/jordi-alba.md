## Scouting Notes

A ghost of his Barcelona self, Alba remains technically sound on the ball and understands positioning well, but his explosive first step has evaporated and he gets turned too easily by even moderate wingers. Still capable of a dangerous cross from deep, but the defensive fragility that was always there is now a genuine liability rather than a trade-off you'd accept. The experience counts for something in MLS, but he's squarely a squad rotation piece.
