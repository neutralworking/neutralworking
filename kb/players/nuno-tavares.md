## Scouting Notes

A left-back with genuine athleticism and the ability to cover ground at pace, offering defensive reliability and a real threat in transition with the ball at his feet. He's improved his technical security noticeably over the past year, and he can complete progressive carries and switches competently. His main limitation is a lack of aerial dominance and occasional lapses in positional discipline against fluid attacking movements; a good, functional left-back rather than an elite one.
