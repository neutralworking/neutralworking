Swedish goalkeeper in the latter stage of a solid journeyman stint, competent with basic shot-stopping and command of his box but without the sweeping instincts or distribution range modern football increasingly demands. He's a traditional stopper—solid angles, reliable hands—but watch him on the ball and the limitations become apparent; he plays it short, simple, rarely progressive. For a club like Al-Fateh, he's serviceable depth or a squad option, but not the type of goalkeeper whose distribution feeds a build-from-the-back philosophy.
