## Snapshot

A no-nonsense centre-back with excellent recovery pace and a real appetite for the physical battle—Trusty doesn't overthink things, which is both his virtue and his limit. He'll win headers, make blocks, and close you down, but his reading of space is functional rather than proactive, and he's occasionally caught ball-watching on the far post. He's a good, honest defender at a big club; he won't hurt you, but he won't anticipate danger like a truly elite centre-back does.
