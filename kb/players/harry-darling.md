## Scouting Notes

A composed, technically capable centre-back comfortable in possession and willing to progress the ball through the thirds, with decent reading of space and a calm demeanor that settles a defense. However, his athleticism is pedestrian—he won't recover well against direct play or aggressive pressing—and there's a fragility about him in one-on-one situations against quicker strikers. Best utilized in a back three or against methodical, possession-based opponents where his positioning can do the work.
