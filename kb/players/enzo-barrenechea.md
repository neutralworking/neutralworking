## Snapshot

A metronome midfielder with elegant technique on the ball and an impressive range of progressive passing; he dictates tempo rather than accelerates it, which can sometimes feel pedestrian when Benfica need urgency. Positionally sound defensively, though he lacks the athleticism to single-handedly break up play against the fastest attackers. Argentine craft and intelligence, but not quite the dynamism needed in midfield's deepest roles at the absolute highest level.
