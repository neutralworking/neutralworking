## Snapshot

A tall, aggressive centre-back who dominates aerially and isn't afraid to press into the midfield or step out of his line—the type of commanding presence that suits second-tier football where physicality and personality matter. His distribution is honest if uninspired, and he's genuinely strong one-on-one in open play. The vulnerability is obvious: he can be caught by pace if split open, and his decision-making under sustained pressure occasionally veers toward the reckless, suggesting he may lack the technical composure required for sustained top-flight football.
