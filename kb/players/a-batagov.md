## Snapshot

A disciplined central midfielder with strong positional awareness and the capacity to contribute in build-up phases, though his technical ceiling is constrained by modest pass accuracy and decision-making in transition. His take-on ability and competitive intensity suggest a player best deployed in structured possession systems where his tactical intelligence compensates for limited ball-carrying responsibility. Batagov suits clubs needing a reliable midfield anchor without aspirations of progressive play—his weakness in blocking and interceptions means he requires defensive cover, making him a fit for deep-lying tuttocampista roles rather than primary ball-winners.
