Recent Form

Cody Gakpo has been a consistent presence for Liverpool during the 2025/2026 season, featuring in 44 matches across all competitions, where he has contributed 8 goals and 4 assists. In the Premier League specifically, he has made 30 appearances, including 4 as a substitute, scoring 6 goals and providing 4 assists. He has also added 2 goals and 1 assist in 5 UEFA Champions League appearances. Gakpo has been a frequent starter, beginning 30 out of 41 possible matches for Liverpool this season, indicating a regular role in the squad.

His form in the current season follows a strong 2024/2025 campaign where he played a crucial role in Liverpool's Premier League title win, scoring in a decisive 5-1 victory against Tottenham Hotspur. He also netted the equalizer in a 2-2 draw with Manchester United in January 2025. Under manager Arne Slot, Gakpo has predominantly been deployed as a left-winger, a position he believes allows him to be particularly dangerous by cutting inside, shooting, or crossing.

Tactical Profile

Cody Gakpo is a versatile right-footed attacker capable of playing as a left-winger, central attacking midfielder, or center-forward. His playing style is characterized by a direct approach, utilizing his speed and skills to advance the ball, bypass defenders, and create scoring opportunities. He demonstrates intelligent movement, adeptly finding space and making runs behind defensive lines, along with a keen ability to anticipate passes.

Gakpo possesses strong technical attributes, including excellent ball control and dribbling, allowing him to navigate tight spaces effectively. He is also noted for his finishing, aerial prowess (ranking in the 92nd percentile for aerials won), and capacity to deliver key passes (81st percentile for key passes per 90 minutes). While he thrives in transition and open spaces where he can leverage his physicality and directness against isolated defenders, his effectiveness can be diminished in possession-heavy systems that require him to be a high-volume technical hub, as evidenced by a lower pass completion rate (29th percentile) and take-on success rate (20th percentile) in such scenarios.

Career Highlights

Cody Gakpo is an academy graduate of PSV Eindhoven, making his first-team debut in February 2018. His exceptional performance in the 2021-2022 season, where he scored 21 goals in 47 appearances across all competitions, earned him the prestigious Dutch Footballer of the Year award. During his time at PSV, he also secured the KNVB Cup in 2022, scoring the winning goal against Ajax in the final, and recorded his first hat-trick in a 7-1 victory over Volendam in August 2022.

Gakpo joined Liverpool in January 2023. He was a key contributor to Liverpool's Premier League title-winning campaign in the 2024-2025 season and also won the English League Cup in 2024. On the international stage, Gakpo has earned 48 caps for the Netherlands, scoring 19 goals. He was a participant in the UEFA Euro 2024, where he was awarded the Golden Boot with three goals, and notably scored in all three group-stage matches at the 2022 FIFA World Cup. He signed a new long-term contract with Liverpool in August 2025.
