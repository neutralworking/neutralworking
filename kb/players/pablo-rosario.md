He's utterly functional in a defensive sense, with the discipline and reading of space to shield a back four without being tested athletically. The honest limitation: he doesn't affect the game in the final third, can't break a line with a pass, and he's deadweight in an open game where Porto need someone to progress the ball or create overloads.
