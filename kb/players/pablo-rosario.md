## Scouting Notes

A tidy, press-resistant midfielder who keeps things simple and moves the ball with efficiency in the lower-to-middle third—his passing range is modest but his positioning allows Porto to circulate and escape pressing with ease. He's utterly functional in a defensive sense, with the discipline and reading of space to shield a back four without being tested athletically. The honest limitation: he doesn't affect the game in the final third, can't break a line with a pass, and he's deadweight in an open game where Porto need someone to progress the ball or create overloads.
