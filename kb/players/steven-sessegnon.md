## Snapshot

A left-back with genuine athleticism and an underrated capacity to affect play in the final third — his recovery pace is genuinely quick, and he's comfortable receiving the ball on the half-turn to drive into half-spaces or thread cutback passes. What holds him back from the next tier is a certain passivity in his decision-making; he'll often choose safety when a more incisive pass is available, and defensively he can be loose-bodied against direct, physical runners who know how to use their frame. Still, he's a solid, dependable option in the Premier League when asked to manage a flank.
