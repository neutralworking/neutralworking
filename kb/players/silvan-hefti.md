## Snapshot

A Swiss right-back with good athleticism and a tendency to get forward and support the attack, offering genuine offensive contributions on the flank. He's reasonably solid in his defensive positioning but can be caught out of shape when the play moves quickly, and he doesn't have the tactical discipline to hold a narrow line. He's a dependable starter for a side playing an expansive style but vulnerable against a disciplined, penetrative attack.
