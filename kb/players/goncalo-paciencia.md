## Scouting Notes

A functional target man who operates with decent positioning in the box and understands the geometry of link-up play from deep, but he's not quick enough to consistently separate from centre-backs and his touch can betray him in tight spaces. He's the sort of player who'll nod one in from a corner or finish a cutback with competence, yet he won't create space through movement or dribbling when defences sit deep. For a club fighting at the top of Liga Portugal, he's useful rotation; for any side with ambitions beyond Portugal, he's a limitation.
