## Snapshot

Ramsey, a Marshal operating as a Metodista, brings strong technical and physical attributes with a tenacious, hot-headed edge. His excellent stamina, first touch, and ability to shield the ball allow him to contribute significantly to build-up and drive play forward from central areas, showing quick reactions. However, his tactical understanding and mental pillar are limited, and he lacks guile in tight spaces, struggling with defensive blocking and prone to disciplinary issues. This archetype fits into the tradition of high-energy box-to-box midfielders who press actively and contribute to both phases of play with relentless effort. As a rising player, Ramsey is a valuable, durable asset for clubs seeking a dynamic, build-up contributing midfielder who covers extensive ground, but his hot-headed nature and limited tactical awareness present a risk that requires careful management within a structured team.
