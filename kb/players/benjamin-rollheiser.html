Career Highlights

Rollheiser's career began in Argentina, progressing through the youth ranks of Estudiantes, Deportivo Sarmiento, and River Plate, where he made his professional debut in 2019. After a period of limited play, he joined Estudiantes in 2022, a move that significantly boosted his career, establishing him as a key player and earning him the number 10 jersey.

During his impactful spell at Estudiantes, Rollheiser was instrumental in the team's success, notably contributing to their victory in the 2023 Copa Argentina. His strong performances, which included 12 goals and 7 assists in 57 games, garnered attention from European clubs, leading to his transfer to Benfica in 2024. He further added to his trophy cabinet by winning the League Cup with Benfica in the 2024/2025 season.

On the international stage, Rollheiser has represented Argentina at the U17 level, participating in the 2017 South American Championship. He has also had the opportunity to train with the senior Argentina national team.

Condition / Medical History

Benjamín Rollheiser has encountered several periods of absence due to injuries throughout his career. In 2019, he sustained a knee injury, and in 2020, he suffered a significant cruciate ligament injury that sidelined him for several months, from February to September.
