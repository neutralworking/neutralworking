## Scouting Notes

A winger-forward with genuine directness and the ability to create chaos in tight spaces through quick feet and surprising strength. He's not graceful, but he's functional and occasionally dangerous in transition or when Clermont are hunting a goal late. Limited in his ability to manipulate space or make telling movements off the ball, and his finishing remains a question mark—he'll have chances, but converting them consistently has never been his forte.
