During his time at Manchester City, he made his Premier League debut in 2019 and contributed to the team winning the Premier League in the 2020-21 season, along with three EFL Cups and the Community Shield in 2019.

He rejoined Barcelona in June 2021 on a free transfer. In the 2023-2024 season, Garcia had a successful loan spell at Girona, where his contributions helped the club secure a third-place finish in La Liga and qualify for the Champions League. Upon his return to Barcelona in 2024-2025, he played a significant role under Hansi Flick.

Garcia has accumulated numerous titles throughout his career, including two La Liga titles (2022-23, 2024-25), one Copa del Rey (2024-25), and three Supercopa de España titles (2022-23, 2024-25, 2025-26) with Barcelona.
