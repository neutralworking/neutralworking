## Scouting Notes

A methodical, positionally intelligent full-back who reads the game well and rarely puts you in genuine danger defensively, but he's fundamentally limited by a lack of explosive pace and the kind of attacking thrust that elevates a defender from 'steady' to 'threatening.' His passing is functional rather than progressive, and he struggles against tricky wingers who can play at two speeds. He's the definition of a solid rotation option at a big club—valuable on the pitch, unremarkable on it.
