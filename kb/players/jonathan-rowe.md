## Scouting Notes

An English winger with directness and dribbling ability that troubles defenders in the half-spaces; he can operate on either flank and creates problems through movement and press resistance. His finishing is the concern—he shoots reasonably but lacks the clinical precision of truly elite wingers—and defensively, he can be caught out of position against sustained pressing. A young player developing well, not yet complete.
