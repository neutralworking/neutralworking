## Tactical Use

### Goalkeeper

- Distribution specialist who starts attacks from the back with pinpoint passing under pressure.
- Reference line: Ederson, Valdés.
- Naming origin: English.
### Centre-Back

- Ball-playing centre-back who steps into midfield to build attacks with progressive passing.
- Reference line: Bonucci, Stones, Piqué, Ferdinand.
- Naming origin: English.
