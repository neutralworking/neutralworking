# Dribbler

**Category**: Technical  
**Core Attributes**: Carries, First Touch, Skills, Take Ons

> Close control and the ability to beat defenders 1v1. Carries the ball under pressure and creates overloads through individual quality.

## Tactical Habitat

Dribblers thrive in systems that create:

- isolated duels after a [Switch of Play](/Users/solid-snake/Documents/chief-scout/kb/concepts/switch-of-play.md)
- space in the [Half-Space](/Users/solid-snake/Documents/chief-scout/kb/concepts/half-space.md)
- overload-to-release patterns
- repeated box-access situations

## What They Solve

A true dribbler helps a team:

- break settled defensive structures
- create [Overload](/Users/solid-snake/Documents/chief-scout/kb/concepts/overload.md) through individual elimination
- force emergency cover
- turn static possession into box threat

## Scouting Indicators

- beats defenders without losing the structure of the attack
- manipulates body shape before contact
- carries with purpose toward space or box access
- attracts multiple defenders and still releases at the right moment

## Common Limitations

- can overplay and kill rhythm
- some dribblers beat one man but solve nothing afterward
- heavy dependence on favorable spacing can make them system-sensitive
