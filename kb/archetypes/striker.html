Striker

Category: Technical
Core Attributes: Close Range, Mid Range, Long Range, Penalties

The finisher. Clinical from close range, mid-range, and distance. Converts chances and adds scoreboard pressure.

Tactical Habitat

Striker profiles are amplified by teams that generate:

  • repeated Box Entry
  • clean Blind-Side Run situations
  • strong Pinning and last-line occupation
  • cut-backs, crosses, and second-ball chances

What They Solve

A real striker archetype:

  • converts access into end product
  • attacks the box with conviction
  • finishes quickly once the structure creates the chance
  • gives the attack a true reference point in scoring zones

Scouting Indicators

  • times movement relative to the pass, not just the line
  • attacks high-value spaces inside the area
  • gets shots off cleanly under pressure
  • turns unstable situations into usable attempts

Common Limitations

  • can become dependent on supply quality
  • some scorers offer little if the team needs link play or pressing
  • box instincts may not travel cleanly across very different tactical environments

Related Concepts

  • Box Entry
  • Blind-Side Run
  • Pinning
