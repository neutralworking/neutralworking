Powerhouse

Category: Physical
Core Attributes: Aggression, Duels, Shielding, Throwing

Physical dominance through strength and aggression. Wins duels, shields the ball, and bullies opponents off possession.

Tactical Habitat

Powerhouses fit environments that demand:

  • duel dominance
  • contact security in both boxes
  • protection in direct or second-ball matches
  • physical resistance in unstable phases

What They Solve

A powerhouse gives a team:

  • contact superiority
  • resilience under pressure
  • physical leverage in transitions and set pieces
  • the ability to impose rather than simply survive

Scouting Indicators

  • wins body battles consistently
  • resists contact without losing action quality
  • imposes themselves in both defensive and attacking duels
  • sustains physical dominance over the match

Common Limitations

  • physical edge can flatter players with limited tactical feel
  • some powerhouses dominate contact but not space
  • value depends heavily on league and game-state context

Related Concepts

  • Compactness
  • Counterpressing Recovery
  • Box Entry
