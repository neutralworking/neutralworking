# Passer

**Category**: Technical  
**Core Attributes**: Pass Accuracy, Crossing, Pass Range, Through Balls

> Distribution specialist. Accurate short and long passing, crosses, and through balls. Moves the ball precisely to create chances.

## Tactical Habitat

Passers are strongest in teams that value:

- [Build-Up](/Users/solid-snake/Documents/chief-scout/kb/concepts/build-up.md) through circulation
- progression via [Line-Breaking Pass](/Users/solid-snake/Documents/chief-scout/kb/concepts/line-breaking-pass.md)
- [Switch of Play](/Users/solid-snake/Documents/chief-scout/kb/concepts/switch-of-play.md) to punish shifting blocks
- controlled [Verticality](/Users/solid-snake/Documents/chief-scout/kb/concepts/verticality.md)

## What They Solve

A true passer gives the team:

- cleaner distribution across phases
- access to weak-side space
- better progression without extra touches
- a route out of pressure when carrying is not available

## Scouting Indicators

- sees the next line early
- can vary speed, angle, and disguise of pass
- improves the receiver's situation rather than just finding them
- shifts defenses with the ball alone

## Common Limitations

- can become sterile in teams that need more carrying or dynamism
- range may mask low tempo influence
- some passers distribute well but cannot survive direct pressure
