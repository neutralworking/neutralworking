# Destroyer

**Category**: Tactical  
**Core Attributes**: Blocking, Clearances, Marking, Tackling

> The ball winner. Tackles, blocks, clears, and disrupts opposition attacks. Sacrifices possession stats for defensive impact.

## Tactical Habitat

Destroyers fit teams that need:

- duel-winning in midfield or the back line
- aggressive responses to [Pressing Trigger](/Users/solid-snake/Documents/chief-scout/kb/concepts/pressing-trigger.md)
- direct suppression of dangerous receivers
- protection in chaotic or highly transitional matches

## What They Solve

A destroyer helps a team:

- kill actions before they develop
- impose physical and defensive resistance
- win contact in zones where passive control is not enough
- bring edge to weak defensive phases

## Scouting Indicators

- attacks duels with intent and timing
- wins contact without constant fouling
- disrupts rhythm of key opposition players
- recovers loose balls after first contact

## Common Limitations

- can damage shape by chasing destruction over structure
- some destroyers win duels but lose the next action
- possession-heavy teams may expose technical ceilings quickly
