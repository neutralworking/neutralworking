# Creator

**Category**: Mental  
**Core Attributes**: Creativity, Flair, Vision, Threat

> The spark. Sees passes others don't, conjures moments from nothing, and carries the creative burden. Often the most-watched player on the pitch.

## Tactical Habitat

Creators are most effective in systems that provide:

- receivers between lines
- [Third-Man Run](/Users/solid-snake/Documents/chief-scout/kb/concepts/third-man-run.md) support
- occupation of key lanes around the box
- enough structure to turn imagination into repeatable threat

## What They Solve

Creators help a team:

- turn possession into chances
- find disguised routes through compact blocks
- create high-value actions from crowded spaces
- change the attack with one decisive pass or action

## Scouting Indicators

- sees options before the defense recognizes them
- manipulates defenders with disguise and timing
- creates useful chaos rather than random risk
- improves the final third without needing constant volume

## Common Limitations

- can become low-floor profiles if structure is poor
- some creators force invention when circulation is the better answer
- defensive tradeoffs often matter more than with controllers
