# 3-1-6

https://x.com/ebl2017/status/1693615896776872374?s=46&t=iRb3BEISGOVchEnVYPXAPQ


A big problem teams who play a 3-1-6 face is a lack of second phase presence in the initial phase of the build-up and in the final third when circulating to try to break the opposition down.

Pochettino tried to mitigate that through the role of Enzo Fernandez.
