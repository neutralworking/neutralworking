# 5-4-1

This is a particularly defensive formation, with an isolated forward and a packed defence. Again, however, a couple of attacking [full-backs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Defender_(association_football)#Full-back "Defender (association football)") can make this formation resemble something like a 3–6–1. One of the most famous cases of its use is the [Euro 2004](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/UEFA_Euro_2004 "UEFA Euro 2004")-winning [Greek national team](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Greece_national_football_team "Greece national football team")
