# Inverting the Role

> *Inverting the Pyramid* told the history of formations. This series tells the history of **roles** — the specific tactical jobs that players do within those formations, and how a single innovator can reimagine what a position means.

## The Arc

1. **The Birth of Roles** (pre-1960s) — roles defined by formation, unnamed, unexamined
2. **The Liberation** (1960s-1990s) — coaches separate role from position
3. **The Taxonomy** (2000s-present) — roles so specialized that a single position contains five different jobs
